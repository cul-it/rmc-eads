Restrictions on Access

Scope and content

Includes material on education legislation; education research; scholarships and financial aid; academic departments and divisions of the University; admissions budgets and correspondence; University budgets; University libraries; R.O.T.C.; campus planning; academic associations; research grants; faculty and staff; minutes of meetings; Board of Trustees records; reports; and other records of the Vice President for Academic Affairs; includes files of Stuart M. Brown.
