COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes correspondence, recipient information, reports and publicity pertaining to the Dean's Fellowship, which was established in 1992 by Francille Firbaugh, following the 1991 New York State College of Human Ecology Conference "Rethinking Women and Home Economics in the 20th Century."
Recipients from 1992-2015 include: Nancy Berlage, Kathleen Babbitt, Julia Grant, Mary Summers, Carolyn Goldstein, Janet Hutchison, Karen Stupski, Bruce Pietrykowski, Amy Bentley, Richard Ahrens, Kathy Cooke, Megan Elias, Jan Scholl, Charlotte Biltekoff, Gabriella Petrick, Helen Veit, Mary-Anne Beecher, Karen Dunn-Haley, Gwen Kay, Anna Flaming, Anna Schatz, Allison Horrocks, Barbara Penner and Anna Myjak-Pycia.
Includes the 2006 undergraduate fellowship recipient, Corey Earle.
