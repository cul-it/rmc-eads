ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

C. R. Henderson, for many years a professor in the Department of Animal Science, was world-renowned in the area of animal breeding.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Records pertaining to research and extension, including the New York State Fair, particularly with sheep, swine, and goats, 1949-1973.
Also, two video cassettes of "The Legacy of C. R. Henderson," from the American Dairy Science Association Symposium, June 1990, and radio talks (1949-1955) given by faculty over the Rural Radio Network.
