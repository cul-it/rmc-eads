Scope and content

This collection documents Vijay Nambisan's literary life and his marriage to Kavery Nambisan, herself a novelist and surgeon. It contains correspondence, clippings, drafts of poetry and prose, notebooks and diaries, paintings and illustrations.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Preferred Citation

Vijay Nambisan papers, #8693. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Biographical / Historical

Vijay Nambisan was a poet, journalist, critic and writer of non-fiction, with three collections of poetry, three books of non-fiction and dozens of essays.
