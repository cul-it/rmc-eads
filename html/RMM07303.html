BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Dorothy Feola served as the editor of the WOMEN'S NETWORK.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Book catalogs, publicity materials on lesbian, gay, and feminist issues, newsletters and other materials from chapters of the National Organization for Women, items relating to publishing, and newsletters of interest to Feola such as The Animals' Agenda and Family Planning Perspectives. As editor of the Women's Network, she collected books and journals of feminist and lesbian poetry and local lesbian and feminist newsletters from all regions of the United States and several titles from Canada. Her collected feminist and lesbian periodicals (over 50 titles filling 9 cubic feet) and books were cataloged as individual titles.
