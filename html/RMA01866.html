Files of Larry I. Palmer, including general correspondence and miscellaneous reports. ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Records from 1967 to 1984 are from the Office of Vice Provost; records from 1985 to 1986 are from the office of the Vice President for Academic Programs. The name change did not reflect any change in personnel or responsibilities.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Files of Larry I. Palmer, including general correspondence and miscellaneous reports. Also, records of the Cornell University Libraries Director Search and miscellaneous reports.
