COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes letters, clippings, account books, and other papers of the Walter Stanton family. Also, reminiscences of Walter Stanton. Named in the papers are Thomas Older, Christopher Burlingham, John Cochran, Gertruyd Cochran, the Gaston family, and the Levy family.
