Content Description

The Cornell University Program Board records (1995-2013) contains correspondence, administrative and financial records, press and promotional materials, and other event planning materials for speakers and other events sponsored by the Cornell University Program Board (CUPB). Events include comedians, motivational speakers, actors and directors, political debates, and theater and performance art.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Preferred Citation

Cornell University Program Board records, #37-7-4569. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Biographical / Historical

The Cornell University Program Board (CUPB) is a student organization located within the Dean of Students Office and Campus Activities, formerly the Student Activities Office (CAO). CUPB is responsible for organizing major lecture and entertainment events for the entire Cornell community. CUPB also works with other campus organizations, such as the Multicultural Community-Fueled Activities Board (MCFAB), to co-sponsor various activities.
Materials in collection are restricted to permission of the office of origin.
