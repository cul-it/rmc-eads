ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Formerly known as the New York State Home Economics Teachers Association (HETA). This association was conceived in 1961, chartered in 1969, and changed its name in December 1999.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Curriculum material, including annual reports of the Association, slides and photographs, texts, old Regents examinations, newsletters, a President's Notebook, meeting minutes.
