Scope and content

Postcards of Cornell University, some used with messages. Includes campus buildings, events, panoramic postcards, postcard books and albums, three folding postcard sets. Some postcards feature people including students. Postcards are both photographic and others are illustrations. Also includes felt postcards and homemade leather postcards. Several of the leather postcards are believed to be the work of Arthur Stickney created for or sent to Corinne Russell.
