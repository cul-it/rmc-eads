Manuscript Box 90
Paper in Hebrew which appears to be about the synagogue
Manuscript Box 90
"Plan to reconstruct the Synagogue Beracha ve Shalom at Joden Savanna – Suriname" booklet
Scope and Contents
Contents: 1. Illustration of the elevation by direction 2. Breakdown of each section of the synagogue, focus on the outside 3. Breakdown of the synagogue's interior 4. Plan of the synagogue, cemetery marked 5. Location of the synagogue in relation to the Suriname River 6. Estimate of the Reconstruction of the synagogue "Beracha ve Shalon" at Joden Savanna 7. Photos – 1U copy 2 "remainder, North-elevation"; 1Y copy 2 "remainder, West-elevation"; 1Z copy 2 "detail of a tomb-stone"; four photos showing the details of a tombstone with Hebrew writing, two winged children, a skull and crossbones, and other imagery
