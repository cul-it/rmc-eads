BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

The collection was assembled by Lenny Roberts, better known as Breakbeat Lenny, a DJ who assembled the seminal compilation series "Ultimate Breaks and Beats" throughout the 1980s.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

The core of the collection is a series of rare soundboard recordings of live performances taken directly from the soundboard, with clear labels indicating dates, venues, and performers. It is likely that these were the master tapes from which "battle tapes" of live performances were made and distributed in the South Bronx. Also includes are Lenny's personal copies of 13 of the original 25 Ultimate Breaks and Beats compilation LPs; 127 hip hop party fliers, and a selection of publications relating to record collecting and DJing.

If an item is in another media format, you may request to have the item digitized for access. Information on ordering access copies may be found on the Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections webpage.
Conditions Governing Use

Use of digitized audio visual materials is limited to on-site access only.
