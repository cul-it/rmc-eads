COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

The records of the Gay Publishing Company of Toronto, Ontario, Canada, consist of incorporation, business, and funding records, correspondence, advertising samples, art and graphics samples, and submissions. Also included are the records of the Gay Publishing Company's affiliated enterprises, including Gay International Press, Lord and Shields Limited, and Scene Publishing Company. Also included is the correspondence of the Gay Publishing Company's editors, publishers, and owners James C. Ward, Robert M. Marsden, and Robert Maynard.
