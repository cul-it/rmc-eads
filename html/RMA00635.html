DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Cornell University Office of Alumni Affairs. Cornell Club regional records, 1911-2012.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
41-6-635
Abstract:
Correspondence and records of the Alumni Office and its regional clubs
Creator:
Cornell University. Office of Alumni Affairs
Quanitities:
38.4 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
Sarasota-Manatee 55th Anniversary
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence and records of the Alumni Office and its regional clubs; include records of Cornell Day celebration also correspondence (1911-1918) concerning the Advisor of Women's position originally held by Mrs.Gertrude S. Martin, including letters from Mrs. Martin and women in various Cornell Clubs to President Schurman expressing their concern that the position was not a faculty one.
Also 5 films from Southwest Regional Office. Various Cornell buttons from the Northeast Regional Office.
Also 12 Cornell buttons.
NOTES

Includes collection #41-6-1492.
