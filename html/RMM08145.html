Content Description

Materials belonging to or created by musician Bruce Yaw, including correspondence, tour itineraries, press packets, news-clippings, magazines, publicity, and 19 cassette recordings.
Biographical / Historical

Bruce Yaw was born in Jersey City, NJ on July 26, 1946. He attended Eastern Kentucky University and graduated from Montclair State University. Bruce begain playing the bass guitar early in life and became known as an inventive RnB groove player. He was a member of the "The Everyman Band," which started touring the world in 1975 and recorded with Lou Reed. In 1986, he begain teaching French and Spanish in the Moravia Central School District in New York.
Conditions Governing Use

Due to the fragility and potential degradation of moving image and sound recordings, viewing and listening is limited to items that have been digitized. If an item is in another media format, you may request to have the item digitized for access. Information on ordering access copies may be found on the Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections webpage.
