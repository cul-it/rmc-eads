BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Robert Zehnder was an amateur viticulturist in Summerville, South Carolina who specialized in breeding "southern" grapes.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, grape vouchers, card file, research, and notes.
RELATED MATERIALS

Also see The Liberty Hyde Bailey Hortorium for grape hybrid vouchers, grape leaf pressings and notes from this collection.
Accession folder has photos of Zehnder, his grapes, and his vineyard. Some photos are on CD-ROM, also two video interview clips.
Forms part of the Eastern Wine and Grape Archive. (EWGA)
