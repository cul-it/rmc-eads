COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Items related to the inauguration of Ronald Reagan as President and George Bush as Vice President include tickets, passes, and invitations to inauguration ceremonies and activities; programs; parking signs; photographs; pro-Equal Rights Amendment items; brochures of commemorative items; and religious tracts and magazines.
Also, clippings and articles concerning various Cornell alumni.
