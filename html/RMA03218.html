INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Elmer S. Phillips papers, #21-42-3218. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Biographical / Historical

Professor Emeritus in Communication, in the College of Agriculture and Life Sciences at Cornell University. Phillips went from being an Instructor (1935), to Assistant Professor (1941), to Associate Professor (1944), to Professor (1955), retiring in 1968. Phillips was a founding member of a wine appreciation society in Ithaca that later became the American Wine Society.
Scope and content

Correspondence, lecture notes, publications, photographs, slides, and other papers of a Communications professor. Includes photographs of the 1937 Ithaca flood, aerial views of Cornell (1937) and movie films taken by Phillips, including Cornell University scenes, faculty members, academic activities, sports, campus events and agricultural activities at Cornell.
