DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Cornell AIDS Action records, 1987-1996
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
51-1-2746
Abstract:
Records of the Sexuality and AIDS educator (1987-1990) and Coordinator of Cornell AIDS Action (1990-1992), Sharon J. Dittman. Includes records of the CU AIDS Advisory Committee (1991-1992); training, workshop, and resource materials from Cornell AIDS Action and SAFER (Students for AIDS Facts Education and Responsibility); clippings on sexuality, religion, and other topics; also, educational videos on AIDS and sexuality.
Creator:
Cornell AIDS Action
Quanitities:
5 cubic feet.
16 mapcase folders.
30 videocassettes.
Language:
Collection material in English
RELATED MATERIALS

Collecting Program: Human Sexuality Collection.
Related Collection: Cornell Biology and Society 451, "AIDS and Society," scrapbooks, #21-44-3298.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Cornell AIDS Action records, #51-1-2746. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Records of Cornell AIDS Action, reflecting the work of Sharon J. Dittman, the Sexuality and AIDS educator (1987-1090) and Coordinator of Cornell AIDS Action (1990-1992). Includes annual reports (1991-1995); records of the CU AIDS Advisory Committee (1991-1992); Dittman's and the Peer Educators' subject files and educational videos on AIDS and sexuality; and training, workshop, and resource materials from Cornell AIDS Action, Peer Educators, and SAFER (Students for AIDS Facts Education and Responsibility), including The AIDS Education Game.
Scope and content

Course packs from Spring and Fall 1992, Fall 1993, and Fall 1994, for "AIDS and Society" class (Biology and Society 451), which Dittman co-taught with Ari van Tienhoven. Includes "I Think Mr. Jingles has AIDS!" a children's book designed by three students in the Fall 1992 class.
Scope and content

AIDS and safe sex posters in numerous languages and from various organizations, mostly from 31 states and the District of Columbia in the United States, as well as from 9 other countries.
