DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Willard Straight Hall records, 1925-2004.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
35-7-503
Abstract:
Files of Cornell University's student union include files relating to the creation of the student union, correspondence, financial records, printed material, scrapbooks, guestbooks, and some Straight family correspondence.
Creator:
Willard Straight Hall (Ithaca, N.Y.)
Quanitities:
43.5 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Files of Cornell University's student union include files relating to the creation of the student union, correspondence, financial records, printed material, scrapbooks, guest books, and some Straight family correspondence. Also some files regarding the Psi Upsilon fraternity.
Includes a copy of The Journal of the Royal Architectural Institute of Canada, University of Toronto Number, Vol. 2, January-February 1925, No. 1 with photographs of Hart House at the University of Toronto, one of the inspirations for Willard Straight Hall.
Photos of Christmas events and decorations in the Straight ca. 1955.
