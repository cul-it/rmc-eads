COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

A collection of pamphlets and selected student reports on forestry and lumbering prepared by the New York State College of Forestry at Cornell University, Bernhard E. Fernow, and others pertaining to the College's operation, its experimental forest in the Adirondacks, and the Swenson Suit which resulted in the abandonment of the College's experimental program.
