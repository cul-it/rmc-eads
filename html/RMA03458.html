DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
David Feldshuh papers, 1972-2016.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
14-24-3458
Abstract:
Professional papers and reviews, newspaper clippings, correpondence, and programs for various plays, including Miss Evers' Boys, a play based on the notorious Tuskegee Syphilis Study in the African American male.
Creator:
Feldshuh, David, 1944-
Quanitities:
13.5 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Professor of Theatre Arts and Director of the Cornell Center for Theatre Arts.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

David Feldshuh papers, #14-24-3458. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Professional papers and reviews, newspaper clippings, correspondence, and programs for various plays, including Miss Evers' Boys, a play based on the notorious Tuskegee Syphilis Study on the African American male; also drafts of his speech at the Inauguration of David Skorton as president of Cornell University, 2006.
Access Restrictions:

Access to Boxes 4-6 (Inauguration speech drafts and Miss Evers' Boys files) restricted to permission of the donor.
