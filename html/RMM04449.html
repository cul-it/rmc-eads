DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Percy Reese Morgan papers, 1900-1961.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
4449
Abstract:
Papers of Percy Reese Morgan, a fruit farmer in Lewiston, N.Y. and observer for the U.S. Weather Bureau in the Lewiston area.
Creator:
Morgan, Percy Reese.
Quanitities:
2.8 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Fruit farmer in Lewiston, N.Y. and observer for the U.S. Weather Bureau in the Lewiston area. His wife was Mary Dodge Morgan.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence with family, friends, business associates and public officials; farm records; farm animal health and veterinary records; weather reports, 1949-1960; handwritten and typescript diaries, 1921-1961; extension reports; records of the Lewiston-Queenston Rotary Club; items relating to the New York State Horticultural Association; and pamphlets and brochures.
