DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Theta Delta Chi, Beta chapter records, 1870-2015.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
37-4-1602
Abstract:
Includes photographs, mostly of fraternity members; pamphlets about the fraternity; records of the fraternity's Building Fund Campaign; and other correspondence and miscellaneous records of Theta Delta Chi at Cornell University. Also includes scrapbooks kept by Luzerne Coville (Class of 1886) and Charles Rodman Stull (Class of 1907); and copies of the chapter newsletter, The Beta Bulletin, 1981-2015.
Creator:

Quanitities:

Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes photographs, mostly of fraternity members; pamphlets about the fraternity; records of the fraternity's Building Fund Campaign; and other correspondence and miscellaneous records of Theta Delta Chi at Cornell University. Also includes scrapbooks kept by Luzerne Coville (Class of 1886) and Charles Rodman Stull (Class of 1907); and copies of the chapter newsletter, The Beta Bulletin, 1981-2015.
Access Restrictions:

Access restricted to the president, secretary and librarian of Theta Delta Chi.
