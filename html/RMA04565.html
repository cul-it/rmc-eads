Content Description

Collection contains drawings by Andrew Seager during his time as an architecture student at Cornell University.
Biographical / Historical

Andrew Seager received a Bachelor of Architecture degree from Cornell University in 1965. He received a Master of Achitecture degree from Iowa State University, taught architectural design and history of architecture for a year at Iowa State, and then taught at Ball State University from 1968 until his retirement in 2008. Since his time at Cornell he has been associated with the Archaeological Exploration of Sardis, Turkey.
