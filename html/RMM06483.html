Cite As:

Ithaca Feminist Radio Collective, "Being Ourselves" Records, #6483. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Biographical / Historical

Designed to address a broad spectrum of women.s issues, the radio show "Being Ourselves" aired interviews with local and visiting activists, writers, artists, and thinkers, as well as women.s music. Collective members changed over the years. An incomplete list of members includes: Leni Hochman, Judy Scherer, Jennifer Greene, Sara Shenk, Robin Whittlesey, Miriam Graham, Patrice Jennings, Deb Whiter, and Wendy Kohli.
