INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Williams family papers, #1148. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Papers of Josiah Butler Williams, banker, businessman, and New York State Senator, contain business, political, and personal papers, accounts, legal documents and correspondence, 1809-1883. Subjects include banking, canals, the lumber business, railroads, and real estate; also, Cornell University, Ovid Academy, the New York and Erie Telegraph Company, the Ithaca and Owego Railroad Company, the Ithaca Cotton Mills, the Merchants and Farmers' Bank, Ithaca, and the Sodus Canal.
Scope and content

Papers of Mary Hardy Williams, wife of Josiah B., contain personal correspondence, a diary, bills of H. S. Williams (1900-1911) social notes, clippings, and family accounts, 1824-1905.
Scope and content

Papers of Augusta H. Williams, daughter of Josiah B., contain personal correspondence, accounts, and business papers, 1879-1948.
Scope and content

Papers of Henry Shaler Williams, son of Josiah B., geologist, containing lecture and research notes used at Cornell and Yale Universities; professional correspondence and scrapbooks of photographs and clippings; articles and manuscripts for publication; photographs, maps, and plates; and miscellaneous printed matter, ca. 1864-1917. Also includes H. S. Williams' personal and household bills (1900-1911). Subjects include geology and politics. Includes Ithaca to Owego geological observations, July 21, 1885, and miscellaneous H. S. Williams items.
Scope and content

The collection also includes miscellaneous papers of Charlotte, Jane, Ella, and Otis Williams and Louisa, Jane, and Charles Elias Hardy, and contains photographs of the Williams family as well as genealogical information. Plans by William Henry Miller for the Williams residence.
