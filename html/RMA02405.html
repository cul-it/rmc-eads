DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Properties Management Department. Records, 1943-2004.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
28-2-2405
Abstract:
Curriculum review reports, consulting files, specifications and estimates for the Statler Hall, research studies, Statler design studios files, and course materials for Properties Management courses at Cornell, including 20 architectural drawings of the Statler Hotel.
Creator:
Cornell University. School of Hotel Administration. Properties Management Department
Quanitities:
29.7 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Curriculum review reports, consulting files, specifications and estimates for the Statler Hall, research studies, Statler design studios files, and course materials for Properties Management courses at Cornell, including 20 architectural drawings of the Statler Hotel.
