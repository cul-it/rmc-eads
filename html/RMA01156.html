Biographical / Historical

Professor of experimental hydraulics, Cornell University. Cornell University Ph.D. 1903.
Scope and content

Includes notes, lectures, lab notes, exams, printed matter, hydraulics texts, and other course material; correspondence; diaries; reports; blueprints; graphs; charts; test data; photographs; glass negatives; and other papers related to Schoder's career as a professor of civil engineering.
