ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Moosewood Restaurant opened in Ithaca, NY in 1973. Moosewood, Inc. is a collectively owned business whose members share responsibilities and participate in the various jobs necessary to run what has grown from a very small natural foods restaurant to become a larger and more diversified company with a line of salad dressings, soups, and frozen main-dish entrees. With an emphasis on healthful natural foods cuisine, Moosewood has been acclaimed as a driving force in the world of creative vegetarian cooking.
