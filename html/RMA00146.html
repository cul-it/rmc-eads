INFORMATION FOR USERS
Preferred Citation

Cornell University Class of 1912 records, #37-5-146. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Cornell Class of 1912 items include scrapbooks of clippings, photographs, programs (including Class Day program), menus, poetry, songs, and correspondence (1908-12); printed material; alumni photographs and negatives; reunion scrapbooks (1912-52) and clothing; a 1912 pillow case; and three Class of 1912 pins.
Scope and content

Also, copies of the "On-to-Ithaca Gazette," 1930-1939.
Related Materials

See also: James Irving Clarke scrapbooks, #37-5-335. Henry A. Schwedes scrapbook, #37-5-4460.
