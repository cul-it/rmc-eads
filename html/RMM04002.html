DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Maurice F. Neufeld papers, [ca.1941]-1949.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
4002
Abstract:
Correspondence, notebooks, pamphlets, and bulletins concerning Maurice F. Neufeld's association with New York State government work during World War II, 1941-1943, and in 1948-1949.
Creator:
Neufeld, Maurice F.
Quanitities:
3 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Maurice F. Neufeld is a Professor of Industrial and Labor Relations at Cornell University.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, notebooks, pamphlets, and bulletins concerning Maurice F. Neufeld's association with New York State government work during World War II, 1941-1943, and in 1948-1949.
