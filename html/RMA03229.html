BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Phil Handler, Cornell University B.Arch. 1964, M.Arch. 1965 and Maddy Handler, B.S. 1965 created videotapes documenting the experiences of Cornell Architecture alumni. They own and run Fly on the Wall Productions, which documents construction projects using video and digital photography.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

DVD versions of videos created by Fly on the Wall Productions: "Cornell University Reunion 2000: AA&P Catalyst for Creativity," with Bruce Abbey, Peter Barton, Tad Mann, Blue Minges, Dell Mitchell, Brad Perkins, Joel Perlman, Peter Rosen, Alan Saret, Warren Schwartz, and Jon Stoumen; "Millennium Box Dedication," 2000; "Cornell University Reunion 2001: AA&P Catalyst for Creativity," with Bob Matyas, Jack Dollard, Stuart Carter, and Bob Jawitz; "Video Vignettes of the AA&P Class of '62 Reunion 2002" and "Cornell University Reunion 2002: AA&P Class of '62 Symposium."
Also, "Cornell University Council: Fifty Years & Counting, October 25-28, 2000."
Clippings, catalogs, articles, reminiscences, and other source materials for the videos.
Reunion 2003: "Video Vignettes of Cornell AA&P Class of 1963" including Marjorie Walker Sayer, Madeleine Leston Meehan, Donna Forsman, Peter Parsons, Tom Beeby, Bob Fox, and Alan Chimacoff, and "Elsie Dinsmore Popkin: A Profile for Cornell Views Through the Seasons," exhibit at the Herbert F. Johnson Museum of Art, June 4,-July 6, 2003 (2 DVDs).
Reunion 2004: "Video Vignettes of Cornell AA&P Class of 1964" including Jack C. Dobson, Leslie Checkley Morse-Goldfarb, Ortrude Busse White, Valerie Jesraly Seligsohn, Paul W. Fox, and Linda Brandt Myers. Also, "Being Eisenman: A Video about Peter Eisenman, Cornell Class of '54 Architecture '55"; "Eisenman: Being Back: Remarks by Peter Eisenman at the AA&P Dean's Breakfast, June 12, 2004"; and "The Architect's Work I Peter Eisenman" exhibit brochure, 2004 (3 DVDs).
"True Big Red: The life & work of Don Greenberg, '55"; and "Journey from Sibley: Jill Lerner '75 B Arch 76," both 2005 (DVDs).
"Big Red/Meier White: A Cornell Story," a video about Richard A. Meier, Cornell Class of '56, Architecture '57, produced in honor of his 50th Reunion, June, 2006 (DVD). Also, folder of documentation for the project.
"Welcoming the Popkin Pastel," Statler Hotel, Cornell University, April 1, 2006 (DVD).
"Simply Squier," 2007, DVD in honor of Professor Emeritus Jack Squier, MFA '52 & Jane Squier. Also, research files and publicity for the project. DVD version of 1963 slide show by Randy Lewis: "The Plantations Sculpture Project," February-May 1963, showing the construction of a sculpture at the Cornell Plantations by Alan Chimacoff, Randy Lewis, and Webb Nichols, under the supervision of Professor Squier, with slides by Lewis and Chimacoff.
"Portrait of a Cornell Artist: Elsie Dinsmore Popkin '58," 2008
"Ratan Tata '59: The Cornell Story, on his 50th Reunion, June 4, 2009." "Celebrating Yervant: Looking Back at 70," video about Yervant Terzian, the David C. Duncan Professor in the Physical Sciences, Department of Astronomy, 2009.
"Voices of 1965: Cornell Reunion Event, June 10-12, 2010."
"A Century at Llenroc," 2011 DVD .
"Jack Squier Day at Cornell," 2007 DVD.
"Remembering Our Days on the Hill: Cornell Class of 1962 50th Reunion, June 7-10, 2012," featuring George Telesh, Mike Duesing, Jane Brody, Clark Halstead, Char Jones Collister, Sam Fleming, Jan McClayton Crites, Alex Steinbergh, and Harry Edwards (DVD).
"Cornell AA&P 125th Anniversary, October 4 & 5, 1996: Part 1 Disc 1 Henry Cobb, Richard Meier & Frank Stella; Disk 2 Ann Markusen; Part 2 Disc 3 Arthur Danto; Disc 4 Herbert Muschamp; Interviews. Colin Rowe Festschrift, April 28, 1996, Part 1 and 2.
Clippings, catalogs, articles, reminiscences, and other source materials for the videos.
