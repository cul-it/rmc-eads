BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Dewitt Clinton Rodenhurst, January 26, 1855-May 5, 1946, was born in Westmoreland, NY and practiced medicine as a physician in Philadelphia, Jefferson County, NY.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Record of births and deaths of patients (1 v., 1880-1944); scrapbook of newspaper clippings, snapshots and photographs, letters, programs, and other otems (1 v., 1911-1945) of articles appearing in THE NORTH COUNTRY ADVANCE (1944-1945) and containing Dr. Rodenhurst's recollections of Philadelphia, New York, around 1880; printed items pertaining to the medical profession.
