ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

In 1969 the Department of Institution Management and the Department of Food and Nutrition merged to become the Department of Human Nutrition and Food.
In 1974 the Board of Trustees voted to abolish the Department of Human Nutrition and Food in the College of Human Ecology and the Graduate School of Nutrition to establish the Division of Nutritional Sciences, an intercollege unit within the Colleges of Agriculture and Life Sciences and Human Ecology.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, minutes, reports, photographs, blueprints, and other material pertaining to the Cafeteria Management (later Institution Management) departments, including the papers of a professor of Institutional Management, Kathleen Cutlar. Subjects include the Purnell Project, "Training Schools," classroom activities, planning of Martha Van Rensselaer Hall, and other matters pertaining to industrial nutrition and education. Includes papers of Alice Burgoin and Katherine W. Harris.
