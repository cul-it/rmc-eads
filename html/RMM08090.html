COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

The collection consists of 252 posters, 46 programs, 24 flyers, 28 team stickers, 13 postcards, 1 magnet, and 1 calendar. Included are posters promoting bouts, fliers, handbills, logos, stickers, photographs, schedules, programs, and a calendar. Materials feature striking graphic design, and information such as team names, schedules, places, dates, sponsors, and websites. Over 19 leagues are represented, both flat track and banked track. Among the teams represented are: Naptown Rollergirls, Lone Star Rollergirls, Providence Roller Derby, Dallas Derby Devils, Rose City Rollers, Down South Derby, The Cincinnati Rollergirls, North Kentucky Roller Derby, Black-N-Bluegrass Roller Derby, Kansas City Roller Derby, Bleeding Heartland Rollergirls, Central Coast Roller Derby, Echo City Knockouts (Hawaii), ICT Rollergirls, Rat City Rollergirls, and Arizona Roller Derby, among others.
