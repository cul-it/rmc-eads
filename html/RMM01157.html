BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

New York State Governor, 1830-1832.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

The Enos Thompson Throop papers contain intra-family correspondence about business, weddings, deaths, and travel; receipts from tradesmen, hardware, agricultural implements, repair, newspaper, general stores, and clothing; a farm labourers accounts, 1834-1838, including prices for produce and purchases. The collection ncludes a letter to State Assemblyman questioning his campaign promise of a bill to place the County court house in Auburn, 1807, and an executive order issued by Governor Throop for the transfer of prisoners from Auburn to Sing Sing, the former prison being overcrowded, February 11, 1830.
