ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

The Cascadilla School was a boarding school for boys in Ithaca, New York.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Catalogues (1887, 1893-1894, 1896-1897, 1923-1924, 1924-1925, 1931-1932), prospectus and yearbook (ca. 1900), and pictures of the school (ca. 1900).
Accession folder contains a "Catalogue of Students of the Cascadilla School, 1876-94."
