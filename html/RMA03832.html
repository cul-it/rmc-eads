DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Cornell Concert Commission records, 1971-2017.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
37-7-3832
Abstract:
Material documenting the shows and operations of the Cornell Concert Commission mostly from 1971-1992, includes tour riders, posters, advertising material, and scrapbooks.
Creator:
Cornell Concert Commission
Chodock, Robert,
Kibrick, Howard
Quanitities:
8 cubic feet.
4 mapcase folders.
Language:
Collection material in English
The Cornell Concert Commission collection (1971-2016) documents the events and operations of the Cornell Concert Commission. The collection contains tour riders, contracts, posters, promotional materials and press releases, student crew surveys, administrative and financial records, scrapbooks, newspapers and clippings, and concert memorabilia.
