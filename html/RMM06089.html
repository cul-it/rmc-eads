ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Very little is known about this congregation except that they purchased a church, formerly owned by the Christian and Universalists, at a sheriff's sale and were later given the Presbyterian church after that congregation became defunct.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

This collection consists of a one volume pre-printed register containing lists of baptisms, marriages, probationers, members and classes.
