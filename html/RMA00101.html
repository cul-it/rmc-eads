COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence and other records of the Director of Extension at the New York State College of Agriculture. Topics include services for farmers, fruit and vegetable growers, and homemakers; other New York State agricultural organizations; New York State counties; and reports on the current state of the New York State College of Agriculture and its extension program. Included is a photograph album illustrating the overcrowded conditions in the New York State College of Agriculture as of 1920.
