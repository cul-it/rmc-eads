BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Dorothy Lobrano (Mrs. Raymond C.) Guth is the daughter of long-time (1938-1956) New Yorker fiction editor, Gus Lobrano (Class of 1924) and the goddaughter of E. B. White. In 1976, she collected and edited an edition of the Letters of E. B. White.
