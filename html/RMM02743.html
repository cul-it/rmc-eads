DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Ithaca Womans Club records, 1897-1963.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
2743
Abstract:
Records include three manuscript volumes containing constitution, by-laws, list of charter members; treasurers' records (1897-1909); and minutes of the Home Economic Section of the Ithaca Woman's Club (1902). Also miscellaneous items including receipts, programs, historical summaries, news clippings. One scrapbook containing a history of the club, lists of charter and early members, photographs of club presidents and copies of presidents' speeches and club brochures (1897-1949).
Creator:
Ithaca Woman's Club (Ithaca, N.Y.)
Quanitities:
4.4 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English

Minute book of the Home Economic Section of the Ithaca Woman's Club
 1902
Scope and Contents
This book consists of typed and mimeographed copies of minutes of meetings, outlines of talks given by various people on different topics such as:
  1. Development of the Home
  2. Books and Pictures in the Home
  3. Artistic Homes
  4. Some Advantages of Vegetarianism (etc.)
People mentioned as giving talks:
  1. Martha Van Rensselaer
  2. Mrs. Effie D. Morse
  3. Mrs. John Gifford
  4. Professor Carpenter
  5. Dr. Wilder
  6. Prof. V.A. Moore
  7. Mrs. M.V. Slingerland
