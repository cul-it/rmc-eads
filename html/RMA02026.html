Title:

Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:

Abstract:
Two small volumes of inventory and financial records, 1877-1890, 1888-1893; and photographs of staff and facilities of the Cornell University Botany Department, now the Section of Plant Biology.

COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Two small volumes of inventory and financial records, 1877-1890, 1888-1893; and photographs of staff and facilities of the Cornell University Botany Department, now the Section of Plant Biology.
