COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, newspapers clippings and memoranda pertaining to Howe's work with the Cornell United Religious Work (CURW). Also includes family correspondence, largely relating to Delaware County, N.Y., especially letters between her father Harley Howe and her mother; photographs;, papers; and a sewing kit, baby bonnet, mittens and bib.
