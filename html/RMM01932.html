BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

W. Franklin Moore was president of the Freeville public schools in the early 1920s.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Graduation programs, alumni reunion program, alumni directory (1917) for Dryden High School; items concerning Dryden Union School and Academy, including a program for eighth grade graduation (1896) and a broadside advertising a "historical allegory and tableaux" to be presented by students (November 1892); and annual informational catalogs on Dryden Union School, 1884-1886 and 1889-1890. Also, leaflet listing teachers and students in the Freeville Public Schools, 1922, and letterhead for the 1921-1922 school year, listing the Freeville school board and teachers.
