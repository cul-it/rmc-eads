DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Stereoscope pictures from Iceland, 1910-1911
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
8516
Creator:
Magnús Ólafsson, 1862-1937.
Halldór Hermannsson, 1878-1958.
Fiske Icelandic Collection
Quanitities:
1.75 cubic feet.
Scope and content

Stereoscope photographs, 213 in number, of Icelandic landscapes, buildings, brifges, people, and events. Captions in Icelandic, several with date 1910 or 1911.
Ownership and Custodial History

Acquired by Halldór Hermannsson.
