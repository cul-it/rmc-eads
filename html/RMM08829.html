Content Description

Archive of hand written testimonies of Venezuelan migrants collected by TodoSomos.org in Colombia between October of 2018 and May of 2022. The testimonies are preserved in the pages of 29 separate blank volumes of 'libros de actas' wherein refugees wrote 'the story of their migration;' writing with the understanding their words would be shared with the community at large.
Biographical / Historical

TodoSomos is a United States-based non-governmental organization (NGO) registered in Colombia.
