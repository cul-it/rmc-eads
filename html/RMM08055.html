Scope and content

Photographs, slides, posters, books, shirts and skateboard decks created by Larry Clark. Photographs are from projects including Tulsa, Teenage Lust, KIDS, and Larry Clark 1992, among others.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Preferred Citation

Larry Clark Archive, #8055. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Biographical / Historical

Lawrence Donald "Larry" Clark was born in Tulsa, Oklahoma in 1943 and has worked as a film director, photographer, writer, and film producer best known for the movie Kids and the photography book Tulsa. His work documents illegal drug use, underage sex and violence, and especially youth who are part of specific subcultures like surfing, punk rock or skateboarding.
