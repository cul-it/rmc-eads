COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Records include office, committee, and personnel files; memos; student records; examinations, including an 1891 entrance examination; English scholarship and fellowship materials including lists of winners; selected records of the Freshman Humanities Program; correspondence; pamphlets; minutes of faculty meetings (1946-1957); and records of the department chairman, 1948-1968.
English Department lectures presented Monday, January 29, 2007 at Hollis E. Cornell Auditorium, Goldwin Smith Hall, Cornell University: Sandra M. Gilbert, "Finding Atlantis: Thirty Years of Exploring Women's Literary Traditions" and M.H. Abrams, "On Reading Poetry Aloud."
