BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Political economist and activist. He taught at Cornell University and served as advisor to the Students for a Democratic Society. In the 1968 US presidential election he was one of the nominees for Vice President as a member of the Peace and Freedom Party.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Materials relating to Dowd's career and writings. Includes correspondence, publications and articles by Dowd and others on various political economic topics, book drafts, book reviews written by Dowd and reviews by others of Dowd's books, materials relating to Dowd's activism some relating to anti-Vietnam war activities and other injustices, including copies of FBI and CIA files. CDs, video, and audio materials relating to Dowd's career. Also, items relating to Thorstein Veblen. Also digital files containing documents, papers, articles, photographs, correspondence of Dowd's as well as audio and video of Dowd's talks and course material. Also copies of the content that appeared on dougdowd.org website managed by Michael Slaughter before it was shut down.
