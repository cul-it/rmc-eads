Merritt family.
Quanitities:
0.6 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Letters from various members of the Merritt family, describing life along the Saint Lawrence River and in Saint Lawrence County and their pioneering travel west to Iowa, Wisconsin, Kansas, and Nebraska; much local color. Also several financial ledgers, account and day books, and some various publications.
