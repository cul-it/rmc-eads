COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes correspondence, memoranda, conference agendas, notes, news releases, and reports of Robert Henry Elias, with particular reference to the American Studies Society, the American Historical Association, and the American Civilization Graduate Club. Correspondents and subjects include Harold Loeb, Marion Cummings, Bedelia Croly Falls, e. e. cummings, Saul Bellow, James Gould Cozzens, John Dos Passos, Katherine Anne Porter, Edmund Wilson, Jerome Kern, Huey Long, Henry Miller, Lewis Mumford, Frank O'Connor, John Crowe Ransom, James Thurber, Richard Wilbur, Theodore Dreiser, H. L. Mencken, and Toni Morrison.
