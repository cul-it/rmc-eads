Biographical / Historical

Cornell University Class of 1907. Ross L. Baker was an inventor and an engineer. He organized R. L. Baker and Co. Engineers, a firm of consulting engineers in New York City who worked with development of early electrical power companies as well as production, distribution, and use of electricity.
Scope and content

Correspondence, clippings, engineering reports, patent, financial records, legal records, travel notes, articles, publications, and personal papers of Ross Lee Baker.
