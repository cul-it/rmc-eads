COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Consists of letters written by Laura (Riding) Jackson to Griselda Ohannessian. It also includes letters sent by Schuyler B. Jackson to her, and letters written by both Laura (Riding) Jackson and Schuyler B. Jackson to Griselda.
Access Restrictions:

Access to the letters is subject to prior written approval by Mrs. Ohannessian.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Griselda Ohannessian collection of Laura (Riding) Jackson letters, #6303. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Biographical / Historical

Griselda Ohannessian is a daughter of Schuyler B. Jackson.s, husband of Laura (Riding) Jackson.
Biographical / Historical

Laura (Riding) Jackson was a poet, critic, and author best known for her association with the Fugitives in the Thirties, for her literary partnership with Robert Graves, and as the editor of Epilogue.
