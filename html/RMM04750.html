ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Based in Ithaca, New York, the Finger Lakes Runners Club (FLRC) organizes numerous road races, trail races, and track meets throughout the year. The club also sends teams to the Upstate New York Cross Country Series and to various regional and national championship events.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Board meeting minutes, race results, photographs and T-shirts from various local races, including the Finger Lakes Marathon and Ithaca 5 & 10.
