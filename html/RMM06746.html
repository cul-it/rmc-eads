BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

The Internet-First University Press was the outcome of a project, "Creating an Open Access Paradigm for Scholarly Publishing," led by J. Robert Cooke and Kenneth M. King and funded by the Atlantic Philanthropies. The project created online and DVD versions of interviews, lectures, and symposia.
