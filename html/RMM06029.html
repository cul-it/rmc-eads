CONTAINER LIST
Container
Description
Date
Item 1
From Henry W. Sage & Co. (Ithaca, N.Y.) to Hiram White (West Cayuta, N.Y.).
 April 29, 1851.
Scope and Contents
Discusses payment of a note.
Item 2
From Henry W. Sage (Ithaca, N.Y.) to Hiram White (West Cayuta, N.Y.).
 May 22, 1851.
Scope and Contents
Discusses payment for a horse.
Item 3
Town of Cayuta, Schuyler County, N.Y.,
 May 10, 1854.
Scope and Contents
License granted to Hiram White of Cayuta to sell liquor by the Commissioners of Excise. (two similar but not identical pcs.)
Item 4
From Watson Harderbeyle, dealer in liquor (N.Y. City) to Hiram White (West Cayuta, N.Y.).
 March 3, 1855.
Scope and Contents
Discusses receipt of payment and wares on hand.
Item 5
Record of indictment,
 September 3, 1855.
Scope and Contents
Of Hiram White for intoxication, Elmira, N.Y.; signed Hiram Potter, justice.
Item 6
From J.M. Brown (Camp Seward, near Rockville, Maryland) to his wife.
 Dec. 1, l86l.
Scope and Contents
Discusses his clothing allotment, the weather, his duties and his salary. Back sheet contains a pencil sketch of the inside of his tent.
Item 7
From Royal S. White (Alexandria, Va.) to William B. White.
 April 21, 1862.
Scope and Contents
Relates plans to travel down river to Ft. Monroe, discusses a shipment of souvenirs to Havanna, N.Y., and the possibility of being in the battle of Yorktowne.
Item 8
From Herman Sawyer (Son-in-law of Hiram), (Newburn, N.C.),
 April 26, 1862.
Scope and Contents
To someone in care of William B. White, his brother-in-law, asking someone to care for his money.
Item 9
From Captain G.V. White (Albany, N.Y.) to William White (Cortland, N.Y.).
 Dec. 17, l86l.
Scope and Contents
Discusses recruits and his situation.
