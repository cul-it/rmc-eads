ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Founded in 1922, the Ithaca Garden Club is a longstanding member of the City Federation of Women's Organizations (CFWO) in Ithaca, NY.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Ithaca Garden Club records, #6881. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Yearbooks, scrapbooks, and albums containing clippings and programs from events, executive board files, annual reports, presidents' reports, and civic development project folders from the Ithaca Garden Club. Also, biographical sketches of Katharine Ziegler Heasley, Leona "Westy" Gelder, Jane E. Hardy, and Cele Kemple, various club histories, and Beatrice Szekely's civic development chronology with commentary.
