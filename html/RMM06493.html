COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Newspapers and magazines reporting events of WWI including the Armistice being signed (Nov. 1918) and major events during World War II (1941-49), including the Washington Times Herald; Washington Post; New York Times; Baltimore News-Post; Washington Daily News; Philadelphia Inquirer; Daily Mirror; Baltimore American; Evening Star; Minneapolis Journal; Wisconsin State Journal; Madison Democrat; Yank: the Army Weekly and The Voice of Victory; Star Herald (1945); Panama Morning Journal (1914).
