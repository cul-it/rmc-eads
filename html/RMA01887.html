BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Hendrik Edelman served as Assistant Director of the Cornell University Libraries from 1969 to 1979, and then became University Librarian at Rutgers University.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Hendrik Edelman papers, #13-1-1887. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Includes alphabetical files of Assistant Director Hendrik Edelman as the Assistant Director of the Cornell University Libraries concerning collection development, 1969-1974.
Scope and content

Also, files relating to research on his book, Dutch-American Bibliography 1693-1794, a descriptive catalog of Dutch-language books, pamphlets, and almanacs printed in America, 1974; research, consulting, teaching, and other professional papers relating to immigrant presses in America and to library collection development; videos of a Pennsylvania State Library teleconference on collection development; and publications by Edelman.
