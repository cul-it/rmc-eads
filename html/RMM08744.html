Content Description

Letters, notes, drafts, and source materials compiled or created by Burton Bernstein in the process of the research and writing of his biography of American writer James Thurber, published in 1975. Includes correspondence between Bernstein and E.B. White and Katharine White.
Biographical / Historical

Burton Bernstein was a staff writer for The New Yorker magazine from 1957 to 1992 and the author of eight books. He is the younger brother of American composer and conductor Leonard Bernstein.
