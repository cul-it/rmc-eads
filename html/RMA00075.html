DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Cornell Law School Deans office records, 1888-1989.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
18-1-75
Abstract:
Correspondence, lists, subject files, memoranda, budgets, inventories and other records of the Law School, with reference to students, scholarships, donors, professional law societies, and other matters connected with the operation of the Law School.
Creator:
Cornell University. School of Law
Quanitities:
25 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, lists, subject files, memoranda, budgets, inventories and other records of the Law School, with reference to students, scholarships, donors, professional law societies, and other matters connected with the operation and administration of the Law School.
Also, Law School Feasibility Study, Shepley Bulfinch Richardson and Abbott, Phase 1, 1983 and Phase 2, 1984 as part of #18-1-2554.
