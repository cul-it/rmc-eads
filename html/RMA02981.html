BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Vice President of Cornell University.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, speeches, reports, subject files, and other papers of Dullea, chiefly during his Directorship of State Operations and Policy Management; includes personal correspondence and files relating to Cornell University. Also, a set of indexed transcripts from the six public hearings of the Temporary State Commission on Constitutional Revision, 1994, and information on the Lake Course Cooling Project and Graduate Student unionization at Cornell (2002).
