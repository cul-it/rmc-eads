Biographical / Historical

American Demographics was a journal founded in Ithaca, New York by Peter Francese in 1979. The magazine focused on analysis of demographic data and its interpretation for planners and marketers. The journal was published until 2004, when it was acquired by Advertising Age, which incorporated the name and some content into its magazine.
