COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, business and accounting records relating the the operation of the Taylor Wine Company in Hammondsport. Letters, notes, and statistics in wine advertising for and by Taylor Wine Company and D'Arcy Advertising Company. Also included are magazine clippings, newspapers, photos, negatives, audio recordings, articles, meeting reports, school coursework and publications. The collection shows the success of the company through smart and extensive marketing. Notable figures in this collection are Walter S. Taylor, Grayton H. Taylor, and William L. Rech.
Also included in this collection are Taylor family photographs, personal correspondence and school papers written by Walter Taylor, along with a set of autographed prints by H. Hawkins of early aviation.
