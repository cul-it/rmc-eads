Content Description

Thirty (30) photographs by Larry Fink featuring Jazz musicians. Several of the photos were part of his Somewhere There's Music project (published 2011). Musicians include Jason Moran, John Coltrane, Albert Ayler, Don Ayler, Archie Shepp, Dave Burrell, Leroy Jenkins, Jackie McLean, and others. Venues include The Village Vanguard and the Jazz Arts Society.
Biographical / Historical

Larry Fink was born in Brooklyn in 1941. He is an American photographer best known for his black and white images depicting people in social situations.
