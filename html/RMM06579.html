BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

John Joseph Brennan, Jr. is a veterinarian. He received his D.V.M. from the New York State College of Veterinary Medicine at Cornell University in 1952.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Class notes and correspondence and miscellaneous papers relating to Brennan's education at the New York State College of Veterinary Medicine at Cornell University. Included are notebooks and annotated texts from classes with Drs. Robert E. Habel, Peter Olafson, William F. Guard, and John H. Whitlock.
