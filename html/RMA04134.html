DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Cornell University ROTC records, 1890-2002.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
33-2-4134
Abstract:
Includes reports, meeting minutes, subject files and photographs pertaining to the Cornell ROTC program.
Creator:
Cornell University. ROTC
Quanitities:
3.3 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes reports, meeting minutes, subject files and photographs pertaining to the Cornell ROTC program and Cornellians who served in the military. Many materials relating to the service of Floyd R. Newman. Letters from soldiers home, many from World War I. Also a 1958 film from The Big Picture series showcasing Cornell.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Cornell University Military Science and ROTC records, #33-2-4134. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Access Restrictions:

Access to individual transcripts in Box 9 restricted to the permission of the student and ROTC office during student's lifetime.
