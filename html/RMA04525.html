Content Description

Collection contains correspondence, teaching material, reports and documentation related to the College of Engineering including Rehkugler's memoir.
Biographical / Historical

Rehkugler is an emeritus professor of Biological and Environmental Engineering, starting at Cornell in July of 1958 and retiring in 1996. He served as Associate Dean for Undergraduate Programs in the College of Engineering from July 1990-1995 and is responsible for organizing the Rau Model Plow Collection.
