DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Edwin R. Hoskins papers, [ca.1911-1962].
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
21-32-1007
Abstract:
Papers concerning administration and supervision of agricultural education; agricultural education curricula; education of teachers; farming programs; agricultural education in secondary schools; institutional on-farm training; and other aspects of agricultural education. Includes reports, surveys, and studies; bulletins; pamphlets; photographs; lectures; corespondence; and other papers.
Creator:
Hoskins, Edwin Raymond, 1891-1982.
Quanitities:
3 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Edwin Raymond Hoskins (Cornell University Class of 1919, M.S. 1924, Ph.D. 1933) was Professor of Rural Education, Cornell University.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Papers concerning administration and supervision of agricultural education; agricultural education curricula; education of teachers; farming programs; agricultural education in secondary schools; institutional on-farm training; and other aspects of agricultural education. Includes reports, surveys, and studies; bulletins; pamphlets; photographs; lectures; corespondence; and other papers.
