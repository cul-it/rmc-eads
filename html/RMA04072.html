Scope and content

Includes reports, publications, and white papers pertaining to the hospitality industry (2003-2012) and Cornell Hospitality Quarterly publication contracts,1995 subscribers study, Elsevier and Sage Publications correspondence and contracts, copyright transfer agreements, copyright clearance (2000-2005), marketing research reports, plans, and proposals.
Scope and content

The Center for Hospitality Research (CHR) is the source for research on and for the hospitality industry. Center Fellows work with business leaders to develop new ideas, theories, and models that improve strategic, managerial, and operating practices. These insights are captured in research reports and industry tools that are available online.
