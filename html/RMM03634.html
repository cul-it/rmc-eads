Abstract:
Correspondence, subject files, constituent bills, reports and case files of Raymond Ashbery from his tenure as New York State Assemblyman. Also, correspondence, case files, estates, wills, and income tax files from Ashberry's private law practice in Trumansburg, N.Y.
Creator:
Ashbery, Ray Stevens, 1902-1974 (Affiliation: (Cornell University Class of 1925)..)
Quanitities:
103 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, subject files, constituent bills, reports and case files of Raymond Ashbery from his tenure as New York State Assemblyman. Also, correspondence, case files, estates, wills, and income tax files from Ashberry's private law practice in Trumansburg, N.Y.
Access Restrictions:

Access to correspondence and other papers restricted to the written permission of donor or heirs.
Restrictions on use:

Case files of Raymond Ashbery's private law practice in Trumansburg, N.Y made available with the understanding that the identity of individuals in these files is not to be made public for eighty years from June 6, 1977. When used in the intervening period, reference may not be made to individual cases.

Conditions Governing Use

Case files of Raymond Ashbery from years in office as a New York State Assemblyman made available with the understanding that the individuals named in the records may not be published or otherwise released in such form that any individual mentioned in the records can be identified.
