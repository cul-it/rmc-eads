Biographical / Historical

Professor of city and regional planning, Cornell University. John Reps, born 1921, received his B.A. from Dartmouth College in 1943 and a Masters of Regional Planning from Cornell University in 1947. He is a member of the American Society of Planning Officials, the American Institute of Planners, and the Society of Architectural Historians. He is the author of The Making of Urban America, 1965; Monumental Washington, 1967; Town Planning in Frontier America, 1968; Canberra 1912: Plans and Planners of the Australian Capital Competitions.1997; Saint Louis Illustrated, 1989; and John Caspar Wild: Painter and Printmaker of Nineteenth-century Urban America, 2006.
