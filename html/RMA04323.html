Biographical / Historical

Professor emeritus in the Department of Design and Environmental Analysis, Cornell University. Dankos design background includes formal training in architecture, industrial, graphic, and interior design. Her scholarship focuses on the intersection of design and leadership with an understanding of creative design process. Danko received a B.S. in architecture from the University of Michigan (1978); and degrees from the College of Creative Studies in Detroit and the Rhode Island School of Design (1983).
