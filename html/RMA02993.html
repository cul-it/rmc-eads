DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Laurence D. Stifel papers, [ca.1970-1994].
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
14-27-2993
Abstract:
Correspondence, notes, professional papers, and subject files relating to Stifel's work in economics and agriculture, with particular reference to Africa, Nigeria, Thailand, and the Philippines.
Creator:
Stifel, Laurence D., 1930-1995.
Quanitities:
11 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Development economist. Secretary of the Rockefeller Foundation, 1974-1984; Director-General of the International Institute for Tropical Development in Ibadan, Nigeria, 1985-1990; Visiting Professor in Cornell University Southeast Asia Program and the Cornell International Institute for Food, Agriculture, and Development, 1991-1995.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, notes, professional papers, and subject files relating to Stifel's work in economics and agriculture, with particular reference to Africa, Nigeria, Thailand, and the Philippines.
