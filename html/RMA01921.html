Cite As:

Biographical / Historical

Professor of classical languages and literature, Cornell University. Harry Caplan received his B.A. in 1916, M.A. in 1917, and Ph.D. in 1921 from Cornell. He taught in Cornells Department of Public Speaking, 1919-1923, and in the Department of Classics, 1924-1980. Professor Caplan died in 1980.
