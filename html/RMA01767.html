COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Records of the Cornell College of Agriculture's Development and Alumni Affairs office include correspondence, proposals, minutes, reports, donor lists, accounts, date books, and other materials relating to corporate and alumni gifts, scholarships, the College of Agriculture and Life Sciences Fund, and other subjects. Names included are Laing E. Kennedy, A. Lee Thompson, William I. Myers, Lansing P. Shield, W. Keith Kennedy, A. Bradford Carruth, Wendell G. Earle, and Elmer L. Townsend. Includes photographs and slides of various events and people, such as David Call and of campus buildings, including the demolition of Roberts Hall in 1990. Collection also includes photographs, chiefly of CALS events from the early 1980s until 2004. Events include reunions, award banquets, Dean-Alumni Get Togethers, and conferences.
