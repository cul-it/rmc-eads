COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes a draft and revision of George Downs Wheeler's autobiography; herd records kept by Charles A. Wheeler; legal documents; family Bible records; genealogy; letters; financial records; and other family papers. Other names in the collection include W. (Mollie) Wheeler; W. A. Gregory; Charles P. Knapp; Henry M. Gregory; William Oringron; Silas Crandall; Thaddeus Mather; William Hawley; William Wheeler; Royal Wheeler; Robert Turner; James Turner; Mrs. George Hewitt; John Peters; Rachel Crandall; Addison J. Wheeler; Asa Babcock; and John Dickinson.
