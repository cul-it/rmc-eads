COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

The collection includes photographs depicting various diplomatic interactions between US and Indonesian diplomats from 1980's to mid 1990's. Some of the most prominent individuals featured in this collection are William Jefferson "Bill" Clinton and his wife Hilliary (1 photo album), Paul Dundes Wolfowitz in his function as US Ambassador to Indonesia (4 photo albums and a set of loose photographs) and James Danforth "Dan" Quayle and his wife Marilyn (18 photo albums and a set of loose photographs). The collection also includes 1 photo album containing photos of the reception for Under Secretary of the Air Force James McGovern at DCM's residence, 1 photo album featuring Pak Atma and 8 envelopes of miscellaneous photographs.
Also, 2 CD-ROMS containing official photos of Susilo Bambang Yudhoyono, Megawati Sukarnoputri, Bacharuddin Jusuf Habibie, Abdurrahman Wahid, Agustadi S.P., Djoko Santoso, Subandrio and Tedjo Edhy P.
