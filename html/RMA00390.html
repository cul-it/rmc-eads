ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Cornell women's organization, formed in 1900 to "welcome newcomers to the campus and to promote friendship among the women at Cornell."
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Secretary's and treasurer's reports, minutes of the executive board and various committees and annual meetings, constitutions, lists of officers, newsletters and brochures, samples of printed forms, reports of activity groups (1936-1941), social reports, newcomers' items (1949-1953), history of the Campus Club, and miscellaneous items. Includes correspondence of Mary H. Donlon, Emily Day, and Helen A. Newman. Also includes a VHS videotape made by the Quilting Group of the Campus Club in 1995 of the presentation of a quilt to Frank and Rosa Rhodes.
RELATED MATERIALS

This collection includes collection #11-8-99.
