Scope and content

Biographical material; reprints and several editions of Hagan and Bruner's Infectious Diseases of Domestic Animals, the seminal veterinary text authored by William Arthur Hagan and Dorsey William Bruner, et al.; and copy inscribed by the authors to Dr. Gillespie of Review of Veterinary Microbiology by Ernst L. Biberstein and Yuan Chung Zee. Also, memoir: "How Lucky Can You Get!", 2004 and two reprints about foot-and-mouth disease in Argentina, 1966 and 1974.
