DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Nelva Weber papers, 1933-1986.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
4840
Abstract:
Correspondence, drawings, published articles, manuscripts of books and articles, biographical material, photoprints and negatives, and sketches; drawings, plans and sketches pertain to various work executed by Weber, particularly for Arrowsmith.
Creator:
Weber, Nelva M.
Quanitities:
8.6 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Landscape architect.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence; drawings and sketches; published articles and manuscripts of books and articles; biographical material; certificates and diplomas; and photographs, slides, and negatives. Drawings, plans and sketches pertain to various work executed by Weber, particularly for Arrowsmith. Slides include pictures of plants and gardens, travel views, including slides of a trip to the Galapagos, and family images. Also 3x5 cards describing varieties of plants. Illustrations to Weber's lectures on geomorphology.
