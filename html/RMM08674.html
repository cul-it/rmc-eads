Language:

Scope and content

Variety of published materials, mostly in languages of Nepal. Many serials, pamphlets, ephemera. Also, a photo album (ca. 1990) made by a Cornell student, presumably one in the Cornell Nepal Study Abroad Program, or perhaps on another trip that took students to Nepal.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Preferred Citation

March and Holmberg collection on Nepal's ethnicities and the People's War, #8674. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Biographical / Historical

Cornell anthropologists Kathryn March and David Holmberg worked in the Himalayas for several decades. They collected intensively on Nepali politics and cultures during the period of the People's War, 1996-2002, and its aftermath.
