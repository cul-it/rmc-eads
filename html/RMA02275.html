Scope and content

Photographs of Daisy Farrand's garden at the Andrew Dickson White House, Cornell University include eight photographs by Margaret Bourke-White and six original prints, three modern prints, two modern negatives of photographs by John Troy; includes photographs before and after renovation. n. Also, recollections of Daisy Farrand by Elizabeth Wells and Kay Butterfield.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Preferred Citation

Margaret Carleton Farrand miscellany, #3275. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Biographical / Historical

Margaret (Daisy) Farrand was the wife of Cornell University president Livingston Farrand. The "Secret Garden" is in back of the Andrew Dickson White House on the Cornell campus.
