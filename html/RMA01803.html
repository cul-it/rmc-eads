DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
School of Hotel Administration Directors records, 1922-1981.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
28-1-1803
Abstract:
Records of the Director's Office and of Howard B. Meek include registration reports as well as school budgets, minutes of faculty and staff meetings, and reports.
Creator:
Cornell University. School of Hotel Administration. Director
Quanitities:
1.1 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Directors and deans represented include Howard B. Meek, Robert A. Beck, and John J. Clark.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

School of Hotel Administration Director's records, #28-1-1803. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Records of the Director's Office include correspondence files, including letters from Joseph Vehling, Martha Van Rensselaer and Flora Rose; biographical files; and subject files, including files of the Courtney Memorial Fund. Records of Howard B. Meek include registration reports, with letters to Meek; correspondence; items relating to a Hotel School European tour; and plaques and awards given to Meek. Also, school budgets, 1954-1978; minutes of faculty and staff meetings, 1935-1981; report of the Education Committee of the American Hotel Association, 1944; and minutes of Deans' Council meetings, 1968-1970.
