COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Artwork on deposit from the Lab of Ornithology. Includes works by George Miksch Sutton, Anna Botsford Comstock, and others.
The John James Audubon, Louis Agassiz Fuertes and John Gould, Arthur Singer art work on deposit for the Lab has been added to the illustrators' corresponding collections.
RELATED MATERIAL

The John James Audubon artwork, #6721, the Louis Agassiz Fuertes papers, #2662 , and the John Gould Artwork Collection, #4736 also contain materials on deposit from the Laboratory of Ornithology The digital collection can be viewed here https://newcatalog.library.cornell.edu/catalog/6280355. Note that not all items in the digital collection have been placed on deposit in the physical collection within the University Archives, a number of items remain on display at the Laboratory of Ornithology.
