COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Brochures from architectural firms showcasing their work. Firms include Amenta/Emma, Bowie Gridley Architects, Burt Hill Kosar Rittelmann Associates, The Design Alliance, EYP Mission Critical Facilities, Inc., Hellmuth, Obata + Kassabaum, Hillier Architecture, HOLT Architects, Mancini Duffy, McCall Design Group, Michael Graves Architects, Odell, Perkins Eastman Architects, Simon Martin Vegue Winkelstein Moris.
Also, "A Proposal for a branch bank of The City National Bank and Trust Company, Gloversville, New York," Edgar Tafel Architect, n.d.; "Schematic Presentation for Atlantic City Performing Arts Center," Taliesin Associated Architects of the Frank Lloyd Wright Foundation, William Wesley Peters, Architect, 1975.
