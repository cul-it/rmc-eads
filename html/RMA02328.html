ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Hotel Ezra Cornell is a student-run annual hotel-for-a-day program at the School of Hotel Administration. The first Hotel Ezra Cornell was staged in Prudence Risley Hall in 1926.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Programs, menus, press releases, broadsides, videocassettes and photographs concerning Hotel Ezra Cornell.
