COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Records of J.W. Spencer as chairman of the Administrative Board of the Laboratory of Ornithology include Administrative Board minutes, budgets, correspondence, and records about the search for a director. Also includes Bird-Watching Days Calendar and Postcards for Project Feeder Watch.
RELATED MATERIAL

All artwork currently on deposit from the Laboratory of Ornithology that was once part of this collection has been moved. Works by Louis Agassiz Fuertes and John James Audubon were relocated to the illustrator's collections, the Louis Agassiz Fuertes papers, 1892-1954, #2662 and the John James Audubon artwork collection, #6721.
The work by George Miksch Sutton, Anna Botsford Comstock, John Gould and others was moved to the Laboratory of Ornithology artwork collection, #17-2-3522.
