BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Chester was professor emeritus of physics and dean emeritus of Cornell's College of Arts and Sciences. He was an authority on liquid helium.
He received a B.S. from Edinburgh University in 1950 and a Ph.D. from King's College of London University in 1953.
