ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Under the name Finger Lakes Alliance on Mental Illness (FLAMI) since 1985 and National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI)- Finger Lakes since it incorporated as a 501c organization in 2006, this group has organized support for and education about having family members and friends with mental illness.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Educational and publicity materials and files on meetings and activities of FLAMI and NAMI-Finger Lakes, arranged basically chronologically, with audiocassettes and ephemera separate at the end.
