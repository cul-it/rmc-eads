Quanitities:
4.3 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Include house minutes of this men's cooperative residence (now co-ed, formerly Llenroc Lodge and Cayuga Student Resident Association), as well as minutes of its executive committee, board of directors, and board of trustees, and of the Inter-Cooperative Council; committee aned financial reports; and constitutions, by-laws, codes, rosters, and programs of the organization. Also, correspondence, mainly of lodge advisor Edward A. Ott, concerning election of officers, functions of the lodge, name changes, and other business; other correspondents include Robert B. Gervan, Hugh A. Moran, and Robert E. Treman. Also the files of William Ballagh which include correspondence and publications documenting the history of the group during the 1930s and 1940s. Also digital scans of the Ballagh material.
RELATED MATERIALS

See also the thesis Building history : how the Cornell family's brick carriage house became Cayuga Lodge by Rebecca Baker.
