Quanitities:
24 cubic feet.
Scope and content

Genetic pedigrees, data, chick books, and other records of a long-term poultry breeding experiment. Also, typed lecture notes from Poultry Course #120, taught by Professor F. B. Hutt, 1936; and a typescript biography of Hutt, 1950
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Preferred Citation
