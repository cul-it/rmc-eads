Scope and content

Scrapbooks, banquet programs from the sorority, and a history. Also newsletters, January and September 1998; 1921 and 1936 volumes of "Songs of Alpha Phi"; biographical information about members; guestbook; obituaries of members; and other miscellany relating to the sorority. Also group portraits, 1950 and 1951; composite photographs, 1956 and 1957; and groups at parties,1950s.

The Delta chapter of Alpha Phi was founded at Cornell University in 1889.
