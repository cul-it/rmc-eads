ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

The Chi chapter of Delta Gamma was founded in 1885 at Cornell University.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Records of the Chi chapter at Cornell of Delta Gamma. Collection contains information regarding history, operations and activities including the Delta Gamma Property Association, Panhellenic items, the Anchora publication, philanthropy, financial items, and roster of members. Also information and photographs pertaining to various conventions and the 1985 centennial and 2010 anniversary celebrations. Includes the original charter given to the national for the Chi chapter.
