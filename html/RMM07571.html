DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Ithaca Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender Task Force records, 1984-2005.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
7571
Abstract:
Correspondence, press releases, original layouts, and other working files from the publication of "OUTLINES: A Newsletter for Ithaca's Lesbian, Gay and Bisexual Community," from the first issue, Dec. 1984 to #23, April/May 1989, and from 1993-1998. Plus, the Ithaca Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender Task Force's publication of local businesses and organizations and mailings sent to members, 1991-2005 (incomplete). One issue of the Big Red Rag.
Creator:
Ithaca Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender Task Force (Ithaca, N.Y.)
Popow, Jeff.
Quanitities:
5.3 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, press releases, original layouts, and other working files from the publication of "OUTLINES: A Newsletter for Ithaca's Lesbian, Gay and Bisexual Community," from the first issue, Dec. 1984 to #23, April/May 1989, and from 1993-1998. Plus, the Ithaca Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender Task Force's publication of local businesses and organizations and mailings sent to members, 1991-2005 (incomplete). One issue of the Big Red Rag.
RELATED MATERIALS

Collecting Program: Human Sexuality Collection.
SEPARATED MATERIAL

The following materials have been cataloged separately:
  1. Act Up Reports, June 1989.
  2. Baltimore Gaypaper, May, June, September, and October 1989.
  3. The Empty Closet, April 1989.
  4. Gay Community News, July 1989.
  5. Hera, August 1989.
  6. Living In Leather, 1989.
  7. Outlines, February/March 1987, February/March/April 1988, Winter Eternal (1988), February/March 1989, April/May 1989, Summer 1989, September 1989, December 1991.
  8. The Washington Blade, November 1989.
