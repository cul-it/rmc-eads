Scope and content

Includes departmental files, 1965-1966; budgets for 1965-1966 and 1966-1967; records of Dean and Directors meetings, 1959-1967; alphabetical files, 1965-1966; agronomy files of Associate Dean W. Keith Kennedy; and records of the Board of Trustees, Committee on the Quality of Undergraduate Instruction at Cornell University, and the Faculty Council. Also records of Associate Dean Kenneth E. Wing.
