COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Campaign posters and other paraphernalia from the major political parties in the Hungarian elections of 1989-1990. Produced by some of Hungary's best graphic artists, these posters are a dramatic depiction of the clash of political symbols in the era of transition to democracy in Eastern Europe.
