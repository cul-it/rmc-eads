Content Description

Collection contains the records from the Journal of Applied Physics. The bulk of the collection represents material from the time R. Sproull served as editor, 1954-1957. Already scheduled papers from the previous administration have kept the names of Professor Gregg and Professor Hutchison, Elme, who served as editors. The Journal of Applied Physics records are divided into accepted papers, including articles and letters to the editor, and rejected, withdrawn, or transferred papers in chronological order.
