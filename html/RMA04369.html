Title:

Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
23-19-4369
Abstract:
Collection includes course material, student projects, proposals and subject files concerning Ashdown's career and early 3-D body scanning work.

Content Description

Collection includes course material, student projects, proposals and subject files concerning Ashdown's career and early 3-D body scanning work.
Biographical / Historical

Emeritus professor in the Department of Fiber Science and Apparel Design, formerly the Department of Textiles and Apparel, in the New York State College of Human Ecology.
