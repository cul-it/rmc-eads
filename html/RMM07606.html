COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Professional and personal subject files pertaining chiefly to the Human Rights Campaign Fund (HRCF), and its work in AIDS education, advocacy, queer politics, women's rights campaigns, and gay and lesbian social and political issues; includes queer ephemera, t-shirts, and buttons, as well as posters from HRCF and Texas political events, such as "Texas March to Stop the Violence, Stop the Hate" and a poster criticizing Senator Kay Bailey Hutchison. One large poster "Save the Amazons: Fight Breast Cancer," has "lesbian librarians" penciled at the bottom. Also, national posters from the 1993 March on Washington for Lesbian, Gay, and Bi Equal Rights and Liberation and other events. Includes publicity materials for San Antionio Black Tie dinners in 1995 and 1997, events that raised money for HRCF and local agencies.
