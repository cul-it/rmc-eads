DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
First Baptist Church of Ithaca records, 1811-2005.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
2004
Abstract:
Materials from the Board of Trustees, boards of deacons and deaconesses, and church meetings; lists of members, baptisms, and funerals; records of women's and youth organizations; Sunday School materials; letters from donors to the church building fund. Tape recordings of services; plans for remodelling the church; records of the Danby Baptist Church and the First Baptist Church of Christ of Ithaca.
Creator:
First Baptist Church of Ithaca
Quanitities:
21.6 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Minutes of the Board of Trustees, boards of deacons and deaconesses, and church meetings; lists of members, baptisms, and funerals; minutes statistics and correspondence of various church officials and boards; constitution; financial records; minutes, reports, accounts, and other records of various women's and youth organizations, including the Women's Missionary Society, Women's Union, and Women's Fellowship; Sunday School rolls and minutes; various other letters, resolutions, pamphlets, scrapbooks, photographs, hymn books, church newsletters, historical data, and letters from donors to the church building fund, 1889-91. There are also tape recordings of services conducted by Rev. Christian B. Jensen; plans for remodelling the church, Clarence A. Martin, architect, 1930-32; and records of the Danby Baptist Church and the First Baptist Church of Christ of Ithaca, 1811-38.
