COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes personnel files and records of applicants for positions, including correspondence of Howard D. Adelmann and letters and grade transcripts of applicants; information concerning fellowships, scholarships, and assistantships; examinations; student grade cards; reports; memoranda; news releases; inventory book of equipment; slides, photographs, and demonstration materials in the Zoology Department, and formulas and instructions for use in a zoological laboratory; seminar, conference, and committee records; report on room estimates for a new building, entitled "Space Requirements in Proposed New Animal Biology Building"; photographs of lampreys, eels, and toads; letters from Professor Edward Bradford Titchener to Professor Simon Henry Gage; a one volume record of zoological specimens, including a partial list of the brain collection of Burt Green Wilder; and other records of the Cornell Zoology Department. Also includes material pertaining to buildings and grounds, the University Policy Committee, the National Science Foundation, and other topics.
