Title:
New York Florists Club records, 1887-1993.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
4532
Abstract:
Minutes, correspondence, financial records, clippings, club notices and newsletters (1909-1987); minutes (1887-1901); photographs of banquets, etc. Subjects include shows, the Fellowship and Scholarship Committee, and other activities pertaining to floriculture.
