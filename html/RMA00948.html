Scope and content

Glass lantern slides; cards with contact prints made from slides; bound volumes of alumni records; photograph of the Civil Engineering faculty (1891-1892); and correspondence (1878-1904) concerning professor John D. Morris and student charges against him of incompetence, as well as other college matters. Also, three group photographs of the Class of 1905, Engineering, including Irvin Norton '05. Includes photo of the Class of 1913 with Dean Albert William Smith belonging to Clifford A. Brown '13.
Scope and content

"American Schools of Marine Engineering and Naval Architecture: School of Marine Construction, Sibley College, Cornell University, Ithaca, New York," in Marine Engineering, May 1899, given to the Library by Robert Henry Thurston. Also, Sibley College Pocket Book for students, 1916-1917.
Scope and content

Eight mimeographed sheets of names of students who withdrew from Sibley College from February 1917 to May 1917, apparently after the U.S. entry into World War I. Reason for withdrawal is listed as well as date of leaving. Most student were going into military service or industrial war work.
Scope and content

Group picture of the senior class of 1930, Sibley School of Mechanical Engineering (identified); mimeographed biographical sketches of the seniors; and three samples of personnel rating sheets, 1930.
