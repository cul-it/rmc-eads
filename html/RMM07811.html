BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Special education teacher at Ithaca's Belle Sherman Elementary School, September 1992- June 2006. Started COLAGE about 1994 and continued thru 2006. Social worker at Fall Creek School, Harriet Alpert, ran a Colage group there and they had a joint event once or twice. Sanders co-led Colage with Duane Diviney and Renee Swatzky (check spelling in current BS staff directory).
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Materials from work leading COLAGE at Belle Sherman Elementary School in Ithaca, NY, 1994-2006, while working as a special education teacher there.
Multimedia materials on the subjects of sexual and gender diversity among young people and families, including: "The GLSEN Lunchbox: A comprehensive training program for ending anti-gay bias in schools" - a kit in a red plastic lunch box, produced by http://www.glsen.org/; "In My Shoes: Stories of Youth with LGBT Parents," Jen Gilomen 2005, DVD produced by COLAGE, with study guide; Judy Shepard PSA Network Version VHS; "Speaking for Ourselves: Portraits of Gay and Lesbian Youths," Diversity Productions, Seattle, VHS, 27 min; "Both of my Moms' Names are Judy: Children of Lesbians and Gays Speak Out," Lesbian and Gay Parents Association, 1994, VHS, 10:16 min; "Gay Pioneers: July 4, 1965, Independence Hall," Equality Forum, DVD - Glenn Holsten, Director, Malcolm Lazin, Exec. Producer.
