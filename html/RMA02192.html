DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Robert Louis Wegryn photonegatives, 1955-1959.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
37-5-2192
Abstract:
Photographs taken for the Cornell Daily Sun, the Cornellian, and the Ithaca Journal of events in and around Ithaca. Subjects include parades and floats; fraternities, particularly Pi Kappa Alpha; and the 1958 riots around President Malott's house.
Creator:
Wegryn, Robert Louis, 1937-
Quanitities:
.5 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Wegryn was a phtographer and photo editor for the Cornell Daily Sun, 1955-1959.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Photographs taken for the Cornell Daily Sun, the Cornellian, and the Ithaca Journal of events in and around Ithaca. Subjects include parades and floats; fraternities, particularly Pi Kappa Alpha; and the 1958 riots around President Malott's house.
