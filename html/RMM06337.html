Title:
John Nolen pamphlet collection, Undated
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
6337
Abstract:
Pamphlets relating to city planning and affiliated issues collected by John Nolen in the course of his career.
Creator:
Nolen, John, 1869-1937
Quanitities:
17.4 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
RELATED MATERIALS

INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

John Nolen pamphlet collection. #6337. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Biographical / Historical

The collection was originally given to the University of North Carolina in Chapel Hill.
Scope and content

Pamphlets relating to city planning and affiliated issues collected by John Nolen in the course of his career.
Scope and content

Also, copies of correspondence relating to the distribution of duplicate copies of the pamphlets to other institutions in 1962.
