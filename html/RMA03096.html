COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Films, videos, and slides of Cornell University lacrosse games. Also audio tape with a sound bite from Richie Moran. Booklet from the 1976 season filled with clippings about the season's games, Cornell Lacrosse Club Annual Awards Banquet programs.
Digitization of films can be arranged if requested.
