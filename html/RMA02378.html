DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Cornell University College of Engineering photographs, 1870-1990.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
16-2-2378
Abstract:
College of Engineering photographic negatives, ca. 1945-1965, unidentified; seven lantern slides of field trips, 1870-1900, identified; photographs of Physics Dept. apparatus, 1870s-1880s, identified; five glass negatives and photographs of the construction of the Hydraulic Laboratory; glass slides depicting College of Engineering equipment and facilities (1870-1900); and photographs of John McMullen with a McMullen Scholarship brochure.
Creator:
Cornell University. College of Engineering
Colby, Victor
Quanitities:
2.8 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Cornell University College of Engineering photographs, #16-2-2378. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

College of Engineering photographic negatives, ca. 1945-1965, unidentified; seven lantern slides of field trips, 1870-1900, identified; photographs of Physics Dept. apparatus, 1870s-1880s, identified; five glass negatives and photographs of the construction of the Hydraulic Laboratory; glass slides depicting College of Engineering equipment and facilities (1870-1900); and photographs of John McMullen with a McMullen Scholarship brochure and electrical engineering photos of equipment, the Arecibo Observatory in Puerto Rico and Professor Victor Colby with his "EE Sculpture" that was installed in Phillips Hall. Includes carte-de-visite photographs of engineering students, 1888, 1893, 1898, 1900, and 1901.
Related Materials

A number of the photographs from this collection have been digitized and can be seen here: http://rmc-images.library.cornell.edu/ .
