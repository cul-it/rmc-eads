COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes sketchbooks, production records, correspondence, exhibition catalogs, articles written by or about Musick, DVD's of interviews and art shows, listings of permanent acquisitions, permanent public installations, outdoor sculpture and site specific installations, solo exhibitions, honors awarded, reprints and reports about art therapy and art psychotherapy, audio cassettes of interviews and photographs.
