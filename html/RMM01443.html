COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Time tables, notices, circulars, employee agreements, material pertaining to the maintenance of equipment, freight and passenger account books, brass checks for non-English speaking employees, special passes, a typescript history of the two railroads, and other material for the New York and Oswego Midland Railroad and its successor, the New York, Ontario and Western Railway Co., and many connecting lines.
NOTES

Includes New York and Western Railroad papers, #2058.
