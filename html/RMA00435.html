DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Alexander M. Drummond papers, 1917-1956.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
14-24-435
Abstract:
Correspondence, news clippings, scripts, programs, playbills, lecture notes, theater notes, photographs, recordings and other memorabilia relating to various aspects of the field of Speech and Drama.
Creator:
Drummond, A. M. (Alexander Magnus), 1884-1956.
Van Loon, Hendrik Willem, 1882-1944.
Shaw, Bernard, 1856-1950.
Hammerstein, Oscar, II, 1895-1960.
O\'Neill, Eugene, 1888-1953.
Quanitities:
19.2 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Professor of Speech and Drama, Cornell University.
Organized the Cornell Dramatic Club and was its Director from 1912-1947. Wrote original plays and a monograph on play production.
