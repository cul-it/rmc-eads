ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

The Alpha Psi Chapter of Chi Psi Fraternity was established at Cornell University in 1869. In 1896, the fraternity moved into the McGraw-Fiske Mansion. On December 6th, 1906, the Chi Psi house caught fire, killing four fraternity brothers and three volunteer fireman.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Annual reports; histories, including materials on the 1906 fire; correspondence; financial records; personnel records; pledging materials; and photographs of Chi Psi Fraternity.
