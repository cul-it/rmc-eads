Title:

Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
8716
Abstract:
A group of pamphlets, newsletters, and other underground or ephemeral publications focused on the topics of vegetarianism, organic eating and food systems, sustainable forest and land management, collective farming, commune living, survivalism, alternative lifestyles, anti-nuclear efforts, human rights and fair labor practices.

Content Description

A group of pamphlets, newsletters, and other underground or ephemeral publications focused on the topics of vegetarianism, organic eating and food systems, sustainable forest and land management, collective farming, commune living, survivalism, alternative lifestyles, anti-nuclear efforts, human rights and fair labor practices.
