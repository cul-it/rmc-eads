ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

The New York State College of Veterinary Medicine was founded in 1894.The College was opened for classes in 1896. The faculty has had regular meetings since this date.
RELATED MATERIAL

Another set of faculty minutes is housed in the Flower-Sprecher Veterinary Library. Access to that collection is also restricted to faculty of the College of Veterinary Medicine.

INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

New York State College of Veterinary Medicine Faculty Minutes, # 24-02-1672. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Minutes of faculty meetings of the New York State College of Veterinary Medicine at Cornell University, 1896-1985. An index for 1964-1972 is included. Also minutes from 2005-2007 and 2008-2009 (2 volumes).
Access Restrictions:

Access restricted to faculty of the College of Veterinary Medicine.
