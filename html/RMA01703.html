BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Cornell University LL.B 1888. Edwin Hamlin was Dean of the Cornell Law School from 1916-1921, and taught there until 1927.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, certificates, and a small scrapbook of newspaper clippings and handwritten quotations. Letters are primarily from associates at Stanford University and students from Cornell University; correspondents include Morris Bishop, George Gleason Bogert, Julian Pleasant Britz, Lord Charnwood, Lewis Clinton, Frederick Diamond Colson, Edmund Ezra Day, John Charles Lounsberry Fish, Felix Frankfurter, James Picken Harrold, George S. Hellman, Mary (Mrs. Timothy) Hopkins, Oscar Lewis, Robert Harper Murray, Jackson E. Reynolds, Sao-Ke Alfred Sze, Martin Travieso, Joseph Edgar Uihlein, and Frederick Campbell Woodward.
