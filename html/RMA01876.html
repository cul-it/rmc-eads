BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Lowell Fritz Randolph (Cornell University Ph.D. 1921) was professor of botany at Cornell University.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Manuscripts, correspondence, reports, publications, notes, photographs, slides, field notes, and other papers concerning corn, iris breeding and his trips to Mexico, Honduras, Guatemala, the West Indies, Europe, India, Soviet Union (USSR) and the Middle East.
