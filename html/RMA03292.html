DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Expanded Food and Nutrition Education Program records, 1969-2019.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
21-24-3292
Abstract:
Includes reports, correspondence, in-service projects, subject files, photographs, videotapes, slides, and curriculum material pertaining to this federal nutrition program (EFNEP). Sponsored by Cornell Cooperative Extension, including records on WIC.
Creator:

COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes reports, correspondence, in-service projects, subject files, photographs, videotapes, slides, nutrition educators training material and curriculum material pertaining to this federal nutrition program (EFNEP. Sponsored by Cornell Cooperative Extension including records on WIC.
