COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Original fanzines, magazines, sound recordings, photographs, and posters documenting Black Metal music, including the production files for the book "True Norwegian black metal: we turn in the night consumed by fire" with photographs by Peter Beste.
6 vinyl records by Ulver/Mysticum, Burzum, Abruptum, and Mayhem.
