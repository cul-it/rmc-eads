COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Two scrapbooks depicting the academic and social life of a Cornell University undergraduate, with particular reference to her courtship with Paul Atterdig (Cornell Class of 1946); includes photographs, clippings, ephemera, and other material with reference to the campus V-12 Program, and many other social activities.
