INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Cornell Athletics Records, #40-1-370. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Athletic event programs and booklets, photographs, annual reports, advertisements, plaques, cups, films and clippings concerning various Cornell athletic events including women's athletics, and a certificate of incorporation of the Cornell Athletic Association. Also Cornell Victorious, the Cornell Campaign for Athletics, ca. 1990, V-790; Design plans for the Bartels Hall (formerly Alberding Field House), V-6028; Illini Reflections, ca. 1990 overview of past University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign events and life as presented by past university alumni. Included are Judi Ford-Johnson '73, Roger Ebert '64, Roger Plummer '84, Lester Leutwiler '29, Tom Haller '58, Ray Nitschke '58, and Dick Butkus '64, V-6029. Films include: Here We Are Again, 2 copies, ca. 1985, film about the history of Cornell athletics, F-478, 479; Stanford Today, 1985, film about Stanford University and its athletic programs, F-480. Also a film of a Cornell Soccer game ca. 1958-1960, F-390. Also slides from the 1974 Cornell and Cornellians presentation by Laurence Koplik.
Restrictions on Use:

Due to the fragility and potential degradation of moving image and sound recordings, viewing and listening is limited to items that have been digitized. If an item is in another media format, you may request to have the item digitized for access. Information on ordering access copies may be found on the Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections webpage. Audio visual media that has been digitized: F-390.
Folders for individual athletes and coaches may contain personally identifiable information and need to be reviewed before being made available.
