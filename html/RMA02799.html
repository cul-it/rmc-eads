BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Helen Powell Smith joined the Department of Textiles and Clothing as Acting Assistant Professor and extension specialist in clothing in 1937. She was appointed Assistant Professor in 1939 and Associate Professor in 1943. In 1952 she became head of the department, a position she held until her retirement in 1958.
