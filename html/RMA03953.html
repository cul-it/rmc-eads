DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Cornell Club of Maryland records, [ca. 1940-2003]
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
41-6-3953
Abstract:
Includes meeting minutes; subject files, including materials on activities and scholarships; newsletters; and files from the earlier separate Cornell Women's Club of Maryland.
Creator:
Cornell Club of Maryland
Quanitities:
3.3 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Contains information from over 60 years about the Cornell Club of Maryland. Includes meeting minutes, information on clubs, activities and scholarships that the Club was involved in, including parties and alumni get-togethers. Also has lists of members throughout the years, and different descriptions of Cornell throughout the years.
