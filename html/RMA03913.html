BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Professor in the Department of Design and Environmental Analysis, Cornell University.
Jennings received a B.S. in Housing and Interior Design (1968) and a M.S. in Housing and Urban Studies (1969) both from Oklahoma State University.
Jenning's research interests focus on oridnary American buildings and interiors, as well as contemporary interior design history, theory, and criticism.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Exhibit items, records and related material for an exhibition researched and designed by Jennning's undergraduate and graduate students (2003-2006).
Includes slides used for teaching; prints and negatives used in her book Cheap and Tasteful Dwellings; photos and slides of student winners of various competitions (1993-2005); information about Interior Archetypes Research and Teaching Project , initiated in 1997.
