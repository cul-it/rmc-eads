Cornell University.

COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, notes, drafts, financial records pertaining to the writing and publishing of GOOD SPORTS; also, correspondence concerning the United State Olympic Committee (1980-1987) and the 1980 Moscow Olympics. Includes John Marcham's memos and other material pertaining to organizing the book.
Also, subject, working, and reference files pertaining to Kane's Directorship of Athletics at Cornell.
