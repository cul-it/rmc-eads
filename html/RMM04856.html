DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Francis G. Scheider papers, 1940-1988.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
4856
Abstract:
Subject files, field records, reports, and checklists, photographs, and other papers of an expert birder from the Rochester, New York area; records pertain mostly to Central New York State, but include accounts of trips to Alaska, Florida, and other places.
Creator:
Scheider, Francis G., 1932-1989.
Quanitities:
9.3 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Francis G. Scheider was a birder, amateur ornithologist, and field collaborator of the Cornell University Laboratory of Ornithology.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Subject files, field records, reports, and checklists, photographs, and other papers of an expert birder from the Rochester, New York area; records pertain mostly to Central New York State, but include accounts of trips to Alaska, Florida, and other places.
Series I. Alphabetical subject files Boxes 1-3, 10
Series II. Chronological records and reports Boxes 3-12
