Abstract:

ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

In 1992, a Department of Fruit and Vegetable Science was established, combining the Departments of Vegetable Science and Pomology. On July 1, 2000, the Department of Horticulture was established, combining the Department of Fruit and Vegetable Science with the Department of Floriculture and Ornamental Horticulture.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Survey and research data, subject files, and other records deriving from the Department, and pertaining to vegetable science and industry in New York State.
Also, four Vegetable Crops/Fruit and Vegetable Science/Horticulture department seminar books, 1961-1972, 1972-1984, 1984-1995; 1996-2003, with listing of weekly seminars, presentations by graduate students, faculty and visitors, and signatures of attendees.
