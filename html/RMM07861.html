Biographical / Historical

Hoshang Merchant is known as India's first openly gay poet. He was educated in Mumbai and in the USA, and has traveled an taught widely, now residing in Hyderabad. Merchant describes his writing as 'therapeutic.'.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Preferred Citation

Hoshang Merchant papers, #7861. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Collection contains papers, correspondence, clippings, and photos that document Hoshang Merchant's poetry, family, travels, and personal life.
