Content Description

Programs, flyers, posters, and playbills associated with the production of Baldwin's plays; a complete set of bookstore promotional materials by Dial Press advertising the publication of The Fire Next Time and reissue of his backlist, and two recordings on vinyl. Also included are ephemera related to Baldwin's participation in the Civil Rights Movement.
Due to the fragility and potential degradation of moving image and sound recordings, viewing and listening is limited to items that have been digitized. If an item is in another media format, you may request to have the item digitized for access. Information on ordering access copies may be found on the Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections webpage.
