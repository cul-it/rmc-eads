INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Delta Kappa Epsilon, Delta Chi chapter records, #37-4-1535. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Minutes, membership lists, initiation records, photographs, typescript narrative history, pamphlets, directories, catalogs, brochures, and memorabilia of Delta Kappa Epsilon. Includes fund raising correspondence and other papers of Walter Bruske concerning Delta Kappa Epsilon at Cornell University; petition from H. William Fogle, Alumni Secretary, to the New York State Dept. of Environmental Conservation to place two Norway spruce on the New York State Famous and Historic Trees Register, 1987; and petition from the Delta Chi Association to the National Register of Historic Places. Also, "The Delta Chi Deke" newsletter, 1935-1991; 1988 Pledge Class DKE history project research tasks binder; and report of the Alumni Historian, 1988. Also, an 1870 Cornellian listing Delta Upsilon as an "Anti-Secret Society." Includes an 1870 Charter, and a 1908 replacement charter. THE CORNELL DEKE HOUSE by William Fogle. Autographed color photographs of Gerald Ford, George Bush, and George W. Bush. Also a recording of the Muskrat Ramblers Dixieland Jazz Band performing at the DKE house at Spring Weekend in May 1967.
Restrictions on Use:

Reproductions require the permission of the President of Delta Chi Chapter, Delta Kappa Epsilon.
