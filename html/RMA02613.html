DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Harold Shadick papers, 1915-1992.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
14-13-2613
Abstract:
Papers of Harold Shadick include files relating to his career as Professor of Chinese Literature, 1946-1971, on administration of graduate study, miscellaneous teaching materials, and letters of recommendation; files as Director of the China Program, 1950-1966, on development of faculty positions, faculty, graduate students, development of the Wason Collection, relations of the Program within and outside the University.
Creator:
Shadick, Harold.
Quanitities:
32.6 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Harold Shadick was Professor of Chinese Literature at Cornell University.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Papers of Harold Shadick include files relating to his career as Professor of Chinese Literature, 1946-1971, on administration of graduate study, miscellaneous teaching materials, and letters of recommendation; files as Director of the China Program, 1950-1966, on development of faculty positions, faculty, graduate students, development of the Wason Collection, relations of the Program within and outside the University.
Includes correspondence, notes, daybooks, class lists, memoirs, and teaching materials. Also, CHINOPERL correspondence and records (Conference on Chinese Oral and Performing Literature).
