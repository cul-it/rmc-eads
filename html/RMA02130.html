DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Kappa Delta Rho, Beta chapter records, 1913-1988.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
37-4-2130
Abstract:
Minutes, certificates, correspondence, photographs, pamphlets, and pledge meeting cards of Kappa Delta Rho fraternity at Cornell University. Also includes correspondence of William I. Myers and files kept by Horace Shackelton, 1942-1988.
Creator:
Fraternity of Kappa Delta Rho. Beta Chapter (Cornell University)
Quanitities:
2.5 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Minutes, certificates, correspondence, photographs, pamphlets, and pledge meeting cards of Kappa Delta Rho fraternity at Cornell University. Also includes correspondence of William I. Myers and files kept by Horace Shackelton, 1942-1988. KDR pin belonging to Robert R. Usher '21.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Kappa Delta Rho, Beta chapter records, #37-4-2130. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Biographical / Historical

The Beta chapter of Kappa Delta Rho was established at Cornell University on May 24, 1913.
