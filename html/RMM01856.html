BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

William Henry Miller (1848-1922), was a student of the Cornell University College of Architecture from 1868 to 1870, later becoming a prominent Ithaca, New York architect. He designed several buildings on the Cornell University campus.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Drawings, photographs (positive and negative), plans, and specifications of Ithaca and Cornell buildings by Miller, list of newspaper articles concerning Miller and notable architecture in Ithaca (1870-1917), and a biographical sketch of Miller by Dethlefsen.
