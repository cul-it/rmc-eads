BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Photographer of erotic, sexual, and radical sexual subjects since 1977. The Seattle Erotic Art Festival named Rosen a Master of Erotic Art in 2012, and the Society of Janus inducted him into its Hall of Fame in 2014. Four books of his images have been published: Sexual Magic: the S/M Photographs in 1986, Sexual Portraits: Photographs of Radical Sexuality in 1990, Sexual Art: Photographs That Test the Limits in 1994, and Lust & Romance: Rated X Fine Art Photographs in 1998. His images also appeared in Femalia (1993, Down There Press), I Am My Lover (1997, Down There Press, second edition), Vanilla Sex (2007), Slow Dance (2016), and Jump (2017).
