Weston Milliken is a philantropist. The Weston Milliken papers document Milliken's grantmaking, organizing and activism.

Weston Miliken interview
Content Description

The Weston Milliken papers document Milliken's grantmaking, organizing and activism. His papers include MAP and NQAPIA reports and conference programs, grant material from Queer Youth Fund, Kicking Assets Fund, The Freeman Foundation and ADaM, articles and clippings, ephemera, The Personage newsletters, material on Creating Change and Funding Forward (National LGBTQ Task Force) and general material on LGBT.
Biographical / Historical

Weston Milliken is a philantropist.
