Deborah C. Hoard (M.P.S. '78) is the president and producer for the company.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes sound mix elements, A and B roll cut negatives, camera reports, 16mm films and VHS tapes, for "Cornell II", "Cornell III" ("Creating the Future"), "Cornell IV", "Cornell V", "Family Matters" which was made in collaboration with Moncrieff Cochran and "Families in the Balance:Child Care in America" made in collaboration with Urie Bronfenbrenner.
