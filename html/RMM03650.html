COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Consists primarily of printed items, newspapers, broadsides, brochures, clippings, newsletters, and pamphlets, relating to Cornell University and its student organizations and other organizations. Includes notes and correspondence. Topics documented include the Black Panther Party; Bobby Seale; Collegetown disturbance, Ithaca, New York, May 13, 1972; Cornell Aeronautical Laboratory (CAL); Giap-Cabral Community; Ithaca Labor Committee; May Day 1971; New American Movement; New Haven 9; the occupation of Carpenter Hall, Cornell University; Students for a Democratic Society; TIOHERO, leaflets gathered in San Francisco, 2007-2008. A number of circa 1972 materials, some published by Glad Day Press, from Ithaca Anti-War Coalition, Peoples Coalition for Peace and Justice, Ithaca United Action Against Imperialism, Peoples Anti-Fascist Resistance League, Southern African Liberation Committee, Ad hoc Committee to Combat Cornell Complicity, and the Committee of Concerned Asian Scholars at Cornell. Poster for The Red Detachment of Women event sponsored by the New China Study Group, China Program Students.
