Biographical / Historical

Professor in Textiles and Clothing, New York State College of Home Economics, Cornell University.
Biographical / Historical

. Frances Spratt received her M.S. from Cornell in 1949. She taught for four years at the University of Texas and then returned to Cornell in 1953 as a faculty member. In 1967 she retired as an associate professor in the Department of Textiles and Clothing.
