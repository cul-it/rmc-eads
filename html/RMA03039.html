DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Gligor Tashkovich papers, 1983-2015.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
41-5-3039
Abstract:
Files relating to student and alumni activities and campus governance including the Committee to Review the Dean of Students Office, Quill and Dagger, the Public Safety Advisory Board, the Campus Store Administrative Board, and the Budget Policies Committee, the Career Center of the Johnson Graduate School of Management, and fundraising efforts for the Johnson School and for the Campus Life Campaign Committee (with memorabilia from the campaign). Also, "Helping Cornell Begin its Journey Towards Legendary Service: Highways, Bumpy Roads and Potholes," by Kenneth Blanchard and Gligor Tashkovich. Program for 1984 memorial service for Erin Nieswand and Young Hee Suh, students murdered in 1983, 1984, with photographs of memorial trees and plaque dedicated to their memory. Folder with material concerning the Membership Solicitation Program. Clippings relating to the Cornell Board of Trustees and the Student Assembly.
Creator:
Tashkovich, Gligor.
Blanchard, Kenneth H. (Title of work: Helping Cornell begin its journey towards legendary service..)
Quanitities:
31.2 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Cornell University Class of 1987, M.B.A. '91. In 2006, he was named Minister for Foreign Investment in the new Government in the Republic of Macedonia.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Gligor Tashkovich papers, #41-05-3039. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Files relating to student and alumni activities and campus governance including the Committee to Review the Dean of Students Office, Quill and Dagger, the Public Safety Advisory Board, the Campus Store Administrative Board, and the Budget Policies Committee, the Career Center of the Johnson Graduate School of Management, and fundraising efforts for the Johnson School and for the Campus Life Campaign Committee (with memorabilia from the campaign). Also, "Helping Cornell Begin its Journey Towards Legendary Service: Highways, Bumpy Roads and Potholes," by Kenneth Blanchard and Gligor Tashkovich. Program for 1984 memorial service for Erin Nieswand and Young Hee Suh, students murdered in 1983, 1984, with photographs of memorial trees and plaque dedicated to their memory. Folder with material concerning the Membership Solicitation Program. Clippings relating to the Cornell Board of Trustees and the Student Assembly.
Scope and content

Also, holiday cards and other correspondence relating to Cornell activities, and letters from Cornell units about 9/11.
Scope and content

Files of brochures, programs, invitations, etc. relating to Seal & Serpent, the College of Arts & Sciences, the Johnson School of Management, the Law School, and the College of Veterinary Medicine; to the Cornell Association of Class Officers, the Asian Alumni Association, the Chimes, the Big Red Band, the Johnson Art Museum, the Library, and the Plantations. Also, program from the NAACP National Equal Justice Award Dinner honoring Jeffrey Lehman.
Scope and content

Also, materials relating to Macedonia, Albania, Azerbaijan, Bulgaria, Rumania, and other Balkan countries. Including files about the Center for Civil and Human Rights (CCHR), the only organization dedicated to serving the needs of and advocating for the LGBT community in Macedonia. Files relating to the Council on Foreign Relations. Copies of "Russian Petroleum Investor," 1994-1999; and other materials on oil pipelines. Materials relating to the elections in Kazakhstan for which Tashkovich was an election observer, 2004. Materials from the Election Observation Mission for the Presidential Election in Azerbaijan, 2008, for which he was an observer. T-shirts from the Olympics in Sidney, Australia, 2000; Athens, 2004; and Beijing, 2008; hat from Salt Lake City Olympics, 2002. Materials from the Election Observation Mission, General Elections, Bosnia and Herzegovina, 03 October 2010.
Scope and content

Also, items collected after becoming Minister for Foreign Investment, Republic of Macedonia. Holiday cards and invitations from Macedonia, 2006.
Scope and content

File of about 90 (Nigerian) advance-fee scam Internet letters, collected by Tashkovich.
Scope and content

T-shirt for Bidzina Ivanishvili from the October 1, 2012 Parliamentary elections in Georgia, the election in which billionaire Ivanishvili's opposition Georgian Dream coalition won a majority of seats.
Scope and content

Card and letters, 1983-2013.
Scope and content

Cornell University-related mailings and memorabilia, memorabilia about Tashkovich's international travel, information about various organizations he is or has been involved with, and printouts of scam emails.
