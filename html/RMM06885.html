BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

This collection comes principally from a donation made to Cornell by the Bolivian bookseller, Alfredo Montalvo, who has supplied the university with library materials for over a quarter century.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

The pamphlets document a century of Bolivian literate culture, beginning in 1848. They show a nation's struggle to establish viable institutions, to develop its economy, to educate its children and the back and forth of political argument.
RELATED MATERIAL

Pamphlets have been digitized and are available online in the Alfredo Montalvo Bolivian Digital Pamphlets Collection.
