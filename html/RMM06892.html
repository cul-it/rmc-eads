Scope and content

Scanned police interrogations relating to specific acts of terrorism in Indonesia by Jemaah Islamiyah and other groups, including bombings in Bali, Abu Bakar Ba'asyir, the Poso detainees, the Mariott bombing, and the Austrialian embassy bombing.
