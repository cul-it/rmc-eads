DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Advocate Research and Education Foundation records, 1978-1981.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
7310
Abstract:
The collection pertains chiefly to the Advocate Experience, an EST-derived seminar for lesbians and gays founded by David B. Goodstein. The contains various materials from 1978 to 1981 of the Advocate Research and Education Foundation Board and the Advocate Experience staff, and manuals pertaining to the Advocate Experience and the Outrageous Experience (a seminar for instructors).
Creator:
Advocate Research and Education Foundation
Quanitities:
12.5 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

In the 1970s, David B. Goodstein created the Advocate Research and Education Foundation, a non-profit structure through which to conduct the "Advocate Experience." Initially the Experience was a one-weekend seminar similar to Werner Erhard's EST, only targeted at a gay audience. The foundation was eventually combined with the Mariposa Education and Research Foundation.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

The collection is divided into four series: Advocate Research and Education Foundation and Advocate Experience Correspondence and Memos' and Alphabetical Subject File; Facilitator Information Sheets; and an Audio and Video cassette series. The Facilitator Information Sheets are restricted until 2041.
