ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

The Vice Provost for Undergraduate Education coordinates and oversees wide areas of undergraduate education relevant to all seven undergraduate schools and colleges. Members of the office work closely with the schools and colleges and other offices across the University to promote academic success for all undergraduate students and cultivate intellectual inquiry, both in and out of the classroom.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Files relating to the operation of the office under multiple individuals including Isaac Kramnick, Michelle Moody Adams, Laura Brown and, Rebecca Stoltzfus. Files include documentation for the New Student Reading Project, Menschel Fellow Awards, the Task Force for Ethnic Studies, Committee on Race and Ethnicity, Council for Undergraduate Research, Diversity Week, Einaudi Center, Intensive English Program, English for Academic Purposes, Asian/Asian American Campus Climate Task Force, Diversity in the Curriculum, humanities, race course, September 11th programs, Phi Kappa Phi, other student services.
