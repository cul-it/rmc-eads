BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Richard M. Juang and W. Carter Smith originated the idea for this conference and were part of the Organizing Committee.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Richard M. Juang's planning documents for the TransPositions conference that took place at Cornell University on March 27-29, 1998. Also, the program and the curriculum vitae and speeches of some of the speakers.
