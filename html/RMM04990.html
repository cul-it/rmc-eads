Series I. Writing & Performance Materials
Box 1
Elementary / High School and early University Writings
 1969-1981
Scope and Contents
Poems, typed and hand-written, primarily from elementary school; a high school biology project; report cards; a high school graduation certificate; academic essays at the high school and undergraduate-level; song lyrics; a published poem in Praxis (Volume 4, Number 2); a blue-hued idea notebook (1978-1979); and an essay, titled "Universe Unfolding," marked by Wolberger.
Box 1
Academic, Religious, and Musical Writings
 1981-1987
Scope and Contents
Musical notation; essays on the Torah and Judaism; poems; a copy of Wolberger's oral examination on ethnomusicology and Jewish music (1987); performance posters; and, a copy of Wolberger's MA thesis.
Box 1
Professional, Dramatic, and Musical Writings
 1987-1996
Scope and Contents
Letters of recommendation from Yale University; a certificate of appreciation from the American Zionist Movement Zionist Song Competition; musical notation; draft copy of Love in the First Degree: a Musical in One Act; copy of Rainbow Warrior; musical notation and draft copy of The Enchanted; and, draft copy of Peace Cantata.
