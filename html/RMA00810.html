BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Professor of Agricultural Engineering.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes reports and minutes of the New York Farm Electrification Council and notes, data and correspondence pertaining to bulk milk cooling and other projects; correspondence concerning the work of the Council, agricultural engineering, and the installation and operation of electrically powered farm equipment, as well as correspondence and material concerning the American Society of Agricultural Engineers; reports, bulletins, articles, and addresses by Turner and others concerning the use of electricity on the farm, with related photographs. Also, notes on tile drainage and material for his course Agricultural Engineering 101.
Also, paper on "The Poole Electric Clock," by Professor Turner, 1987, published in the Bulletin of the National Association of Watch and Clock Collectors, Inc., August 1988.
