ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Formerly the Regional Science Association. Academic and professional individuals concerned with practice and advancement of urban and regional analysis and related studies.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, conference meeting files, papers delivered, annual meeting records, subject files pertaining to North American meetings, Summer Institute and workshop files, Council minutes, RSA Newsletters, proposals, research and editorial files, audiotapes, publications, and other records of the organization; includes personal correspondence and working files of David E. Boyce, conference coordinator and program chair of North American meetings.
Includes material related to organizing the 1976 North American Annual Meeting of the Regional Science Association
