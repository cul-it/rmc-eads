COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

This collection contains programs of concerts and recitals by Cornell and non-Cornell groups that took place at Cornell University. Cornell groups represented in the collection include the Wind Symphony, Chorus, Chamber Orchestra, Symphony Orchestra, Contemporary Chamber Players, Percussion Ensemble, Wind Ensemble, Cornell Jazz Festival, Cornell Glee Club and others. Also programs for Cornell United Religious Works and Sage Chapel events and services such as organ recitals and vespers.
