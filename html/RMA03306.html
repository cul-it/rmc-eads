ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

The Division was formed in 1992 with the combination of the Department of Rare Books, the Department of Manuscripts and University Archives, the History of Science Collections, and the Icelandic Collection.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Division exhibit items, records, and related material.
Also DVDs of Reunion and other exhibition-related lectures including "History of Wine," by Thomas Pinney, June 5, 2008; "The Collector and the Editor: Tales from the Mark Twain Papers," Robert H. Hirst, April 23, 2010; and "All Full of Tears and Flapdoodle: The Genius of Mark Twain," June 10, 2010.
Copy of exhibition guest book and visitor questionnaires, 2008-2009.
