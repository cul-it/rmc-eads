Abstract:
Videotapes of sessions from the symposium and published proceedings, "Future Directions in Electrical Engineering: Proceedings of the Cornell University Electrical Engineering Centennial Symposium series, March 15-June 12, 1985," edited by Simpson Linke. Topics discussed include technological innovations in electrical engineering and electronics, social aspects of electrical engineering, space sciences, and power resources.
