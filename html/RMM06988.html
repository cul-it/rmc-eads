Biographical / Historical

Diacritics is a forum for scholars writing on the problems of literary criticism, based in the Dept. of Romance Studies at Cornell University, published by The Johns Hopkins University Press. It was founded in 1971 under the editorship of David Grossvogel.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Records relating to the management of the journal. Includes administrative files, proofs and galleys, issues of Diacritics, submissions and reviews of articles. Also interviews with individuals involved in the publication.
