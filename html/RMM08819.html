Abstract:

Creator:

Content Description

Ephemeral publications documenting cultures of resistance in response to economic, social, and ecological problems caused by unrestrained corporate profit, including grassroots movements seeking environmental sustainability and anti-capitalist models. The materials were actively collected at protest marches and rallies in public places.
