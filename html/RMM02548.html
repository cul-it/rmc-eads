COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence; diary (1962) of Requardt's tour of Scandinavia, Holland, and West Germeny; reports; certificates; clippings; photographs; press releases; biographical sketches on the Requardt family; and printed and photocopied items, mainly concerning Requardt's student years at Cornell University, his participation in alumni activities, his career in sanitary engineering, and his interest in national politics and public affairs. Includes material relating to his partnership in Whitman, Requardt, and Associates (Baltimore, Maryland). Chief correspondents include John Marshall Butler, George H. Fallon, and Herbert R. O'Conor.
