COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, minutes, reports, and other papers relating to various departments in the College and at Cornell, particularly regarding issues of funding, research, appointments, and projects, and relations with the United States Department of Agriculture. Directors include Carl E. F. Guterman, Charles E. Palm, and W. Keith Kennedy. Also includes records of the Sugar Beet project.
