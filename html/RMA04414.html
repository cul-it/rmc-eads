Content Description

The Thomas Gold papers (1954-1986) contains publications, tape recordings, correspondence, clippings, and photographs related to Tommy Gold and his research. The collection also contains reports and photographs related to the planning and construction of the Arecibo Observatory.
Biographical / Historical

Thomas Gold (May 22, 1920-June 22, 2004) was an Austrian-born astrophysicist and professor of astronomy at Cornell University.
