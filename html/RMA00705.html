Scope and content

Records of the Acquisitions Dept. include correspondence of Felix Reichmann and Arthur Sweet; miscellaneous correspondence; general correspondence and fund books (1898-1948); and correspondence with vendors, 1985-1986.
Scope and content

Accessions volumes (1874-1948), including Andrew Dickson White's personal library, 1881-1891 (vol. 3), Zarncke Library, 1893-1899 (vol. 11), Thurston Collection, 1905 (vol. 24), Barnes Collection, 1913-1947 (vols. 49-50), Comstock Library, 1914-1947 (vols. 52-53), Goldwin Smith Library, 1907-1947 (vols. 54-56); Wason Collection, 1918-1947 (vols. 63-64); Dante Collection, 1907-1947 (vols. 66-67); Petrarch Collection, 1907-1943 (vol. 65); and Maps, 1898-1946 (vols. 87-88) Also, Chemistry Library accession books (7 vols., 1927-1962); Latin Seminary accession book, (1892-1914); and catalog of the Franz Bopp collection.
Scope and content

Includes printouts from ACQNET, the acquisitions listserv from 1990-1994.
Scope and content

"Cost of Current Periodicals, Showing the Amounts Charged to the General Periodical Fund and to the Book Appropriations for the Respective Subjects from the Sage Endowment Fund," 1911 and "Statement Concerning the Distribution of the Book Funds of Cornell University Library, 1911-1912."
