ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

The Cornell University College of Veterinary Medicine Annual Conference for Veterinarians began in 1909 when the college was still known as the New York State College of Veterinary Medicine. The purpose of the conference was and is continuing education for veterinarians in New York State.
