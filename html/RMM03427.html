DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Waldemar H. Fries Audubon research papers, 1805-1980.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
3427
Abstract:
Correspondence, manuscripts, and other materials relating to the writing and production of The Double Elephant Folio; correspondence, slides, books, notebooks,pamphlets and artwork relating to John James Audubon and his family, and other ornithologists. Also, material concerning Audubon exhibitions, including catalogues, Audubon slides, stamps, and photographs.
Creator:
Fries, Waldemar H., 1889-
Quanitities:
17.2 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English German French
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Waldemar Hans Fries graduated from Cornell University in 1911.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, manuscripts, and other materials relating to the writing and production of The Double Elephant Folio; correspondence, slides, books, notebooks,pamphlets and artwork relating to John James Audubon and his family, and other ornithologists. Also, material concerning Audubon exhibitions, including catalogues, Audubon slides, stamps, and photographs.
RELATED MATERIAL

The John James Audubon artwork, collection #6721 also contains original and reproductions of Audubon prints.
