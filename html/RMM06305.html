INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Dorothy Simmons collection of Laura (Riding) Jackson letters, #6305. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Biographical / Historical

Dorothy Simmons was a sculptress and author, and member of a literary circle that included American poet and critic Laura (Riding) Jackson.
Biographical / Historical

Laura (Riding) Jackson was a poet, critic, and author best known for her association with the Fugitives in the Thirties, for her literary partnership with Robert Graves, and as the editor of Epilogue.
