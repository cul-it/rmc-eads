Scope and content

Contains out-takes of "In the Year of the Pig," a film about the Vietnam War that includes French-era, combat, and rare Ho Chi Minh footage, dating between 1964 to 1968.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Preferred Citation

Emile DeAntonio, Collector. In the Year of the Pig, #7038. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Biographical / Historical

Emile DeAntonio was born in Scranton, Pennsylvania, and graduated from Harvard University in 1940. After serving in World War II he began making films; the first to achieve notice was "Point of Order" a documentary/compilation concerning the McCarthy hearings of the early 1950's. He continued in that genre through "In the Year of the Pig.".
