ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

The Baker Institute for Animal Health is one of the oldest research centers dedicated to the study of veterinary infectious diseases, immunology, genetics, and reproduction.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Institute News and Views, 1987-1995, Veterinary Virus Research Institute Annual Reports, 1951-1975 and memos and correspondence.
Also, magnet, bookmark, brochure, and notecard, 2009.
Files relating to the history and accomplishments of the Baker Institute and to John M. Olin, including some photographs, 1950-1997.
