BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Hugh Pomeroy (1899-1961) served as Executive Director, American Society of Planning Officials, director of the Los Angeles County Regional Planning Commission, and director of the Westchester County Department of Planning and was a member of the International Fraternity of Lambda Alpha.
