DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Cornell University Department of Mathematics records, 1877-1976.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
14-19-2216
Abstract:
Bound term reports and student lists, 1890-1976, with some correspondence (lists were computer-generated after 1956); teaching schedules, 1910-1975; Mathematical Club records including minutes and papers presented, 3 volumes, 1891-1901; Mathematical Department Scrapbooks of examinations, 1877-1893; and Differential and Integral Calculus, by Virgil Snyder and John Irwin Hutchinson, with a syllabus and a list of other titles in the Modern Mathematical Series, including ones by other Cornell professors.
Creator:
Cornell University. Department of Mathematics
Snyder, Virgil, 1869-1950. (Title of work: Differential and integral calculus..)
Hutchinson, John Irwin, 1867-1935.
Mathematical Club (Cornell University)
Quanitities:
8.8 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Bound term reports and student lists, 1890-1976, with some correspondence (lists were computer-generated after 1956); teaching schedules, 1910-1975; Mathematical Club records including minutes and papers presented, 3 volumes, 1891-1901; Mathematical Department Scrapbooks of examinations, 1877-1893; and Differential and Integral Calculus, by Virgil Snyder and John Irwin Hutchinson, with a syllabus and a list of other titles in the Modern Mathematical Series, including ones by other Cornell professors.
