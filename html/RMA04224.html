ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Candid Camera was a practical joke reality television show produced by Allen Funt, who received a BA in Fine Arts from Cornell University in 1934.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Copies of films from the Candid Camera television series showing various practical jokes on the unsuspecting public. The films were used by the Psychology Department in class and for research to document human behavior. Included are a more than 200 episodes dated throughout 1961 and 1966. Professor James Maas used the films in his Psychology 101 class until his retirement in 2011. Several canisters did not contain the episodes they were labeled to, including Rude Waiter (10/27/1963), Disquis Throws, Surprise TV Commercial (3/15/1964), Kids With Box ( 10/21/62), Kids Make Up (2/4/62), Piano School, and California Dogs.
