COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Descriptive materials regarding wineries, vineyards, and associated businesses and organizations in the Eastern wine and grape industries. It's geographic scope will include all of the U.S. states east of the Rocky mountains. Currently the majority of the material covers New York State, particularly the Finger Lakes region. Types of materials include but are not limited to: news clippings, magazine articles, brochures, wine trail maps, tasting sheets, price lists and product catalogs.
Items are arranged in specific regions if possible. Documents containing information regarding a broader area over several regions may still contain region and even winery specific information. Undated folders will contain items with bulk dates from 1980s to 2000s. New York State winery items divided by region using in part the Uncork NY website www.newyorkwines.org for winery location information.
