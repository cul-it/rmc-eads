Content Description

Publications, articles, speeches, relating to Rob Young's career, city and regional planning, ecology and the environment.
Biographical / Historical

Assistant Professor at The University of Texas at Austin School of Architecture. Young graduated from Cornell University with the Class of 1982 with an AB studying Government and English. He received his Masters of Community and Regional Planning in 1995 and a PhD in Natural Resources in 2007.
