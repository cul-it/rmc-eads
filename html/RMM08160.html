Content Description

Sketches, notes, manuscripts, flyers, designs, photographs, publicity and marketing materials, and other ephemera documenting Bobbito's books, films, and other projects, featuring his work as DJ, filmmaker, author, and publisher, basketball player and coach, media figure. The materials also includes copies of his books, CDs, films, and video games, and a full run of the quarterly magazine Bounce: From the Playground (32 issues), of which he served as editor-in-chief starting in 2003.
Biographical / Historical

Robert Garcia, aka Bobbito, DJ Cucumber Slice, and Kool Bob Love, was born in New York City in 1966. He is an American DJ, radio host, author, film maker, and former professional basketball player in Puerto Rico.
Conditions Governing Use

Due to the fragility and potential degradation of moving image and sound recordings, viewing and listening is limited to items that have been digitized. If an item is in another media format, you may request to have the item digitized for access. Use of digitized audio visual materials is limited to on-site access only.

Scope and Contents
Magazines and booklets, chiefly on basketball, and many of which featuring articles by and about Garcia. Also included are 28 issues of Bounce Magazine: for the Playground, a quarterly magazine created by Garcia devoted to streetball, particuarly in New York City. In addition to serving as an editor, Garcia also served as writer and photographer for the magazine. While most items contain features on or written by Garcia, others served as references for his work.
