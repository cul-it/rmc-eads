Content Description

Original artwork, lyrics manuscripts, a photograph, 2 news clippings, contracts and agreements, and ephemera by or pertaining to Joeski Love.
Biographical / Historical

Joeski Love is a US Hip Hop artist from the Bronx best known for his platinum 1986 single "Pee Wee's Dance", which has been sampled numerous times. Love was originally part of a group signed by Vince Davis on his Elektra-owned Vintertainment imprint.
