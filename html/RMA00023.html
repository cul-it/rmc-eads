Scope and content

Correspondence and circular letters surrounding the search for a new Dean or Director for the College of Civil Engineering. Correspondents include Cornell University President Jacob Gould Schurman, Robert H. Thurston, Frank S. Washburn, R. H. Treman, Charles D. Marx, Walter C. Kerr, and many others.
