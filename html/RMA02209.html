COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Records of an interdepartmental research training seminar for the social sciences including proposals; reports; faculty and student notes; project data for an Elmira voting study, 1948; and interviews from a St. Joseph's Hospital, Elmira study, 1951. Also correspondence, research reports, and internal memoranda from the Digby, Nova Scotia, Stirling County Study conducted by Alexander Leighton and Garnet E. McCreary, 1952-1955

Scope and Contents
Series contains VHS tapes of programs dating between 1979-1998. Programs include anthropology lectures by visiting scholars, and documentaries on India, the Gurka'wuy region in Australia, and the activities of the Greenpeace organization
Series is open for research.
