Scope and content

Includes correspondence and general and subject files; Cornell Club of Buffalo material; 45th and 50th reunion material of the Cornell University Class of 1917, of which Johnston was secretary; printed material; cancelled checks and checkbooks; and other miscellaneous papers of Herbert Richard Johnston.
