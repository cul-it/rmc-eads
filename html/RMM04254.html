Series. Consult Dr. Brothers
Scope and Contents
Consult Dr. Brothers changed titles and is also referred to as "The Dr. Joyce Brothers Show" or "The Dr. Joyce Brothers Program" in the early years. It is unclear when exactly the name changed, but was clearly Consult Dr. Brothers in 1961. Documents that were not labelled with the show title were labelled as "Consult Dr. Brothers" for consistency. The show was picked up by NBC and later ABC (1961). It ran from 1959 to circa 1961 and preceded "Tell Me, Dr. Brothers". 65 episodes. In 1961, letters say "ready to shoot" 1-28, so maybe Dr. JB Show was pre-61, and Consult started with ABC in 1961? but referred to in publicity pamphlets in 1962 as "The Dr. Joyce Brothers show"
