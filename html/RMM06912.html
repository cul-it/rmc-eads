ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

The Prisoner Express program, sponsored by the Durland Alternatives Library at Cornell University, promotes rehabilitation by offering inmates information, education and the opportunity for creative self-expression in a public forum.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Art work and letters sent to the Prisoner Express program from people in prison in the United States, Prisoner Express newsletters and outreach materials, ads and material related to an exhibition of the art at Cornell in November 2006. The artwork includes colored pencil, crayon, and ink on paper, fabric, envelopes, and illustration board, plus origami.
