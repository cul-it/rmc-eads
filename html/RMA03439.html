Scope and content

Banners, pennants, pillows, runners, jackets, blazers, reunion items, graduation robes and regalia, stuffed animal mascots, and other cloth items. Examples include a Cornell University doctoral hood, originally owned by Dr. William J. Edens, Ph.D. 1937 and Class of 1941 blazer owned by Arthur Townsend Klinger.
