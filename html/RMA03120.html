DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Cornell University Basketball collection, 1934-2010
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
40-3-3120
Abstract:
Films, video cassettes, and video reels of Cornell basketball games. Includes games with Air Force, Bowling Green, Broome Tech, Brown, Bucknell, Canisius, Carlton, Cleveland State, Clemson, Colgate, Columbia, Dartmouth, Fordham, Harvard, Hofstra, Manhattanville, Niagara, the University of Northern Iowa, Northeastern, Northwestern, Notre Dame, Oklahoma, the University of Pennsylvania, Portugal, Princeton, Rensselaer Polytech, Robert Morris, Rochester, St. Bonaventure, St. Joseph's, Syracuse, Utica, Vermont, and Yale. Includes films of basketball tournaments.

Cornell University
Cornell University. Department of Athletics and Physical Education
Quanitities:
21 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Collection contains photographs, ephemera, programs, films, video cassettes, and video reels of Cornell basketball games. Includes games with Air Force, Bowling Green, Broome Tech, Brown, Bucknell, Canisius, Carlton, Cleveland State, Clemson, Colgate, Columbia, Dartmouth, Fordham, Harvard, Hofstra, Manhattanville, Niagara, the University of Northern Iowa, Northeastern, Northwestern, Notre Dame, Oklahoma, the University of Pennsylvania, Portugal, Princeton, Rensselaer Polytech, Robert Morris, Rochester, St. Bonaventure, St. Joseph's, Syracuse, Utica, Vermont, and Yale. Includes films of basketball tournaments. Ephemera includes sign created on March 26 by Olin Library Access Services employees, Michelle Hubbell, Debbie Cook, and Michelle Nair for the Welcome Rally for the basketball team, signed by the players. Sign created by Libe Cafe employees. Ticket stubs from the Carrier Dome game, with a letter from Ezra Cornell describing the event. NCAA 2010 Basketball Championships, East Regional, Program from the Carrier Dome game and special edition of the Daily Orange, 2010. Also, programs and tickets from the 2013-2014 season and a picture of the 1934 team.
