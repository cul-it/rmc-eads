ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Barbara Booker was an avid runner, camper, hiker, triathlete and cross country skier and the first woman president of the Finger Lakes Runners Club. Under her direction the club began to focus on open competition and women's running in road races and marathons.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes records pertaining to various track and cross-country skiing races and events, including meet results, workout schedules and memorabilia.
