COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Game with trivia cards and instructions in a cardboard box. Provides cards with information about gay men and lesbians in the categories of nightlife, history, style, entertainment, travel, and the arts, designed to be used as a supplement to any of the standard trivia board games or as a game by itself. Caption from the box top: "An adult game with thousands of questions to amuse, edify...titillate."
