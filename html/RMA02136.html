Abstract:
State budget records and other records relating to S.U.N.Y. the Commission on the Future of the Cornell Cooperative Extension Service with Robben Fleming as the chair, the Cornell Commission on Free Speech, and correspondence of James W. Spencer and M.C. Nesheim.
Creator:

COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

State budget records and other records relating to S.U.N.Y. the Commission on the Future of the Cornell Cooperative Extension Service with Robben Fleming as the chair, the Cornell Commission on Free Speech, and correspondence of James W. Spencer and M.C. Nesheim. Includes autobiographical data sheets compiled by Dr. Spencer reflecting on his life, achievements, and influences.
