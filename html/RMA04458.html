Content Description

Papers of Kathryn Jean Boor as the Ronald P. Lynch Dean of the College of Agriculture and Life Sciences at Cornell University. Boor served in this position from July 1, 2010 to September 30, 2020 before transitioning to the Dean of the Graduate School and Vice Provost for Graduate Education at Cornell University.
