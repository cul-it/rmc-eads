DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Galvin family papers, 1906-1987.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
4933
Abstract:
Correspondence, passports, photographs, certificates, awards, biographical clippings, and other papers of an upstate New York physician and college professor. Includes correspondence, accounts, historical descriptions, constitution and by-laws, and other papers pertaining to Cornell University's African-American fraternity, Alpha Phi Alpha.
Creator:
Galvin family.
Galvin, Gregory Alexander, 1907-.
Galvin, Corinne Brown, 1907-.
Quanitities:
.8 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Family members include Gregory Alexander Galvin, M.D., Meharry Medical College, and Corinne Brown Galvin, Ph.D., Ithaca College professor.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, passports, photographs, certificates, awards, biographical clippings, and other papers of an upstate New York physician and college professor. Includes correspondence, accounts, historical descriptions, constitution and by-laws, and other papers pertaining to Cornell University's African-American fraternity, Alpha Phi Alpha, including a paddle.
