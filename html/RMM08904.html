Content Description

The collection contains different models and types of e-book readers including the Amazon kindle, Kindle fire, Nook color Barnes & Noble (with leather cover), RCA eBook which inludes a folder of 2002 Gemstar reader newsletters.
Biographical / Historical

E-book readers are electronic devices that are designed primarily for the purpose of reading digital books and periodicals.
