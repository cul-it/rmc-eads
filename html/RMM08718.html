DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Motion picture stills collection, 1931-1956
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
8718
Abstract:
Approximately 316 commercial movie stills published between 1931 and 1956, from studios including Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer, Republic Pictures, Warner Brothers, RKO Radio Pictures, Puritan Pictures, Astro Pictures Corp. and 20th Century Fox.
Creator:
Cornell University. Herbert F. Johnson Museum of Art.
Quanitities:
.4 cubic feet.
Content Description

Approximately 316 commercial movie stills published between 1931 and 1956, from studios including Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer, Republic Pictures, Warner Brothers, RKO Radio Pictures, Puritan Pictures, Astro Pictures Corp. and 20th Century Fox.
