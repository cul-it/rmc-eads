Biographical / Historical

Milton Esman, was the John S. Knight Professor of International Studies. He joined the Dept. of Government in 1969, served as director of the Mario Einaudi Center for International Studies (1969-1983) and retired in 1989. He was a specialist in peace studies and ethnic studies and he helped draft Japan's post-war constitution.
