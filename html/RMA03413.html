ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Established in 1937, Ceres Circle provides women who either work at the NYS Geneva Experiment Station or are wives of Station employees, the opportunity to get acquainted and participate in formal programs that are planned at the meetings. The club is named after the goddess of agriculture.
