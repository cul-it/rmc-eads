COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Movie posters and other advertisements for films involving witches, witchcraft, possession, devil worship and similar subjects. The films are often of a "cult movie" nature from the United States and Europe with a few from Asia and Africa. Also includes other advertisements and mass market materials involving witches including two political T-shirts from 2010, one concerning the Delaware Tea Party (showing a witch stirring a cauldron-sized cup of tea) and one depicting Senate candidate Christine O'Donnell as a witch.
