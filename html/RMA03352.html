ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

The Biomedical Communications Department of the New York State College of Veterinary Medicine, Cornell University, was created in the 1970s. It was the photography department for the College, and the precursor to the Image Lab. It was dissolved in 1995.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Photographs, negatives and contact sheets maintained by the Biomedical Communications Department of the New York State College of Veterinary Medicine, Cornell University, from 1973 to 1996.
