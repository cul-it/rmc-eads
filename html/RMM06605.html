Abstract:
Two scrapbooks of programs, newclippings, etc., documenting Morris Erdheim's career in veterinary nutrition. There are also several loose programs and articles, and a small collection of photographs from Erdheim's education at the New York State Collegeof Veterinary Medicine at Cornell University.

Two scrapbooks of programs, newclippings, etc., documenting Morris Erdheim's career in veterinary nutrition. There are also several loose programs and articles, and a small collection of photographs from Erdheim's education at the New York State Collegeof Veterinary Medicine at Cornell University.
