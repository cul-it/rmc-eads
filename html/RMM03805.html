COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Pamphlet of Bernhard's memoirs, and a second pamphlet which is an addendum to his memoirs. Discusses his association with Shreve, Lamb and Harmon (later Shreve, Lamb and Harmon Associates) and includes the names R. Harold Shreve, Irvin Leslie Scott, Serge P. Petroff, Robert W. Jones, and Philip Will, Jr.
