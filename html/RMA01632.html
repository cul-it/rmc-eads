COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence concerning students, courses, graduate study, research and Sigma Xi matters; includes several letters from a former student written from the Women's Christian Medical College in Shanghai, China. Reprints of Kingsbury's articles. Also course outlines, lecture notes and several notebooks belonging to Professor Howard Adelmann; and a photograph of Kingsbury and others at Woods Hole, 1897. Correspondents include Cornell president Livingston Farrand, W.P. Van Wagenen, Simon Henry Gage, B.H. Grave, F.K. Richtmyer, Ross G. Harrison, Carl Zeiss Inc., K.M. Wiegand, and others.
