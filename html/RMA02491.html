DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
New York State College of Agriculture Department of Botany records, [ca. 1882-1963]
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
21-17-2491
Abstract:
Photographs, prints, and lantern slides; memorabilia about Botany Department field trips and social activities, and department minutes.
Creator:
New York State College of Agriculture. Department of Botany
Quanitities:
2 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Memorabilia, prints, scrapbooks, photographs and lantern slides of field trips, and meeting minutes related to the Department of Botany.
Photographs, including some of Barbara McClintock and Karl Wiegand; scrapbooks belonging to Professors Muenscher and Knudson; and diaries belonging to Professor Walter Muenscher.
