Content Description

Photographs taken for Fiske by E. Thorbergsson [i.e Eiríkur Þorbergsson] in June of 1902 in Grímsey, Iceland, of people and places. Includes one photograph taken by Anna M. Magnúsdóttir of Guðný Guðmundsdóttir in July of 1901.
