DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Jacob Herbert Bruckner papers, 1938-1968.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
21-31-1326
Abstract:
Includes material on animal and poultry societies.
Creator:
Bruckner, Jacob Herbert, 1905-1970.
Quanitities:
12 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

J. Herbert Bruckner was a professor of poultry science at the Cornell University College of Agriculture, and was chairman of the department from 1940-1965.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes material on animal and poultry societies; professional organizations; faculty, students, and various committees at Cornell University; movie film, film strips, and slides; patents; professional journals; and correspondence.
