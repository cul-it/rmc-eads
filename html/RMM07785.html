COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

A collection of clippings documenting anything related to gay people, mostly from Madison, Wisconsin newspapers for 1978-1979 and mostly from Chicago newspapers for 1980-1991. Plus photographs of gay softball teams and pride parades in Chicago in 1981 and correspondence to public officials and others about gay issues.
