ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

In 1991, the Development Office became Library Public Affairs.
Adrienne McNair, Director of Development for the Cornell Libraries, and Kappa Alpha Fraternity organized a project in 1980 to sell stuffed bears in order to endow a new book fund at Cornell.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Files on important donors to and benefactors of the library, 1957-1984; general office files, 1981-1987.
Includes a bear wearing a shirt printed with "I'm a Bear for Books, Cornell." Also, a one page description of the fund raising project.
