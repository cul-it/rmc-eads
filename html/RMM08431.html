ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Pro-Fac was a major fruit and vegetable processing cooperative founded and headquartered in Western New York. It was formed in 1961 and disbanded in 2012.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes correspondence, reports, meeting minutes, publications, audiotapes, slides, videotapes and photographs, documenting the history of the cooperative.
