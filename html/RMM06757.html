Ornithologist.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Files relating to Asian birds include species files and lists from Japan and India; files relating to bird banding, especially in Thailand; letters from people throughout Asia describing banded birds they recovered; files relating to the Migratory Animal Pathological Survey (MAPS); files on ectoparasites of birds and rodents in Asia; correspondence; photographs; diary; trip/conference reports; publications, particularly annual reports of the U.S. Army Medical Research Lab, the Armed Forces Institute of Pathology, and MAPS.
Spiral notebook, with a hand-written summary table of every species McClure himself banded over 50 years (74,092 individuals of 525 species).
