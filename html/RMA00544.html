COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes class rosters and grade books; student grade reports; lists of course registrations and fees; reports and results of experiments, mostly potato and small grain experiments, such as for control of disease; Fellowship Reports of the New York Florists' Club, the Nassau County Farm Bureau, and the American Rose Society; U.S. Department of Agriculture correspondence, mostly regarding agricultural agreements with South American countries; correspondence of agricultural colleges and agricultural experiment stations, including the Cornell College of Agriculture and Life Sciences and the New York State Agricultural Experiment Station; project proposals and budget estimates; agenda for meetings; photographs and scrapbook; and other records of the Cornell Department of Plant Pathology.
