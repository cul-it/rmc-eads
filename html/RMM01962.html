Box 8
Approximately 200 birthday cards, postcards, Christmas cards, 1900. Cornell and Rensselaer alumni circulars. Approximately 50 travel brochures. Three yearbooks from Oneonta High School - 1896, 1897, 1900.
Box 9
Personal effects - medical circulars, flower pressings, etc., etc. Religious miscellanea - includes assorted pamphlets and notes, a Spanish/English gospel of St. John (undated), 1930-1931 reports of Methodist Episcopal Missiions, Near East Foundation pamphlet, copy of "Colored Maps of Bible Land" (1898), 2 pamphlets titled "Joyous Living."
Box 10
Files containing extracts from Munsey's magazine on "The Stage" - 1893-1899 - containing current comments. Also press cuttings on following people, collected over last 30 years: William Seward, Herman Melville, Henry Pringle, Kenneth Roberts, Mark Twain, Richard Mansfield, FW Woolworth, Martin Van Buren, David Warfield, Frank Sullivan, Willard and Michael Straight; also misc. theatre clippings.
