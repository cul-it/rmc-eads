COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Letters and other papers from the files of Edgar Heermance, Henry W. Hicock, and A.B. Recknagel, officers of the Council (1942-58); papers pertaining to the dissolution of the Council (1958-59); pamphlets and miscellaneous material relating to Council activities and the forest products industry (1942-59); and incomplete runs of WOOD NOTES and THE COUNCIL BULLETIN.
RELATED MATERIALS

Related collection: Arthur Bernard Recknagel. Papers, #1891.
Related collection: Empire State Forest Products Association. Records, #1280.
