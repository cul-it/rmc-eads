Restrictions on Access

Access to Box 6 (contracts and business correspondence) is restricted until 2035.
Scope and content

Contains newspaper and magazine clippings, art work, hip-hop flyers, photographs, materials documenting the activities of Rock Steady Crew, books, catalogs, posters, awards, and other ephemera. The collection also includes video documentation of dance and related hip-hop events.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Preferred Citation

Crazy Legs Papers, #8489. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Biographical / Historical

Richie Colon (Crazy Legs) is the President of the Rock Steady Crew, an American breakdancing and hip-hop group.
Conditions Governing Use

Due to the fragility and potential degradation of moving image and sound recordings, viewing and listening is limited to items that have been digitized. If an item is in another media format, you may request to have the item digitized for access. Information on ordering access copies may be found on the Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections webpage.
