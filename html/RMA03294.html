INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Paul Hartman papers, #14-22-3294. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Includes correspondence, laboratory and course notebooks, talks, publications, and photographs relating to Hartman's teaching career and the histories he wrote on the Materials Science Center, the Physics Department, and Applied and Engineering Physics at Cornell University. Also, unpublished lecture notes on quantum mechanics by Josef M. Jauch and A. Nordsieck.
Scope and content

Includes a letter from Richard Feynman discussing interplanetary travel, December 5, 1945.
