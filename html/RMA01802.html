COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes constitution and bylaws, committee records, correspondence, minutes of the Board of Directors, and other records of the Statler Club, Cornell University. Also, records kept by Charles Sibley, President of the Statler Club, 1957-1958 and records kept by Jane Hardy, 1993-1998.
Includes bound dinner menus (1950-1953) and luncheon menus (1950-1956) from the Statler Club.
