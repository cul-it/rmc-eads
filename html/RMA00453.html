DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Frank A. Pearson papers, 1923-1965.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
21-10-453
Abstract:
Professional correspondence, manuscripts by Pearson and Warren; notes; speeches and publications; lecture notes on prices; research material on cream; statistics; notes and drafts and related correspondence on world hunger, the price of gold, and foodstuffs. Also, photographs and slides of Cornell and other scenes and subjects.
Creator:
Pearson, Frank A. (Frank Ashmore), 1887-
Quanitities:
28.4 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Professor of Prices and Statistics, New York State College of Agriculture.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Professional correspondence concerning prices, statistics, and economics; correspondence and notes on the career of George Frederick Warren; mss. by Pearson and Warren; notes on a trip to England and other subjects; speeches and publications; lecture notes on prices; research material on cream; statistics (30 v.); notes and drafts and related correspondence on world hunger, the price of gold, and foodstuffs. Also, photographs and slides of Cornell and other scenes and subjects.

Includes collection 21-10-814.
