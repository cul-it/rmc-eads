COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

The collection includes correspondence, meeting minutes, annual reports, newsletters and other printed matter, publications, photographs, and architectural plans reflecting the activities of the organization. Includes official Board business; Treasurers' Reports, Board meeting minutes, fund raising campaigns, public relations and advertising, financial allocations, the United Jewish Appeal, the Ithaca Jewish Welfare Fund and the Committee for the Resettlement of Jewish Refuges in Ithaca.
Access Restrictions:

Access restricted to the permission of the Chair or other officers of the Board.
Restrictions on Use:

Information that personally identifies an individual by name and/or address cannot be published in either print or electronic form.
