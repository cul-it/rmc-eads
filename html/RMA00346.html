INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Cornell University commencement programs and class printed materials, #37-8-346. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Memoranda to Trustees and faculty on Baccalaureate and Commencement services from the Committee on Commencement Arrangements, 1960, 1963.
Scope and content

Carbon copy of President Malott's letter, November 4, 1957, inviting Wilbour Eddy Saunders, President of Colgate-Rochester Divinity School, to conduct the Cornell Baccalaureate Service on June 15, 1958; also two pamphlets giving program and information concerning the Service and Commencement Exercises.
