Scope and content

Lectures in various topics related to biology and society, including the biology of man, man's diseases, agriculture, technology, economics, medicine, vision and perception, and computers.
Scope and content

Also, transcripts of taped lectures by Richard D. O'Brien on "Psychoactive Drugs," May 9, 1970; Robert S. Morison on "Experimentation on People," January 29, 1973; and Karen Arms on "Development of Sexuality," March 12, 1973.
