ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

The New York State Veterinary Medical Society Auxiliary is a women's organization affiliated with the New York State Veterinary Medical Society.

INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

The New York State Veterinary Medical Society Auxiliary records, #6600. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Scrapbooks and subject files kept by the New York State Veterinary Medical Society Auxiliary from 1948-2000. Also, files relating to annual meetings, awards sponsored by the Auxiliary, and State Fair displays.
Scope and content

Includes reports, correspondence and financial files.
