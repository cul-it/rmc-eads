ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Gavras was one of the four original Finger Lakes Runners Club members listed on the club charter. He was a competitive masters runner who competed on a Finger Lakes Runners Club masters team all over the United States and in Europe and continued to run into his 80's.
