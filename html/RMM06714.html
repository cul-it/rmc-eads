DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
New York State Wine Grape Growers records, 1965-1991.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
6714
Abstract:
Records document the activities of the organization. Other items include, member files, grape prices, news items, legislation that pertains to the wine and grape, correspondence with legislators, marketing, Vinefresh, annual meeting items, press releases, and newsletters. Also included are the files of Monty Stamp as secretary to the organization these have meeting minutes, financial information, copies of mailings from the organization.
Creator:
New York State Wine Grape Growers
Quanitities:
4 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

New York Wine Grape Growers mission is to benefit in any way possible members and the New York grape industry.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Records document the activities of the organization. Other items include, member files, grape prices, news items, legislation that pertains to the wine and grape, correspondence with legislators, marketing, Vinefresh, annual meeting items, press releases, and newsletters. Also included are the files of Monty Stamp as secretary to the organization these have meeting minutes, financial information, copies of mailings from the organization.
RELATED MATERIALS

See also collection #6695 Box 4 for some oversize items from NYWGG including advertising proofs for NY wine and grape campaigns.
See also Cornell Cooperative Extension Finger Lakes Grape Program records, #6773.
Forms part of the Eastern Wine and Grape Archive. (EWGA)
