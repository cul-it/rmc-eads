Biographical / Historical

Lithographs have been a conspicuous feature of South Asian visual culture from the 19th century on. They have served devotional, pedagogical, political, and decorative purposes in many or most Indian homes and offices. This collection of political posters centers on heroes of the Indian Independence movement.
