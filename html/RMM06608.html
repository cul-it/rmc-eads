BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Dr. Raymond J. Garbutt was the chief veterinarian of the American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals in New York City in the 1920s.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Writings, scrapbooks, photographs and lantern slides from Dr. Raymond J. Garbutt, DMV. The collection pertains to his his career as a small animal practitioner and his work at the American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals.
