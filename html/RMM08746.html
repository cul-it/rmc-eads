DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Shannon Engineering, Inc., records, 1970-2008
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:

Abstract:
Collection consists of engineering reports and drawings covering 44 different airplane types, 6 helicopter types, and 11 engine types. Includes documentation on hush kits for noise abatement of the Boeing 707 and others, critical structural modifications of the 747 for heavy loads, testing Sikorsky helicopters to accept an 11,000 pound external load and photographic studies taken in flight. In addition, reports touch on regulatory matters, and compliance with evolving requirements of the F.A.A. and its international peers. The collection encompases material during a transitional time period - analog technology to computer/electronic breakthroughs developed during the space program to the digital age and Internet.

Content Description

Collection consists of engineering reports and drawings covering 44 different airplane types, 6 helicopter types, and 11 engine types. Includes documentation on hush kits for noise abatement of the Boeing 707 and others, critical structural modifications of the 747 for heavy loads, testing Sikorsky helicopters to accept an 11,000 pound external load and photographic studies taken in flight. In addition, reports touch on regulatory matters, and compliance with evolving requirements of the F.A.A. and its international peers. The collection encompases material during a transitional time period - analog technology to computer/electronic breakthroughs developed during the space program to the digital age and Internet.
