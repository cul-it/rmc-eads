Content Description

Artwork and career documentation for Ken Feingold. Includes digital copies of Feingold's works in video and film, writings by Feingold and other theorists on digital and electronic art, as well as extensive documentation of exhibitions and numerous publications related to exhibitions.

Bibliography

Ken Feingold was born in 1952 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. He is a contemporary American digital and interactive electronic artist based in New York City. He attended Antioch College, Yellow Springs, Ohio from 1970-1971 and recieved both his BFA (1974) and MFA (1976) from California Institute of the Arts, School of Art; Valencia CA.
