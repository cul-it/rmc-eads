Quanitities:

Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Subject files concerning fertilizer, milk products, and advertising; financial reports; minutes; alphabetical files, many of Henry W. Jeffers III, vice president of the company, including appointment calendars, cash payments and receipts, payrolls, reports, photographs, and other records.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Walker-Gordon Laboratory Company Records, #3150. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Access Restrictions:

Access to material in Boxes 12, 27, 31, 35, 38, 44, 45, 47, 52, 55 relating to Henry W. Jeffers II and Henry W. Jeffers III restricted to the permission or their heirs.
