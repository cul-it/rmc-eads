BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Gordon E. White, Cornell University Class of 1955, was photo editor of the Cornell Daily Sun and the Cornellian.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Collection contains negatives, 4x5 cut film, taken at Cornell University for the Cornell Daily Sun, for the Cornellian and for personal interest; photographs and prints for the Cornell Daily Sun; Class of 1955 reunion documents; student ephemera; Cornell Daily Sun newspaper clippings; and publications.
