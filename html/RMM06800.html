DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Division of Community Education videotapes, 1950-1991 1950-1965
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
6800
Abstract:
Videotapes of films made by the Division of Community Education, many of them directed by Jack Delano. Some are documentaries on current developments in Puerto Rican performing and visual arts; others are evidently fictional.
Creator:
Puerto Rico. División de Educación de la Comunidad
Delano, Jack.
Quanitities:
42 videocassettes.
Language:
Collection material in English
ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

The División de Educación de la Comunidad (DIVEDCO) was created within the Dept. of Education of Puerto Rico in 1949.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Videotapes of films made by the Division of Community Education, many of them directed by Jack Delano. Some are documentaries on current developments in Puerto Rican performing and visual arts; others are evidently fictional.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Conditions Governing Use

Due to the fragility and potential degradation of moving image and sound recordings, viewing and listening is limited to items that have been digitized. If an item is in another media format, you may request to have the item digitized for access. Information on ordering access copies may be found on the Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections webpage. Audio visual material that has been digitized: V-1049
