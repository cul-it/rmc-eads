BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Boyd McDonald was a writer and anthologist who started "Straight to Hell: The Manhattan Review of Unnatural Acts" and compiled about a dozen subsequent anthologies based on true-life stories of gay men's sexual experiences. He was involved in the publication of Boston-based "The Guide: Gay travel, entertainment, politics, & sex" and "Alibi."
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Files relating to Boyd's and others' work with "The Guide: Gay travel, entertainment, politics, & sex," and "Alibi" including the photo archives and correspondence of "The Guide" and "Alibi," 1988-2003. Also contains correspondence with Richard Magary regarding "Straight to Hell."
NOTES

Collecting program: Human Sexuality Collection.
Related collection: The William Andriette Papers, #7725, include a video interview with Boyd McDonald.
