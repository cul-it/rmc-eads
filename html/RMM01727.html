COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, administrative papers, annual reports, records and logbooks, memoranda, statistical data, and a scrapbook of newspaper clippings pertaining to the operation of a tuberculosis sanatorium in Ithaca, New York.
Restrictions on Use:

Information obtained from these records may not be published or otherwise released in such form that any individual patient mentioned in the records can be identified.
