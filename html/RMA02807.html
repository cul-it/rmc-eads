ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Department was formed in 1985 upon the dividing of the Department of Design and Environmental Analysis into the Department of Textiles and Apparel and the Department of Interior Design, Planning and Management; Design and Environmental Analysis had evolved from the Department of Textiles and Clothing, which was formed in 1925.
The Textiles and Apparel Department changed its name to Fiber Science and Apparel Design in the spring semester, 2007.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Annual reports, faculty meeting minutes, department newsletters, committee minutes, and other papers of the department; includes a history of the evolution of the department. Also, material on course syllabi, outlines and descriptions of exhibits, featuring Mary McFadden, and Jon Haggins; audiocassettes of talks by Mary Purchase and Anita Racine.
