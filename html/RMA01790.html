DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Associate Dean of Students records, 1931-1978.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
37-1-1790
Abstract:
Files of Associate Dean of Students Ruth W. Darling including records of the Women's Judiciary Board and the Women's Self Government Association, Women's Student Government Association.
Creator:
Cornell University. Associate Dean of Students
Darling, Ruth W.
Cornell University. Women's Self Government Association
Cornell University. Women's Student Government Association
Quanitities:
8.9 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Files of Associate Dean of Students Ruth W. Darling include records of the Women's Judiciary Board, the Women's Self Government Association, Women's Student Government Association, staff meetings, scholarships and funds, student housing, fraternities, and other topics; includes memoranda, reports, senate actions, and registration forms for major campus events.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
