COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Travel brochures, city guides, maps, books of views, postcards, and related materials documenting world cities and tourism in the 19th and 20th centuries. The Montes collection also includes more than 1,000 books (most cataloged separately and searchable in the library catalog) on world cities and urban areas, the majority published between 1798 and 1960.
NOTES

Donated by Gregory E. Montes in memory of Dr. Gustavo E. and Marica S. Montes.
