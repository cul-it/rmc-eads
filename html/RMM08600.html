BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Dilip Purushottam Chitre was a poet and critic who published in Marathi and English in India's post-independence period, as well as a painter and film-maker.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Collection consists of literary papers, including typescripts, manuscripts, publications.
