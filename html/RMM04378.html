DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Helen Vandervort papers, 1922-1994.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
4378
Abstract:
Correspondence, financial records, reports, speeches, photographs, printed materials, certificates, and biography relating to Vandervort's work with the Tompkins County Public Library and the Finger Lakes Library System; the Tompkins County Senior Citizens' Council and senior citizens issues; the New York State College of Human Ecology; the New York State Fair; and women's rights issues. Also, letters from the Bull family (1922-26), interviews with Helen Vandervort by Eleanor Randles and Kathleen Babbitt.
Creator:
Vandervort, Helen, 1905-1994.
Randles, Eleanor Foster, 1920-
Babbitt, Kathleen
Quanitities:
7 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Extension education administrator.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Helen Vandervort papers, #4378. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Correspondence, financial records, reports, speeches, photographs, printed material, certificates, and biographical material relating to Helen Vandervort's work with the Tompkins County Public Library and the Finger Lakes Library System; the Tompkins County Senior Citizens' Council and senior citizens issues; the New York State College of Human Ecology; the New York State Fair; Alpha House in Trumansburg, New York and women's rights issues. Also, letters from the Bull family (1922-26), and interviews with Helen Vandervort by Eleanor Randles and Kathleen Babbitt.
