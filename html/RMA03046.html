ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Informal social group of New York State Veterinary College faculty and graduate student wives. The collection was assembled by Edith M. Poppensiek who was active in the organization during the tenure of G. C. Poppensiek as Dean of the College.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Minutes, membership lists, and publicity about the group, including typewritten and manuscript records kept by the group's secretary and treasurer. Also includes newsletters and other material relating to the Women's Auxiliary of the American Veterinary Medical Association, its junior and student chapters.
