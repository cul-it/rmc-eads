Title:

Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections

Abstract:
Files on graduate students and undergraduate advisees; general correspondence and subject files relating to entomology; research and conference notes and reports, files on insect control research and recommendations, and extension newsletters. Includes photographs of people and projects in Gyrisco's career.

BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Professor of entomology, Cornell University.
George Gordon Gyrisco, born 1920, received his Ph.D from Cornell University in 1947.
