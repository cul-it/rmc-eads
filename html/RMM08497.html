BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

American stage and recording artist, author, and publisher, the editor of the zine Rollerderby and member of the band Suckdog. Her work spans the worlds of publishing, art, transgressive sex, and gender politics and performance. In Roller Derby, Carver and her friends interview bands and other celebrities; the zine also includes comics, illustrations, games, and letters to the editor.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Periodicals, pasteups, proofs, ephemera, audiotapes, and a videotape, documenting Carver's work, life, and interests. Includes a complete run of Rollerderby (issues 1-25), as well as preliminary pasteups, proofs, artwork, and photographs for use in the zine. Also, ephemera and poster artwork for an issue of the zine Generation L, Suckdog and other projects; eight cassette tapes of music or performance sounds; and a VHS videotape containing a rough cut of the work Anatomically correct.
Lisa Carver's diaries, notebooks, additional photographs, correspondence including letters from Bill Callahan of Smog and Boyd Rice, material documenting the fanzine Dirt, and Lisa Carver's laptop from 2006.
