Content Description

The James Cunningham photographs (1968-1973) contains photographs, contact sheets, and negatives taken by James Cunningham during his time as a student at Cornell University. Photographs include candids of students, Cornell Athletics games, campus events, and campus scenes.
Biographical / Historical

James Cunningham graduated from Cornell University in 1971.
