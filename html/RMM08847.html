Content Description

Papers include manuscript materials relating to Alain Elkann's literary works, periodicals and periodical publications by Alain Elkann, correspondence and interview notebooks.
Biographical / Historical

Alain Elkann is an Italian novelist and journalist. His works focus on the history of the Jews in Italy, their centrality to Italian history, and the relation between the Jewish faith and other religions.
