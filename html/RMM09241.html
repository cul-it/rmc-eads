COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

This collection contains ephemera covering artists, both Native American and non-Native American, whose artwork often focused on Native American subjects. Brochures, auction and exhibition catalogs, correspondence, biographical information, programs from events, photographs, publications, slides, small books, some original sketches. The collection was split between Non-Indian and Indian artists.
