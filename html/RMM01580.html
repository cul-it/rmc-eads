DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Harry Clifford Reed papers, 1948-1952.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
1580
Abstract:
Correspondence, manuscripts of articles, genealogical notes, clippings, and photographs pertaining to Reed's secretaryship of the Hobasco Masonic Lodge in Ithaca, New York; correspondence related to the early history of Cornell University, and the city of Ithaca; also, genealogical material pertaining to the Stevenson and Dumont families.
Creator:
Reed, Harry Clifford, 1882-1953.
Quanitities:
.9 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Engineer.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, manuscripts of articles, genealogical notes, clippings, and photographs pertaining to Reed's secretaryship of the Hobasco Masonic Lodge in Ithaca, New York; other correspondence pertains to the early history of Cornell University, and the city of Ithaca; also, genealogical material pertaining to the Stevenson and Dumont families.
