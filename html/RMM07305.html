COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

The collection consists of material of a homoerotic nature assembled by Gordon Martin, and includes typescripts and photocopies of erotic/pornographic short prose fictions, a few of which are illustrated with drawings or photographs; catalogs and brochures of mail order enterprises dealing in erotica and pornography; and erotic and pornographic films.
