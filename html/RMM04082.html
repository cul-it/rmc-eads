DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Price family papers, 1881-1982.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
4082
Abstract:
Published and unpublished stories and articles by Edgar Price and genealogical material concerning the Price family. Correspondence from between family members. Typescript field notes by David Price on Nambiquara (Satones) Indian groups in Brazil. Notes, tape recordings, memos, published and unpublished reports used to prepare book about Nambicuara Indians of Brazil; and autobiographical writing by David Price.
Creator:
Price family.
Price, David, 1940-1999. (Title of work: Traditional and transformational analysis of Sherente kinship terminology..)
Quanitities:
15.5 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, diaries, travel albums and journals, accounts, financial and legal papers, post cards and photographs, published and unpublished stories and articles by Edgar Price, advertising copy and other writing, and genealogical material concerning the Price family, primarily Edgar Dayton Price, Paul D. Price, Arthur B. Price, Francis Smith Price (Mrs. Edgar D.), and Florence Price Beeson, and including letters and a silk color sample book from her dressmaking establishment in Shanghai, China.
Also, correspondence from Paul and Hazel Price to David Price, 1943-1982; correspondence, notes, notebooks, population data, and other material written by David Price in the 1950s and typescript field notes by David Price on Nambiquara (Satones) (Nambicuara) Indian groups in Brazil, 1967-1970. Notes, tape recordings, memos, and published and unpublished reports used to prepare his book about Nambiquara; and autobiographical writing by David Price,1998.
Also, Master's thesis that David Price wrote at the University of Chicago, "A Traditional and Transformational Analysis of Sherente Kinship Terminology," 1967, Bd. Ms. 1.
