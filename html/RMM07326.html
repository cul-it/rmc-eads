DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Family planning, birth control, and sexuality publications, circa 1934-2004.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
7326
Abstract:
Reports, conference proceedings, and books retired from Planned Parenthood's library; also audiovisual materials on contraception, menstruation, teenage pregnancy, world population, and abortion.
Creator:
Planned Parenthood of Tompkins County (N.Y.),
Quanitities:
10.2 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Reports, conference proceedings, and books retired from Planned Parenthood's library; also audiovisual materials on contraception, menstruation, teenage pregnancy, world population, and the 1993 New York abortion hearings; and some publications by Planned Parenthood of Tompkins County, including the 1996 Sexuality Education Center's Resource Guide and the 2000 pamphlet by Lauren R. Weinstein, "I Survived the Pap Smear and It Was Okay."
