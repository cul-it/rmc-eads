COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Brochures and maps describing vacation destinations for a gay clientele. Includes Montreal, Toronto, Puerto Rico, the U.S. Virgin Islands, Wisconsin, Chicago, Texas, and the areas in the Northeast United States, including Provincetown, Long Island, and Manhattan. Includes 1959 European travel magazines collected by the Mariposa Education and Research Foundation.
