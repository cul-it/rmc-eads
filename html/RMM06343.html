Scope and content

Photographs, cartes de visite, tintypes, portrait photographs, catalogs, clippings, fashion magazines and other visual material depicting period fashion styles and clothes. Includes a set of catalogs of clothing fashions from American and French retailers from 1874-1921, including Bedell, Jardinière, Columbia Book of Yarns, the Domestic, Gimbel Brothers, Macy's, McCall Patterns, and E. Butterick and Co. Also included is a small scrapbook of darning and mending samples, and a handpainted postcard.
