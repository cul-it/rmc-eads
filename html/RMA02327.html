DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
James J. Eyster papers, 1969-1998.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
28-2-2327
Abstract:
Course materials for courses; research reports; a student housing project study from the University of Pennsylvania; presentation and consulting notes for various hotels; research interview notes; publications; lecture notes and the Homeless Program Summary.
Creator:
Eyster, James J.
Quanitities:
7.7 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Professor of Hotel Administration, Cornell University.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Course materials for courses in Financial Analysis and Planning, Hospitality Financial Management, Integrated Case Studies in the Hospitality Industry, and Financial Accounting; research reports, primarily from the firm of Laventhol & Horwath; a student housing project study from the University of Pennsylvania; presentation and consulting notes for various hotels; research interview notes; publications; lecture notes and the Homeless Program Summary.
