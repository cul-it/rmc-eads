Scope and content

Collection contains the 1949 commencement program and reunion materials from 1989, 1999, 2009, and 2014, inlcuding lists of attendees, reunion booklets, and a DVD of oral histories recorded at the 2014 reunion from members associated with the class of 1949. Also includes the results of a 1959 class questionnaire.

Scope and Contents
DVD of oral histories recorded at reunion 2014 from members associated with the Class of 1949. The DVD features Martha Coler Risch '49, Vera Farrell '49, Bill Feinberg '49, Don Sutherland '49, Martha Ohaus '47, Jerry Alpern '49, and Carl Schwarzer '49. Topics include coming to Cornell, women at Cornell, World War Two and military service, dating, exams, and other campus life.
