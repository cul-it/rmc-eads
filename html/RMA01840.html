Title:
New York State College of Agriculture Administration records, 1896-2002.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
21-1-1840
Abstract:
Faculty personnel forms and work sheets of statistics drawn from the forms.
Creator:
New York State College of Agriculture
Quanitities:
5.3 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Faculty personnel forms (1927-1928) listing name, salary, educational background, previous teaching experience, previous experience at Cornell University, courses taught, hours per week for each course, and number of graduate and undergraduate students in each course; also, work sheets of statistics drawn from the forms.
Includes committee meeting minutes, including faculty meetings (1896-1978) and correspondence.
