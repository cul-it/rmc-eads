BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Professor of Development Sociology, Cornell University from 1987-2007.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes correspondence, course material, photographs, and publications concerning Lyson's work on community, civic engagement and problem-solving approaches to development, as well as local and global aspects of agriculture, food, nutrition, and health. Papers and examinations from his years as a doctoral student at Michigan State and files and a manuscript relating to academic job searches in the late 1970s.
