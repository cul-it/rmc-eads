INFORMATION FOR USERS
Preferred Citation

Worswick travel views and photographs collection, #8757. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Content Description

About 588 travel views from around the world, including North America and Europe, by many well-known early travel photographers such as Francis Frith, James Valentine, George Washington Wilson and Carlo Ponti.
Separated Materials

Two volumes of Erskine Beveridge's Wanderings with a Camera (1922) as well as 11 photograph albums featuring views of Scotland, France, Belgium, Wales, England and the Netherlands cataloged separately.
