BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Benmarl uses the slogan America's Oldest Vineyard. The winery is located in the Hudson Valley. Mark Miller and family took over the estate in 1957 naming it Benmarl. Ben is Gaelic for hill and marl describes soil. Miller helped lobby to pass winery legislation in New York. Benmarl holds N.Y. State Farm Winery Act license #1. Miller is also a noted wardrobe designer, illustrator and author.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Collection includes information about Benmarl, press kits, publication clippings, photographs other ephemera. Drafts of Mark Miller's book For the Love of Wine. Speeches given by Mark Miller about the wine industry and legislation. Videos and audio tapes regarding the winery. Information on the Benmarl Societe des Vignerons. Also some artwork by Miller. Farm Winery License FW1 and the pen used by Governor Hugh Carey to sign the act.
RELATED MATERIALS
