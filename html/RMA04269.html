ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

The Class Notes are a co-ed a cappella group founded in 1983 that performs a varied repertoire of contemporary Pop, R&B, Rock and Hip-hop songs.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Music, photos, CDs, videos, sheet music arrangements, membership lists, history and files relating to the administration and performances of the Class Notes.
