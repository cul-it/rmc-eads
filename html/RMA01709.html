Scope and content

Includes drafts, promotional material, author's permissions, and reviews for BIOLOGICAL FOUNDATIONS OF LANGUAGE; research material; unpublished drafts; course material, including some for a course on the brain and behavior; glass slides of brains; correspondence and other general files; tape recordings of lectures by Lenneberg and of speech samples and patients in Germany; and related papers of Eric H. Lenneberg.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Preferred Citation

Eric H. Lenneberg. Papers, #14-23-1709. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Biographical / Historical

Professor of psychology, neurobiology. Eric H. Lenneberg was a professor of psychology and neurobiology on the Cornell University faculty from 1968 until his death in 1975.
