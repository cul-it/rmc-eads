COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Memorabilia from the 1994 Gay Games, including registration packets, photographs, ephemera, T-shirts, periodicals and newspapers. This Collection also includes some memorabilia from the 25th anniversary of the Stonewall riots since both events took place in New York City in June, 1994.
