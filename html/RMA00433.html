Scope and content

Correspondence relating to the renovation of the bells, 1905-1907; brochures, articles, plans, photographs and other miscellany relating to the Chimes; also a broadside announcing an ice cream social marking the beginning of the 1998-1999 renovation; three photograph albums of the project; two cassette tapes of the tuning acceptance tests at the foundry of Meeks, Watson, and Company, Georgetown, Ohio, April, 1999; and program from the World Carillon Federation bulletin, 1999 recognizing the project. Also includes sheet music from Chimes concerts.
