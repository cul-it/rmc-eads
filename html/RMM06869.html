COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

The collection includes a field journal, two fair-copy journals, an annotated 1844 map of Iceland, several proof sections of Shepherd's The North-West Peninsula of Iceland (1867) and a copy of the published book. It also includes correspondence and newspaper clippings.
