COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Glass negatives of dairy barns, cheesemaking, and dairy science.
Includes "Food Science Facts" (1981-1984) , Food Science Club files, including its newsletter (1974-1993) , booklets listing Food Science Graduates (1990-1995) and other information pertaining to the Institute of Food Science.
Also faculty meeting minutes (1986-1990); Department Research Projects (1986, 1988), department brochures and other department files from the 1980s and a video "A Matter of Judgement: The Case for Food Safety in the Supermarket Deli."
