BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Emeritus professor in the Division of Nutritional Sciences.
Diva Sanjur received a B.S. in 1958 from the University of Puerto Rico. She earned a M.P.H. degree from the University of California-Berkeley in 1962 and a Ph.D. in nutrition at Cornell in 1968. Between periods of graduate study, she worked as a nutritionist in the Department of Health in Panama. Sanjur joined the Cornell faculty in 1968 and remained a member of the faculty until her retirement in December 1999. She also held appointments as an ad hoc professor at the University of Puerto Rico and at the Research Center for Food and Development in Hermosillo, Mexico.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes course material, correspondence, publications and proposals, relating to Diva Sanjur's career in the Division of Nutritional Sciences and her research on cultural factors in the Hispanic community that affect dietary preferences and the socio-economic variables associated with infection with intestinal parasites.
