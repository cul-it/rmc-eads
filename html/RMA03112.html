DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Big Red Band records, 1921-2009.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
37-6-3112
Abstract:
Material documenting the history of the Cornell University Big Red Marching Band, including print and audio visual items.
Creator:
Cornell University. Big Red Marching Band
Palumbo, Matthew.
Quanitities:
7.9 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Big Red Band miscellany, #37-6-3112. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

"Big Red Band Notes," newsletters; "Through the years with the Cornell University Big Red Marching Band," 1990; clippings; t-shirts, 1983, 2001-2003; and alumni visor hat. Also, movie films of band shows from the late 1940s through the late 1950s, and Big Red Marching Band CDs 1997, 2007, 2008. 16mm motion picture films are in color and black and white. Includes footage of the bannd at Niagara, Princeton, Columbia 1948; University of Pennsylvania, n.d.; Presidential Review, 20 May, 1954; Coumbia 1 November 1958; Penn. crew formation. Other reels are unidentified.
Scope and content

Includes files, photographs, scrapbooks, and recordings.
Scope and content

Also, a clarinet played by Matthew Palumbo; its bell cracked from the cold at a home football game in 1981.
