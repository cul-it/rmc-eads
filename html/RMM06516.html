COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Brochures, booklets, political broadsides and published material relating to Malaysia. Tourist brochures and pamphlets produced by the Tourist Development Corporation, Malaysia, Ministry of Culture and Tourism. Information brochure from the Inter Islamic University, Malaysia. Political broadsides, brochures, stickers and posters relating to the Malaysian election of 1999, including the Common Manifest of the Parti Islam Semalaysia, Partai Keadilan, Kertas Perintah and the Democratic Action Party.
This collection also contains programs, pamphlets, manifestos, buttons, stickers and flags from the political campaigns of 2004 and 2008. Campaign material is included from Barisan Nasional and its founding member UNMO, the Democratic Action Party (DAP), Islamic Party of Malaysia (PAS) and the collective Barisan Alternatif, consisting of Islamic Party of Malaysia (PAS), the Democratic Action Party (DAP), Malaysian People's Party (PRM), Parti Sosialis Malaysia (PSM), Malaysian Indian Congress (MIC), and People's Justice Party (PKR). It also includes a series of government issued booklets.
