DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
William Keithan papers, 1925-1990.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
28-1-4102
Abstract:
Includes photographs, printing blocks, and glass negatives (1930s) of the hotel school and campus, and files from when Keithan was dean.
Creator:
J. Keithan, William.
Quanitities:

BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Received his B.S. from Cornell's School of Hotel Administration in 1950 and later served as acting dean of the school.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes photographs, printing blocks, and glass negatives (1930s) of the hotel school and campus, and files from when Keithan was dean.
