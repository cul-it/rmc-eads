DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Richard L. Schlegel papers, [ca. 1960-1980]
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
7306
Abstract:
Legal files pertaining to SCHLEGEL VS U.S., 1961-1969; files relating to PACE! A DIGEST OF SEX AND HUMOR, 1970; subject files on sexual topics in general and homosexual topics in particular; and erotic photographs and other materials.
Creator:
Schlegel, Richard LaMar
Quanitities:
11.9 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Gay activist and editor and publisher of gay magazines. Born in Berrysburg, Pennsylvania, February 11, 1927 and died February 25, 2006.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Legal files, 1961-1969, pertaining to Schlegel vs U.S., a court case relating to Schlegel dismissal from U.S. Army employment and 1970 files relating to Pace! A Digest of Sex and Humor, for which Schlegel served as editor and co-publisher, including letters to the editor, business correspondence, and materials concerning the finances and advertising. Also, correspondence, erotic photographs and writing, and information on models collected for possible publication, some of which (when noted) came from John W. Emmerson (born April 23,1920, died April 18,1986) from Battle Creek, Michigan. Also clippings, a subject file, magazines, and books on sexual topics in general and homosexual topics in particular.
