DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Ruby Loper slide collection, 1950-1960
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
23-16-3265
Abstract:
Black and white glass lantern slides depicting prefabricated housing to room interiors and furniture were used by Loper for teaching and research. Some were made commercially and some taken by Cornell photographer Jon Troy.
Creator:
Loper, Ruby.
Troy, John P.
Cornell University. Department of Housing and Design
Quanitities:
.8 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Loper was New York state's first female extension architect and worked at Cornell from 1946-1967.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Black and white glass lantern slides depicting prefabricated housing to room interiors and furniture were used by Loper for teaching and research. Some were made commercially and some taken by Cornell photographer Jon Troy.
