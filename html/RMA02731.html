COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Laser-generated hologram paperweight designed by Simpson Linke meant to serve as a memento for individuals who have rendered distinguished service to the School of Electrical Engineering.
Includes meeting minutes and memoranda concerning the centennial in 1985, annual reports from 1980-1987 and faculty meeting minutes.
