COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Collection includes correspondence, preliminary sketches, and blueprints concerning the construction of the Women's Community Building in Ithaca, New York (1950s); general and committee correspondence; correspondence and survey materials pertaining to a proposed Ithaca War Memorial (1949); histories of the Federation; Constitution and bylaws; reports; minutes; financial reports; scrapbooks and yearbooks; clippings; program booklets; and other materials.
