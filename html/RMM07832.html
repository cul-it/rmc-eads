BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

The Mid-Hudson Valley Transgender Association (MHVTA) is a support group for transgender people in the Hudson Valley region of New York State and New Jersey. Meetings have been held in Poughkeepsie, New York and other cities.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Programs, photographs, books, serials, papers, conference materials, and videos documenting the Club's activities and recent literature on transgender themes, including intersexuality. Books and serials from their library are cataloged individually.
Access to Box 2, folder 27 and Box 3, folders 3 and 59 restricted until January 2041 (relocated to Accession File).
Conditions Governing Use

Box 5: No reproductions or images, no sharing of people's contact information. Material may not be shared online or published until Jan 2041. Researchers are asked to sign a form agreeing to abide by these restrictions.
