ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

The National Extension Association of Family and Consumer Sciences educates and recognizes extension professionals who improve the quality of life for individuals, families, and communities and provide education in food preparation, safety and nutrition, financial management, healthy lifestyles, home and work environment and safety, relationship and parenting skills.
Formerly the National Association of Extension Home Economists (NAEHE)
