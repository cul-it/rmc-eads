DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Cornell University Office of the Judicial Administrator records, 1871-2014.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
34-10-1666
Abstract:
Includes minutes of Judiciary Board meetings; correspondence; newspaper clippings; material concerning student protest, 1970; records of the Student Conduct Committee, 1931-1968; Student Code, 1871-1959; and other records of the Men's Judiciary Board, the Undergraduate Judiciary Board, and the Freshman Judiciary Board at Cornell University.
Creator:
Cornell University. Office of the Judicial Administrator
Quanitities:
4.5 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes minutes of Judiciary Board meetings; correspondence; newspaper clippings; material concerning student protest, 1970; records of the Student Conduct Committee, 1931-1968; Student Code, 1871-1959; and other records of the Men's Judiciary Board, the Undergraduate Judiciary Board, and the Freshman Judiciary Board at Cornell University.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Cornell University Office of the Judicial Administrator Records, #34-10-1666. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Access Restrictions:

Access restricted to permission of office of origin with the exception of Box 5 (annual reports).
Access to individual case files restricted to permission of student or 80 years after the creation of the record.
