DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Cornell University College of Arts and Sciences records, 1905-1993.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
14-2-767
Abstract:
Correspondence of Deans Robert M. Ogden, Cornelis Willem de Kiewiet, Leonard S. Cottrell, Paul M. O'Leary, and Francis E. Mineka with members of the faculty and administration; reports of department heads to the Deans; actions of the Board of Trustees; correspondence and reports dealing with faculty appointments, promotions, working conditions, departmental plans, curricula, research, budgets, the School of Education, the work of various committees of the College, the administration of the Dean's Office, and other matters.
Creator:
Cornell University. College of Arts and Sciences
Quanitities:
26 cubic feet.
8 videocassettes.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence of Deans Robert M. Ogden, Cornelis Willem de Kiewiet, Leonard S. Cottrell, Paul M. O'Leary, and Francis E. Mineka with members of the faculty and administration; reports of department heads to the Deans; actions of the Board of Trustees; correspondence and reports dealing with faculty appointments, promotions, working conditions, departmental plans, curricula, research, budgets, the School of Education, the work of various committees of the College, the administration of the Dean's Office, and other matters.
