BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Co-executive Director, National Gay Task Force. O'Leary served on the Continuation Committee and Structure Committee of International Women's Year.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

The Jean O'Leary Papers consist of correspondence, notes, minutes, and press releases pertaining to the International Women's Year and its national conference in Houston in November 1977, for the Gay Task Force, of which she was Co-executive Director. International Women's Year questionnaires are included, as well as lists of delegates. In addition, there are several relevant supplementary publications relating to the International Women's Year and its conference, and to women's and lesbian issues in general.
