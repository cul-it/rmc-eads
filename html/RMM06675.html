Creator:
Ithaca Sign Company
Quanitities:
4.4 cubic feet.
Scope and content

37 painted fabric banners featuring 32 different sign designs, produced by the Ithaca Sign Company for various stores, businesses, fairs, and political campaigns in cities such as Springfield; Utica, N. Y.; Manchester; Poughkeepsie, N. Y.; Syracuse, N. Y.; Rockville, Md; Millville; Altamont, N. Y.; Trenton; Muskogee; Ogden; New York City, N. Y.; Dallas, Texas; and Los Angeles, Calif.
