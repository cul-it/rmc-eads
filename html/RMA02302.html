Abstract:
Newspaper clippings, brochures, drawings for proposed buildings and gardens, journals and photographs. Oversized maps, plans and drawings. Also, videotape of the "The 150th Birthday Celebration of Beebe Lake," May 1, 1988.

COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Newspaper clippings, brochures, drawings for proposed buildings and gardens, and photographs. Also, videotape of the "The 150th Birthday Celebration of Beebe Lake," May 1, 1988.
Includes journals kept by gardeners from 1956-1999.
Now includes oversized maps and drawings of gardens and natural areas supervised by Cornell Plantations.
RELATED MATERIALS

See also the video A Journey Through Time and Nature, Cornell Plantations Path and Robert E. Jones Cornell Plantations papers, #34-4-4130.
