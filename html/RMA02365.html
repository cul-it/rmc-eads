ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

In 1925 the Department of Family Life was organized and in 1945 the name was changed to the Department of Child Development and Family Relationships. In 1969 the name changed to Human Development and Family Studies, and during the 1998-99 year it was shortened to Human Development.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Nursery School records, ca. 1920-1970; Nursery School administrative records, ca. 1920-1950; and Family Life class student reports, 1940s. Includes photographs of various activities in the Nursery School from 1920-1979, a scrapbook describing all aspects of the Nursery School in the 1930's, wooden puzzles produced by Milton Bradley that were presented yearly, and "Domecon" baby books from the 1920s. Also a film showing a nursery school, bathing cute baby; Library Tower; Students on quadrangle; alumni reunion; singing on steps of Goldwin Smith; feeding baby. about 1930, it is unclear if this film is from this collection and whether it shows the Cornell Nursery School.
RELATED MATERIALS

The films Practice Babies and Nursery School from this collection have been digitized and can be found on Cornell's Library Media Space website along with other Home Economics and related films.
