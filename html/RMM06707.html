ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

New York Wine & Grape Foundation (NYWGF) is a not-for-profit trade association of the New York wine and grape industries that is charged with promoting the products of the New York grape growing and wine producing industries and funding research relevant to these industries.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Records documenting the work of the Foundation including promotion, legislative work, funded research, and trade shows and conferences. Includes information on area wineries and the Uncork New York! campaign. Many items showing the involvements of President Jim Tresize. Also papers regarding other groups such as the New York State Wine Grape Growers and The New York Wine Council.
RELATED MATERIALS

See also collection #6695 Box 4 for oversize items including advertising proofs for NYWGF ad campaigns.
A brief history of the New York Wine & Grape Foundation was published in Wine East volume 22 number 3, September - October 1994, under the title "How a Foundation Helped Uncork New York" by Hudson Cattell. A copy is available in the accession folder.
