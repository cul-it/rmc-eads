DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Director of Research files, [1905]1935-1965.

Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
21-12-1074
Abstract:
Collection of correspondence, reports, research studies and committee reports for the Directors of Research, Department of Agriculture, primarily focused on the late 1950s to late 1960s with a small amount of documents from 1905 and the mid-1930s.
Creator:

8 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Collection of correspondence, reports, research studies and committee reports for the Directors of Research, Department of Agriculture, primarily focused on the late 1950s to late 1960s with a small amount of documents from 1905 and the mid-1930s.
