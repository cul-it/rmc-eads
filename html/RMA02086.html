Title:
Olin Library Circulation Department records, 1947-1985.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
13-5-2086
Abstract:
Records and material pertaining to Olin Library and various other libraries on the Cornell University campus.
Creator:
Cornell University. Libraries. John M. Olin Library. Circulation Department
Quanitities:
6 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes material pertaining to FAUL (Five Associated University Libraries), CULSA (Cornell University Libraries Staff Association), the Committee on Economic Status of Librarians, South Central Research Library Council, the Olin Library Reference and Technical Services Departments; automation of library functions, particularly circulation; reclassification of library materials; memoranda and reports of the Academic Assembly; procedures and policies of the Acquisitions Department; records of various libraries on campus; and memoranda, minutes, shelving tally sheets, personnel manuals, job descriptions, evaluations, tour information, monthly reports, minutes of department staff meetings, 1974-1984; and other records of the Olin Library Circulation Department.
