COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Files belonging to Gary Hallinen from various gay rights organizations including the Gay Liberation Front, GALS, and the Gay Alliance of the Genesee Valley. These include meeting minutes, correspondence, articles of incorporation, administrative files as well as posters and pamphlets.
