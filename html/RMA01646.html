24-1-1646
Abstract:
Includes records of George C. Poppensiek as Dean of the New York State College of Veterinary Medicine.
Creator:
New York State College of Veterinary Medicine. Office of the Dean
Poppensiek, George C.
Quanitities:
12 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes State University of New York correspondence, minutes of administrative staff, and other materials; records of the Cornell Faculty Senate; material on physical biology, 1957-1964; records of the Veterinary College Council; material on new Veterinary College buildings; budget requests and related records; date books and calendars; records of veterinarians' conferences; Association of American Veterinary Medicine Colleges material; American Veterinary Medical Association correspondence; traditional Christmas letters from the Dean to alumni, 1959-1973; and other records of George C. Poppensiek as Dean of the New York State College of Veterinary Medicine.
