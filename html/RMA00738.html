COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Records of the Class of 1937 Women's Secretary, Carol Cline, include correspondence, programs, pamphlets, photographs, memoranda, and memorabilia pertaining to reunions; questionnaires and other biographical information for each reunion; a guide for class secretaries, clippings, and other printed material; "Class of 1937 Women, History Since Graduation, 1937-1990," and tape recordings of the Women's Reunion Banquet and Midnight Dormitory Party (1962), Introduction to 1937 Banquet, and the 45th Reunion Banquet at the Johnson Art Museum. Also, questionnaires about the class, 1972-1991. Class of 1937 class and reunion records; and reunion memorabilia, particularly "Flip the Frog" class mascot items.
Also contains files of Merle Ohlinger, including correspondence with Carol Cline. Reunion photographs (1947, 1957, 1962) and parody issue "Naval Day Nudes, May 23 1936."
