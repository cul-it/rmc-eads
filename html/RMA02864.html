Biographical / Historical

From its founding to ca. 1969 the dept. was called Household Economics and Management. From ca. 1969 to ca. 1977 it was known as the Dept. of Consumer Economics and Public Policy. After 1977 it changed to the Dept. of Consumer Economics and Housing and in 1998 it merged with the Dept. of Human Service Studies to become the Dept. of Policy Analysis and Management.
