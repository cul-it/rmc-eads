INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Cornell University Glee Club records, #37-6-2399. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Collection includes bound managerial papers, correspondence, and financial papers for every year from 1929 to 1976 (excluding war years), scrapbooks, photographs, programs, notebooks, recordings, songbooks, and miscellany concerning the Cornell University Glee Club (CUGC), its managers, directors, members, and alumni. Also included are materials relating to the Cornell Glee, Banjo, and Mandolin Clubs (predecessor to the Glee Club), the Cornell Musical Clubs Council (CMCC) (the Glee Club's governing body for many years), the Hangovers (a cappella subset), and the Department of Music. Also included are some papers of the Cornell Chorus, or Cornell Women's Glee Club (CWGC).
Scope and content

Also, scrapbooks (1954-55); USSR/England tour (1960-61); publicity and managerial records (1951-65); and an LP album, "Cornell music," consisting of music of the Glee Club, the Cornell chimes, and the Cornell Concert Band. Also microfilmed minutes and a poster for a Glee Club concert in the Philippines.
