Scope and content

The Robert Henry Thurston papers include correspondence, reprints, a photograph album from Brown University (Class of 1859) and diplomas, and one bound volume of some of Thurston's publications from 1878-1891, mainly reprints of journal articles. The correspondence concerning experiments, inventions, engineering and scientific problems, scientific machinery and instruments includes letters from Charles Kendall Adams, Edward Atkinson, Alexander Graham Bell, Alonzo Cornell, George William Curtis, Chauncey Depew, Thomas A. Edison, John Ericsson, Daniel Coit Gilman, Abram Hewitt, Edwin J. Houston, B.F. Isherwood, Lord Kelvin, Samuel P. Langley, Seth Low, Cyrus McCormick, Charles Porter and Nikola Tesla.
