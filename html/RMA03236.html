Scope and content

Includes master tapes, phonograph records, and compact disks of various Cornell a cappella groups, including Cayuga's Waiters, Touchtones, Class Notes, Nothing But Treble and Hangovers.
Scope and content

Also 5 digital audio tapes: commencements 1993, 1994; Cornell state of the university, 1993; and Cornell address by Dick Cheney.
