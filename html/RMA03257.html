BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Professor of Natural Resources, Cornell University.
Harlan Brumsted received a B.A. from Dartmouth College and an M.S. and Ph.D from Cornell University. He began teaching at Cornell in 1954, with responsibility for Extension programs in wildlife management, aquatic management, natural resources conservation, and outdoor recreation.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes subject files, correspondence, course materials, publications, slides, and tape recordings pertaining to Brumsted's career in the Dept. of Natural Resources.
