Title:

Abstract:
Includes correspondence concerning the graduate program of the New York State Agricultural Experiment Station in Geneva, 1940-1983; selection of a head for the Entomology Department at Cornell University, 1965; and Rachel Carson's SILENT SPRING, 1962-1963. Also, research material concerning SILENT SPRING.

INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Paul Jones Chapman papers, #22-1-2102. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Includes correspondence concerning the graduate program of the New York State Agricultural Experiment Station in Geneva, 1940-1983; selection of a head for the Entomology Department at Cornell University, 1965; and Rachel Carson's SILENT SPRING, 1962-1963. Also, research material concerning SILENT SPRING, the experiment stations, grant files, annual review articles, and photographs.
