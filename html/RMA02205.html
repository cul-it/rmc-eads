COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Films and videos of Cornell University football games.
Also, "The Big Red Machine: Exciting play-by-play highlights of Cornell's 1971 Ivy League Championship season narrated by Jay Levine."
Digitization of films can be arranged if requested.
RELATED MATERIALS

Some films labeled "highlights" may refer to a single game.
University of Pennsylvania Archives has film of all Penn-Cornell games.
See also Cornell University Football Game Highlights, #40-2-1000, for some 1950 and 1951 reels. Also Hunter Ripley Rawlings Papers, #3-13-2843, for 1991 highlights.
See also Howard Schuck papers, #37-5-2629, for more information on the team, particularly on the Ohio game of 1939.
See also Cornell University Museum Items, #47-7-1948.
See also Big Red Band Records, #37-6-3112.
See also Football Miscellany, #37-5-3981.
Ernest William Schoder papers, #16-4-1156 for some 1929-1930 games.

Scope and Contents
Note that most films were used for practice, not broadcast. Some films were recorded by position following this pattern: reels labeled 1 = Offense, 2 = Defense, 3 = Special Teams. Other times reel numbers indicate quarters and show all plays.
