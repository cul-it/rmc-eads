ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

1946 the Department of Home Economics Education was established in the College of Home Economics, today's College of Human Ecology. In 1969 the name was changed to Community Service Education and in 1978 the name was changed again to Human Service Studies. In 1997 the department merged with the Department of Consumer Economics and Housing to become the Department of Policy Analysis and Management.

Five Year Review material and Curriculum Committee minutes; also Department memos and correspondence, faculty meeting minutes, manuals, reports, and budget material.
