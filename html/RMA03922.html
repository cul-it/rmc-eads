BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

The President's Council of Cornell Women (PCCW) was founded in 1990 by former President Frank Rhodes. It is a group of alumnae working to enhance the involvement of women students, faculty, staff, and alumnae as leaders within Cornell University and its many communities.

Scope and content

PCCW Fall meeting material, 2014; PCCW Annual Meeting At-A-Glance program, 2014; 25th Anniversary Annual Meeting 2015 meeting packet, infographic poster signed by meeting attendees, and letter of congratulations from President David J. Skorton.
