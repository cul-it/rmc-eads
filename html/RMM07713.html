Scope and content

Videorecorded productions relating to human sexuality, including commercial productions, documentaries, feature films, erotica, safe sex tapes, and comedy; products of the Mariposa Education and Research Foundation, the Gay Men's Health Crisis, and the International Foundation for Gender Education; videos from the "Advocate Men Live!" series; a video produced by the American Family Association, titled "Suffer the children: answering the homosexual agenda in public schools"; and a fundraising video produced by the Lesbian Herstory Archives.
Also, some covers, without the videotapes.
Conditions Governing Use

Due to the fragility and potential degradation of moving image and sound recordings, viewing and listening is limited to items that have been digitized. If an item is in another media format, you may request to have the item digitized for access. Information on ordering access copies may be found on the Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections webpage. Audio visual material that has been digitized: V-653
