Cornell and Ithaca concert and event programs, #37-7-3854. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Includes general concerts at Ithaca High School and Cornell University, 1943-1971; Bailey Hall and Statler concert series, 1907-1986; Sage Chapel Choir, 1945-1972; miscellaneous events at Cornell and Ithaca College, 1926-1989, including theater, Gilbert and Sullivan operettas, dance, and ice skating; community events, including the Civic Opera and Ithaca High School concerts, 1946-1966; and Ithaca Community Players, 1925-1959 and 1978-1990. Also "Concerts at Cornell," 1933-1934 and 1934-1935. Programs from Wilgus Opera House for the Ithaca Quartet, 1886 and Cornell Banjo and Guitar Club, 1890. Program for Festivals on Ice presented by the Cornell Figure Skating Club, March 1962.
