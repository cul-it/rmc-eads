ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Mortar Board is a national senior honor society for women.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Mortar Board Honor Society records, #37-4-2199. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Early records of the organization. Includes a copy of a letter from Caroline H. Swartout to Miss Osborn about the society and a typescript four-page history, 1938. Also a minute book, 1919-1930. Letter and tape recorded reminiscences of Lulu S. Howard and letter from Lois Osborn, both to Marjorie E. Lasko of Ohio State University for a Mortar Board history project, 1984.
Scope and content

Recent records of the organization include publicity for lectures and other events, project evaluation forms, planning calendars, and Executive Board agendas and meeting minutes.
Scope and content

Also: record of activities, yearly report, Cornell Sun articles, minutes, agendas, announcements.
