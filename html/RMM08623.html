Scope and content

The collection consists of documents accumulated while Harrisson worked as Curator of the Sarawak Museum, including maps, photographs, notebooks and papers/manuscripts by Harrisson and research assistants.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Preferred Citation

Tom Harrisson Central Borneo Documents, #8623. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Biographical / Historical

Tom Harrisson was a British polymath. In the course of his life he was an ornithologist, explorer, journalist, broadcaster, soldier, guerrilla, ethnologist, museum curator, archaeologist, documentarian, film-maker, conservationist and writer. After serving in WWII, he became the Curator of the Sarawak Museum from 1947-1966.
