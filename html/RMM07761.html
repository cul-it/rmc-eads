COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Posters promoting X-rated movies. Includes a number of films directed by Henri Pachard, Harold Lime, and Leon Gucci; and films starring Joanna Storm, Ron Jeremy, Candida Royalle, Annie Sprinkle, and Eric Edwards, among many others. Also, pressbooks with ordering information for certain movie posters.
