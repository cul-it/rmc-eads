Content Description

Collection contains research, correspondence, and teaching materials relating to the professional career of Bernd Lambert. A number of the materials focus on his work in Kiribati.
Biographical / Historical

Bernd Lambert was a professor of anthropology emeritus and a faculty member in Cornell University's College of Arts and Sciences. His studies included kinship among the Pacific islanders of the Republic of Kiribas.
