Scope and content

The John Clair Miller Image Collection of Twentieth-Century Architecture in Iceland contains photographic images made by Professor Miller of more than a dozen architectural installations in Reykjavík and in other locations in Iceland (also some in Finland). Architectural projects include private residences, lighthouses, places of worship, and government buildings.
