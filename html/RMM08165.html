Content Description

Correspondence from musician and artist Eric Couch of the Portland, OR, punk Hardcore band Lockjaw to Greg Wise of the Ohio punk zine Scalp. The mail contains letters, artwork, Lockjaw flyers and photographs, scene reports and band news.
