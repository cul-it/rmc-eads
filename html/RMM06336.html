COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Reports and studies deriving from Nixon's work as a consulting economist with particular reference to the Hong Kong Airport, Cairo water studies, and other projects. Also includes early files on New York State economy and politics, 1950s; "Research Memoranda" from the Committee for Economic Development, Area Development Division by Nixon and others, 1960s; works on real estate indexation, 1990s; and speeches and articles.
