COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes scrapbooks regarding Chi Phi activities; Craigielea Memorial Education Fund, Inc. correspondence, 1947-1979, and file book, 1983-1985; material on the chapter house; Chapter House Association file books, 1979-1987; checkbooks; pictures of Chi Phi plaques; published history CHRONICLES OF CHI PHI, by Dr. Theodore B. Appel, 1939, and revised version, 1993; and other records of Chi Phi, Xi Chapter at Cornell University. Also renovation plans for the chapter house by William Henry Miller and Arthur N. Gibb, ca. 1895.
Biographical / Historical

The Xi chapter of Chi Phi was founded at Cornell University in 1868. The fraternity house is known as Craigielea, built for the fraternity in 1890 and designed by William H. Miller '72.
