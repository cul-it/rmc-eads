DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Cornell University Press and Comstock Publishing Company records, [ca. 1880-1935]
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
35-6-257
Abstract:
Correspondence, manuscripts, proofs, and illustrative material pertaining to books published by the Cornell University Press and the Comstock Publishing Company; correspondence pertaining to a merger of the Nature Study Review and Nature Magazine; and correspondence pertaining to John W. ("Uncle John") Spencer's stories and letters for children on nature study and rural life; correspondents include Anna Botsford Comstock, Mary Eleanor Davis, Paul S. Galtsoff, Albert H. Hartzell, Frank E. Lutz, James G. Needham, Arthur Newton Pack, Cuthbert W. Pound, Percival S. Ridsdale, John W. Spencer, and Paul S. Welch.
Creator:
Cornell University Press
Fuertes, Louis Agassiz, 1874-1927.
Quanitities:
12 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
