COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, publications, newspaper clippings, scrapbooks and related records pertaining to the Housing and Feeding the Homeless Program that existed in the School of Hotel Administration at Cornell.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Housing and Feeding the Homeless Program, #28-1-3161. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Biographical / Historical

The Housing and Feeding the Homeless Program was established in July 1987 and consisted of three components: the Housing and Feeding the Homeless Course; the Industry Linkage Program; and the Research, Resource, and Advocacy Center.
