Scope and Contents
State files contain various materials on redistricting in the state in general, as well as some correspondence and other material on D.W. involvements in the state. Note, for example, Colorado, where League of Women Voters successfully campaigned to get proposal drafted in part by D.W. onto the ballot through initiative process. Proposal passed (1974) and was incorporated into state constitution.
