COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes correspondence, publications, research material on regional modeling, regional science, economic development and public policy analysis in the United States and abroad, and course material on quantitative and modeling, statistics, econometrics, input-output and social accounting matrix models, regional science and quantitative public policy analysis.
