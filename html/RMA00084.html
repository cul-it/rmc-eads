DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Liberty Hyde Bailey, New York State College of Agriculture and Agricultural Experiment Station records, 1903-1914.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
21-2-84
Abstract:
Director's files including correspondence, committee reports, and files concerning construction projects and the general administration of the college.
Creator:
Bailey, L. H. (Liberty Hyde), 1858-1954.
Quanitities:
48.7 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Director's files including correspondence, committee reports, and files concerning construction projects and the general administration of the college.
Also, signatures from students in appreciation to Liberty Hyde Bailey, 1908.
RELATED MATERIAL

See also Liberty Hyde Bailey Papers, #21-2-3342 and Liberty Hyde Bailey Forestry Papers, #21-2-576.
