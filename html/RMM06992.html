COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Letters, stock certificates, drawings, photographs, and publications related to Simon Lake's designs for submarines.
SEPARATED MATERIAL

Books on Simon Lake and the Submarine.
  1. Underwater Torpedo Boats: Their Merits and Their Menace, Lake Torpedo Boat Co. Includes content on "The Protector" sold to Russia. Rudin # SL-43.
  2. Ancient and Modern Ships: Part 1, George C.V. Holmes, 1906. Signed by Lake. Rudin # SL-44.
  3. Ancient and Modern Ships: Part II, George C.V. Holmes, 1906. Rudin # SL-45.
  4. The Storage Battery, Augustus Treadwell Jr., 1902. Rudin # SL-46.
  5. Sixty Years' Recollections of Milford, Nathan Stowe, 1917. Signed by Lake. Rudin # SL-47.
  6. A Genealogy of the Lake Family and Submarine. Rudin # SL-48.
  7. The Autobiography of Simon Lake. Rudin # SL-49.
