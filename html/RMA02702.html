ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

The Mu Chapter of Omicron Nu, a national home economics honor society, was founded at Cornell in 1919. Dr. Georgia L. White of the Michigan Agricultural College acted as the installing officer. The aims of the organization were to promote scholarship and to further home economics education at Cornell. The chapter also provided a scholarship to the freshman woman in Home Economics with the highest scholastic standing. In 1990, Omicron Nu merged with Kappa Omicron Phi to form Kappa Omicron Nu Honor Society.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes membership records and membership book; information about scholarships; minutes of meetings, 1920-1989; secretary's book, 1936-1942; records of events; presidents' annual reports, 1940s-1960s; scrapbook about the founding of the chapter, 1919; photographs; and programs, and the original charter from 1919.
