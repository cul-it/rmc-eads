Title:
Cornell University Women's Studies Program records, 1968-1997.

Records of department faculty and speakers; course lists; seminars; annual reports and other material.

ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Women's Studies was renamed Feminist, Gender & Sexuality Studies (FGSS) in 2002.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Records of department faculty and speakers; course lists; seminars; annual reports; Advisory Group and other committee meetings; correspondence; yearly budgets; material concerning visiting scholars; and other records of the Women's Studies Program at Cornell University.
