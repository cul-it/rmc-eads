Content Description

Approximately 342 show flyers for Hardcore punk music events in Washington DC, 1981 - 1991. Bands featured include Minor Threat, Suicidal Tendencies, Fugazi, Scream, Iron Cross, Government Issue, Trouble Funk, Big Boys, Black Market Baby, The Faith, No Labels, Void, Black Flag, Circle Jerks, Marginal Man, Velvet Monkeys, Braille Party, and many more. Venues include DC Space, 9:30 Club, Space II Arcade, All Souls Church, Wilson Center, along with local schools and rec centers.
