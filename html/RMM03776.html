Interviews and reminiscences with senior citizens; also, organizational and administrative material associated with the project.
Access Restrictions:

Access to tape 693 side two restricted to the permission of Lakeside Nursing Home employees

Conditions Governing Use

Due to the fragility and potential degradation of moving image and sound recordings, viewing and listening is limited to items that have been digitized. If an item is in another media format, you may request to have the item digitized for access. Information on ordering access copies may be found on the Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections webpage. Audio visual material that has been digitized: TR-701, TR-711
