COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Departmental files include material concerning course offerings and conferences; fellowships, grants and funding sources; department and director's correspondence; and records of the European Studies Program. Also, correspondence files; files of Prof. Richard N. Rosecrance; and accounts files.
Records of of the Air War in Indochina Study Group, conducted by Raphael Littauer and Norman Uphoff, which led to the publication of The Air War in Indochina, with an introduction by Neil Sheehan, 1972

Biographical / Historical

In 1961 professor Mario Einaudi led an effort to establish the Cornell Center for International Studies, whose goal was to support and coordinate the university's efforts in all facets of international research and learning. In 1990 the center changed its name to the Mario Einaudi Center for International Studies.
