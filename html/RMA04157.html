BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Kenneth A.R. Kennedy was a physical anthropologist who specilized in forensic anthropology and human skeletal biology in South Asia. He studied at the University of California, Berkeley and was Professor of Ecology and Evolutionary Biology, Anthropology, and Asian Studies at Cornell University.
