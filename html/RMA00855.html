DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Dean of Students Office records, 1951-2007.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
37-1-1570
Abstract:
Administrative and organization records; files of the Student Finance Commission including minutes, Selection Committee information, information on student organizations, financial statements, Student Activities Groups accounts, Travel Committee requests, Student Government reports, and time cards. Also records of the Graduate Coordinating Commission. Also, files on the Small Living Unit Council, head residents of dormitories (including head residents' manuals), the Intersession Program on Women (1968-1969), the Housing and Dining Commission, and housing issues on campus.
Creator:
Cornell University. Office of the Dean of Students
Quanitities:
19.9 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Administrative and organization records; records of the Graduate Coordinating Commission; and files of the Student Finance Commission including minutes, Selection Committee information, information on student organizations, financial statements, Student Activities Groups accounts, Travel Committee requests, Student Government reports, and time cards. Also, files on the Small Living Unit Council, head residents of dormitories (including head residents' manuals), the Intersession Program on Women (1968-1969), the Housing and Dining Commission, and housing issues on campus.
