BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Member of the Class of 1994. Member of the Cornell Fencing Team, 1990-1994; Captain, 1992-1994; Assistant Coach, 1994-1995. One of nine female athletes to bring a Title IX lawsuit against Cornell in 1993 after the Women's Fencing and Gymnastics teams were cut. The teams were reinstated in December 1993.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Team schedules, brochures, captain?s letters, newspaper clippings, programs, and ephemera from the Cornell Varsity Fencing team from 1990-1997. Includes press releases, correspondence, and newspaper clippings about the Title IX lawsuit brought against Cornell University by members of the Women?s Fencing and Gymnastics teams.
