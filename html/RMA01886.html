Box 1
Life of John West Jackson
 1943
Scope and Contents
REMOVED now part of World War II servicemen alumni files, #41-2-822. Bound volume titled Life of John West Jackson 1917-1942 by his mother Isabel Morrison West Jackson at 14 Cooper Parkway Pittsfield Massachusetts has been removed and added to Jackson's deceased alumni folder. Jackson was a member of the Cornell University Class of 1942 and was commissioned as a pilot. He was killed in an automobile accident July 15, 1942. The volume includes some documentation of him spending time at Heller House. The volume, written to Isabel's grandchildren, John's children, documents John's life and tells family history. It includes family photos and also photos of flying a glider and Jackson with a plane. There is mention of his time at Cornell and riding a motorcycle. Also some poetry including a hand written copy of High Flight by John G. Magee Jr..
