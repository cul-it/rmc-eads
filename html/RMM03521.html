BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

W. R.Kellough became a member of the Ely Chapter of Lambda Alpha International in 1965, one of three of the first members from outside the continental United States. In 1967, he formed the first International chapter in Toronto Canada, the Simcoe Chapter, and was its first president. He was editor of the Land Economics Journal for a number of years and was International President for 1982-1983. He heads W.R. Kellough & Associates (Canada) Inc., appraisers and business and real estate economists.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Reports, lectures and speeches, articles, pamphlets and books concerning city and regional planning, real estate valuation, and other aspects of real estate development and land use; includes material concerning several areas of Ontario, Canada. Also includes miscellaneous papers and correspondence of Lambda Alpha, 1971-1982 (3 v.); correspondence and articles for preparation of the Land Economics Journal (2 v.); minutes and correspondence of the Simcoe chapter (1 v.); correspondence and data on the Comparative Study of the U.S. and Canadian Cities (1 v.); curricula vitae of new members and correspondence relating to the formation of the St. Louis, Phoenix, and Oklahoma Chapters; correspondence and other material of the International President, 1982-1983, and one volume of committee records for that period; and Lambda Alpha International Donors and Archives Content Cornell University Archives, a guide to the collections, compiled by Kellough.
