BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Marjorie Grene (December 13, 1910 - March 16, 2009) taught at the University of California at Davis from 1965 to 1978, and from 1988 onwards served as the Honorary University Distinguished Professor of philosophy at Virginia Tech. She served as president of the Metaphysical Society of America in 1976. Grene wrote extensively on the history of philosophy, epistemology and philosophy of science, especially that of biology.
