ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Ithaca Airways was founded by Herbert M. Peters in Ithaca, Tompkins County, New York. It later became Ithaca Flying Service Inc.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Records include correspondence, 1928-29, and Ithaca Airways miscellany; financial records including cancelled checks, deposit slips, notes, mortgages, cancelled stock certificates, and invoices, 1928-40, also ledger books, cashbooks, checkbooks, receipt books, and bank books for the Ithaca Airways Corporation and the Ithaca Flying Service, 1928-42, also one daybook of the Cornell Flying Club, 1935-36; pilot information including training forms, maps, notes, exams, pamphlets, an atlas, and logbooks, 1929-41; and Herbert M. Peters' personal papers including financial records and miscellany, 1927-42; and Dorothy B. Peters' records as treasurer for Girl Scout Camp Comstock 1939, and School District 16, Town of Ulysses, 1939.
