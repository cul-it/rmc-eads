DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Howard E. Evans papers, 1943-2012.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
24-2-1870
Abstract:
Material relating to publications; professional correspondence, ca.1950-2002; minutes and printed material on the Veterinary College, the University faculty, and the Board of Trustees. Also, Veterinary College grade books, 1891-1989, kept by Professors Hopkins, Miller, and Evans and scrapbooks, 1987-2012.
Creator:
Evans, Howard E. (Howard Edward), 1922-
Quanitities:
59.3 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Professor of veterinary anatomy. Dr. Howard Edward Evans (Cornell University Class of 1944) is an emeritus professor of anatomy at the Cornell University College of Veterinary Medicine.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Professional correspondence, ca.1950-2002; minutes and printed material on the Veterinary College, the University faculty, and the Board of Trustees. Also, Veterinary College grade books, 1891-1989, kept by Professors Hopkins, Miller, and Evans. Also, correspondence and other material relating to the publication of the various editions of Miller's Guide to the Dissection of the Dog and Miller's Anatomy of the Dog.
Panorama group photograph of Officer Candidate School, Class 35, Camp Lee, Virginia, 1944, which included Cornell, Michigan, and Texas R.O.T.C. units. With photocopy of photograph of Cornell R.O.T.C. Quartermaster Unit, 1942-43, Major E. K. Halbleib, QMC. "before leaving for active duty induction at Ft. Niagara," May 1943.
Scrapbooks compiled by Howard and Erika Evans, 1987-2012.
