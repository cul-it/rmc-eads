Biographical / Historical

City planner, real estate consultant. In 1912, Farr joined the firm of Farr, Chinnock & Sampson, Inc., Chicagos oldest real estate firm, founded by his father in 1872. He also advised many cities on redevelopment and renewal as a member and former president of the Urban Land Institute. Member, International Fraternity of Lambda Alpha, Ely Chapter.
