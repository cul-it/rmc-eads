DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Statler Hotel records, 1928-1988.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
28-3-1801
Abstract:
Includes correspondence, files concerning Statler's new wing; construction and engineering publications; other records of Cornell's Statler Inn; financial statements; photograph; and Statler Inn registration cards for Harry Truman, Barry Goldwater, and Robert Kennedy.
Creator:
Statler Hotel (Cornell University)
Sayles, C. I. (Charles I.)
Quanitities:
5.3 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes correspondence of Professor Charles I. Sayles; files concerning Statler's new wing, 1952-1959; correspondence regarding equipment; construction and engineering publications; and other records of Cornell's Statler Inn. Also, financial statements from the Statler Inn and Alice Statler Auditorium; photograph of Statler Hotel and Inn employees, Christmas, 1986; and "Statler Hotel and Conference Center Operating Criteria," 1987-1988.
Also Statler Inn registration cards for Harry Truman, April 20, 1960; Barry Goldwater, January 26-27, 1961; and Robert Kennedy, September 28-29, 1964.
