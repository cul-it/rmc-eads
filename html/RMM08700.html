DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Jeremy Freeman Reggae collection, 1960-1990
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
8700
Abstract:
The collection includes original acetates from the 1960s to the early 1990s and paper and digital ephemera related to Deadly Dragon Sound System and to reggae history generally, including two hand painted signs advertising shows in Jamaica.
Creator:
Freeman, Jeremy
Kugelberg, Johan
Quanitities:
9 cubic feet.
Content Description

The collection includes original acetates from the 1960s to the early 1990s and paper and digital ephemera related to Deadly Dragon Sound System and to reggae history generally, including two hand painted signs advertising shows in Jamaica.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Preferred Citation

Jeremy Freeman Reggae collection, #8700. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Separated Materials

Materials separated from the resource: Panther's Collection by Mirjam Wirz (Rollo Press, undated); Sonidero City by Mirjam Wirz (undated); and Volcano Revisted: Kingston Dancehall Scene 1983 by Tero Kaski and Pekka Vuorinen (2011).
