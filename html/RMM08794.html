DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Alden Family scrapbooks, 1949-2011
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
8794
Abstract:
Travel and year scrapbooks documenting the trips and activities of John and Ann Alden. Includes booklet.
Creator:
Alden, John Doughty
Alden, Ann Buccholz
Quanitities:
20 cubic feet.
Content Description

Approximately fifty-four "Travel" and forty-eight "Year" scrapbooks (some scrapbooks contain both travel and year activities) created by John Alden and Ann Buchholz Alden, documenting their international travels and annual activities.

Preferred Citation
