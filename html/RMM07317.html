DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
David P. McWhirter papers, 1976-1984.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
7317
Abstract:
The David P. McWhirter papers relate to human sexuality in general and to homosexuality in particular. Subject files derive from sexuality and privacy commissions on which McWhirter served. The collection includes numerous typescripts of pornographic stories.
Creator:
McWhirter, David P., 1932-2006
Quanitities:
2.5 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Magazines, brochures, reports, manuscripts of stories, files, bulk mailings and miscellany relating to human sexuality in general and to homosexuality in particular. Subject files derive from sexuality and privacy commissions on which McWhirter served. Collection includes numerous typescripts of pornographic stories. Many of the items were in the Mariposa Research and Education Foundation library.
PROVENANCE

Many of the items were in the Mariposa Research and Education Foundation library. The provenance of the pornographic manuscripts is not clear. The Bruce Voeller manuscript Murder at McGurk was transferred to the Voeller Collection, #7307.
RELATED MATERIAL

Bruce R. Voeller papers, #7307.
Several cubic feet of periodicals have been catalogued individually; clippings and straight magazines and newspapers were discarded.
