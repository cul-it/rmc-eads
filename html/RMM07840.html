BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

A weekly gay entertainment guide for New York City, 1991 to July 10, 2009, HX, titled Homo Xtra for a while, was available for free in bars and other locations, concentrated in the West Village and Chelsea and, during the summer, in the Pines and Cherry Grove. Its slogan was: "The totally biased, politically incorrect party paper".
