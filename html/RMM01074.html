COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Included are photographs, maps, brochures, booklets, programs, broadsides, invitations, historical outlines, and other printed items pertaining to subjects including local politics and government, urban renewal, religion, civic and social events and organizations, musical and dramatic productions, transportation, educational institutions, and geology of the region.
Broadside: "REWARD - Who Killed the Harris Family?...," 1990.
"Ithaca, New York: A General Plan" (1971) with sketches by Peter Kahn; members of the Planning Board include Edward J. Conley, Richard P. Korf, and John W. Reps. Also, environmental impact statements prepared for the Strand Theatre, 1992.
Poster relating to Wharton Studios and the 75th anniversary of movie making in Ithaca, produced by Terry Harbin, 1990
