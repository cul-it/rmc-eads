Biographical / Historical

The Bronfenbrenner Center for Translational Research (BCTR) was created in 2011 and it consolidated and expanded programs and resources previously housed in the Family Life Development Center (FLDC) and the Bronfenbrenner Life Course Center (BLCC). The BCTR fosters research to understand and discover solutions to human problems across multiple levels of analysis, as well as to identify, evaluate and disseminate evidence-based and cost-efficient practices and programs.
The Bronfenbrenner Center for Translational Research records collection contains brochures and flyers of various talks and events. Also included are translational research podcasts by various Cornell faculty.
