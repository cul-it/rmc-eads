COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes correspondence concerning prospective students, applications for admission, requests for job information, requests for jobs to be filled, requests for professional information, professional and student societies, student prize competitions, complaints to Buildings and Grounds, the United States Mint, lecturers, applications for faculty and library positions, budgets, the College library, and personal and business correspondence of deans and professors. Other materials include minutes of the Committee on Student Conduct; Semi-Centennial Endowments items; bulletin board notices, fellowship announcements, accreditation reports, infirmary reports, grades, attendance lists, financial statements, postcards, blueprints, scale drawings, newspaper clippings, and miscellaneous pamphlets; clippings, correspondence, and other items pertaining to Margaret Bourke-White; list of the Clifton Beckwith Brown Memorial medalists (and blank medal) and the Charles Goodwin Sands Memorial medalists from 1900 to 1988, compiled by Professor Alexander Kira, and Sands medal (bronze), presented to Herbert P. Atherton, 1908. Also, transcripts of talks at 1977 Alumni Symposium by Robert Alexander, Edmund Bacon, Henry Tova, Jason Seley, Donald Greenberg, K. C. Parsons, Frank Rhodes, and students. Scrapbook of clippings and photographs relating to Frank Lloyd Wright, Dragon Day, and other activities.
