DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Sibley School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering records, 1917-2009.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
16-5-1151
Abstract:
Includes correspondence, course material, and equipment manuals of the Sibley School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, formerly the Sibley School of Mechanical Engineering. Also the papers of Charles Osborn Mackey, Howard N. Fairchild, and Frank Oakes Ellenwood.
Creator:
Sibley School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering
Mackey, Charles O. (Charles Osborn), 1903-1965.
Fairchild, Howard N.
Ellenwood, Frank O. (Frank Oakes), 1878-
Phelan, Richard M.
Quanitities:
15.5 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
NOTES

Includes collection #16-5-508, and #16-5-993.
INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Sibley School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering records, #16-5-1151. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Includes papers of Charles Osborn Mackey, Howard N. Fairchild, and Frank Oakes Ellenwood, including materials about refrigeration, heat pumps and heat transfer, compressors, vacuum pumps, diesel engines, and turbines. Also, a photograph of the Cornell University Faculty, 1916 and the Sibley Faculty, 1917.
Scope and content

Includes Annual Reports to the Dean (1949-1997), faculty meeting minutes (1947-1996) and Richard Phelan's planning drawings for the sundial on the Engineering Quad.
