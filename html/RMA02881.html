INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Fenner photographic collection, #4-3-2881. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Collection consists of negatives and photographic prints of Cornell University buildings, professors, athletics, and events. Photographs were taken by John (?) Fenner, an Ithaca photographer. The work dates from 1889 through 1953, however the majority of the images were taken in the 1930s and 1940s.
