BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Watkins earned a B.S. in consumer services and business in 1965 and an M.S. in textiles and clothing in1967 both from Pennsylvania State University. In 1967 she was hired in the Department of Textiles and Apparel at Cornell University as an assistant professor, becoming a professor emeritus in 1999. She founded the academic field of functional apparel design and in 1991 was inducted as a Fellow into the International Textile and Apparel Association.
