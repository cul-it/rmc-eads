Gay American poet.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Forty-two small pamphlets issued between 1980 and 2007 resembling chapbooks, each containing one or several poems on erotic or philosophical subjects. Some are printed, others handwritten, and others have text printed out from a computer and then mounted on paper decorated by hand. Also, 30 pieces of George-Murray's erotic artwork, collage and mixed media (fabric, thread, photographs, and paint). Most of the art incorporates written words. The pieces are 9 1/2 x 12 1/2 inches or smaller. He started making them in the late 1960s and continued until shortly before he died.
