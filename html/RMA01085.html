Cornell University Department of Rural Sociology records, #21-33-1085. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

The Cornell University Department of Rural Sociology records documents the functioning of the Department, 1920-2010. It includes minutes of the New York State Interdepartmental Committee on Farm and Food Processing Labor (1948, 1950-1955); descriptions and reports concerning migrant labor in New York State and various funded projects; requests for information; and other records of Olaf F. Larson at the New York State College of Agriculture Department of Rural Sociology at Cornell University. Also, reports and bulletins (1920-1955), department newsletters (1961-1967), mimeographed proposals, notices, and position papers relating to challenges to governance at Cornell, collected by Harold Capener (1965-1977), Capener's correspondence (1963-1977), and a bibliography: "One Act Plays for Mixed Casts" compiled by the Department of Rural Social Organization (mimeograph), post-1914.
