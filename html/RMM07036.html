DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
John V. Breedlove papers, [ca. 1963]-1986.
Repository:
Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:
7036
Abstract:
Includes letters to family, certificates and medals, maps, Army records, insignia, receipts, and memorabilia of a helicopter pilot in the Vietnam War.
Creator:
Breedlove, John V. (John Vincent), 1945-
Vietnam War Veterans Archives
Quanitities:
7.4 cubic feet.
Language:
Collection material in English
BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Vietnam War veteran.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes letters to family, certificates and medals, maps, Army records, insignia, receipts, and memorabilia of a helicopter pilot in the Vietnam War. Also, videocassette of movies taken in Vietnam and tape recordings sent home during the war; includes family papers and business records, and photographs.
NOTES

Part of Vietnam War Veterans Archives.
