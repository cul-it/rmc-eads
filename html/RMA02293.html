Title:

Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections
Collection Number:

Abstract:
Curriculum vitae, drafts of speeches, lecture and teaching notes, reports, research projects, typescripts of articles, published articles, manuscripts, and reprints about regional planning and regional science.
Creator:
Czamanski, Stanislaw.
Quanitities:
1.5 cubic feet.

BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Stanislaw Czamanski was a professor of city and regional planning at Cornell University.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Curriculum vitae, drafts of speeches, lecture and teaching notes, reports, research projects, typescripts of articles, published articles, manuscripts, and reprints about regional planning and regional science. Some materials are in Polish.
