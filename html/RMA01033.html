INFORMATION FOR USERS
Cite As:

Blanchard Rideout papers, #34-8-1033. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Includes files involving Peace Corps activities at Cornell and Blanchard Rideout's role in Frank Rhodes' inauguration and as marshall for the 1971-1979 commencement ceremonies. Also correspondence with Morris Bishop, and drafts of his memorial statement which Blanchard wrote, including an explanation of the quote found on the memorial plaque to Bishop in Sage Chapel. Also a box of material, sub collection #8-1-1445, about Peace Corps Project Peru RCA that contains financial documents, biographies of participants, photographs, training material, and correspondence.
Access Restrictions:

Access to Box 5 restricted to the permission of the University Archivist.
