COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Records of the Presidential Search Committee and other materials associated with Presidential searches at Cornell University. Includes files relating to the searches of James Perkins, Dale Corson, Frank H. T. Rhodes, Jeff Lehman, David Skorton and Elizabeth Garrett, including minutes of meetings and correspondence concerning the work of the Trustee Committee and faculty and alumni advisory committees. Much of the material relating to Perkins, Corson and Rhodes are the files of Board of Trustee and Search Committee member Austin Kiplinger.
