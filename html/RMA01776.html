COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

11 volumes of correspondence, administrative records, and statistics of the Cornell University Graduate School; also, announcements, speeches, reports, catalogs, applications, minutes, department self studies, program reviews, and external reports. Includes Graduate School annual reports for 1922 and 1952/1953-1986/1987 as well as 2001/2002-2002/2003. The report for 1922 is titled "Problems of the Graduate School: an informal report by the Dean, to the members of the faculty"; it was conceived of as a supplement to the annual Dean's report to the President.
