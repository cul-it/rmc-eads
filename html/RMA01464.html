ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

The Office of Equal Opportunity handled diversity, disability, sexual harassment and other inclusion issues. The director reported to the Vice President of Human Resources. The functions of this office were changed to the Office of Workforce Diversity and Inclusion (now the Department of Inclusion and Workforce Diversity) and Student Disability Services in 1999.

Advisory Board minutes and reports and other records of the office.
