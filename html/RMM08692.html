Scope and content

The collection consists of correspondence, photographs, newspaper clippings, biographical material and ephemera, bound volumes and original writings by Paul Brunton, including journals, notebooks and manuscripts.
Biographical / Historical

Paul Brunton was a British author who devoted his life to communicating the spiritual teachings and practices of Eastern traditions in a form suited to the contemporary Western reader. He wrote eleven books, and travelled to lecture and to interview hundreds of teachers, fakirs, saints, and sages around the globe. After retiring, he continued his philosophical writing for several decades. Most of it is contained in this archive.
