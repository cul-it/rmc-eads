COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

District development plans for specific districts in Kenya, 1974-1978, 1979-1983, and 1984-1988; project reports, 1986-1991; reports from various individuals on planning topics including training, information systems, employment, food shortages, rural-urban policy and development, demography, arid and semi-arid land development, and financial aspects; and project reports. Participants included Ward Henevelt, John M. Cohen, Richard M. Hook, F.D. Van Buer, A.U. Wandera, Emery Roe, Marcia L. Odell, Alan G. Johnston, Klaus Bethke, Clay G. Westcott, Sid Saltzman, Judith Geist, David B. Lewis, Hugh E. Evans, George Ruigu, Barclay G. Jones, Peter Weisel, R.W. Machina, John Carlsen, and John T. Saywell.
