ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

In 1966, the status of the Biometrics Unit was recognized in the renamed Dept. of Plant Breeding and Biometry. In 1998, the unit became the Dept. of Biometrics, affiliated with the Dept. of Statistical Science. As of 2002, it was the Dept. of Biological Statistics and Computational Biology.

Dept. of Biometrics records, #21/40/3204. Division of Rare and Manuscript Collections, Cornell University Library.
Scope and content

Correspondence, annual reports, budgets, course lectures, syllabi, labs, exams and problem sets of the Dept. of Biometrics, New York State College of Agriculture and Life Sciences, Cornell University.
Scope and content

Includes Annual report of the Biometrics Unit, Department of Plant Breeding and Biometry and Dept. of Biometrics Annual Report. In 2001, the title became Annual report of the Department of Biological Statistics and Computational Biology, which is cataloged separately.
