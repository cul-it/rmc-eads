COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes publications, reports, subject files, course material, and proceedings of the Cornell Symposium on Theriogenology honoring Foote, 1992; "Artificial Insemination to Cloning: Tracing 50 Years of Research," 1998; reprint of "Development of Reproductive Biotechnologies in Domestic Animals from Artificial Insemination to Cloning: A Perspective," in Cloning, Vol. 1, Number 3, 1999; Proceedings of the International Symposium: "From the first artificial insemination to the modern reproduction biotechnologies: traditional ways and new frontiers of animal production, Regio Emilia (Italy) October 8-9,1999;" "Highlights in Dairy Cattle Reproduction in the Last 100 Years," 2005. Also articles, video tape, audio tapes, and photograph.
