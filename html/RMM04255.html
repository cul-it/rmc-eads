BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

A city planner, Britton Harris is Emeritus Professor of Planning at the University of Pennsylvania.

Articles and other publications by Britton Harris on land use development, transportation, use of computers, and various other topics in planning and regional science. Part of Regional Science Archives.
