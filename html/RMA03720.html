BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Professor emeritus of Psychology (formerly the Department of Human Development) in the College of Human Ecology, Cornell University.
Mon Cochran received a B.A. from Harvard (1964) and M.A.(1969) and Ph.D. (1973) degrees from the University of Michigan. He directed the Cornell Early Childhood Program and retired in 2008.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Correspondence, grant files, project files, and videos pertaining to Cochran's career and his work with child care and prekindergarten programs, the Cornell Early Childhood Program, the Swedish Practicum in Childhood, Family, and Social Policy Program, and the Family Matters Project.
