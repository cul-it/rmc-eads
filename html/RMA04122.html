BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

Morse was a bee biologist in the Dept. of Entomology. He received his B.A. in 1950, his master's degree in 1953, and his PhD in 1955, all from Cornell.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Includes correspondence, course material and photographs related to beekeeping, his membership in the Empire State Honey Producers' Association, and Morse's career in the Dept. of Entomology. Also included is a bound volume titled "Burma and Nepal programs and reports," consisting of typewritten documents and correspondence concerning projects that Morse was involved with there. Its spine title is Morse--Asian journey--1982.)
