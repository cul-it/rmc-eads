ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

Founded in 1949, the New York State Foundation cultivates, secures, and stewards financial resources to support the Cornell Cooperative Extension New York State 4-H program in developing and delivering 4-H youth development programs.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Minutes of the Board of Directors and various other committee meetings, memos, financials, scholarship and trustee files.
