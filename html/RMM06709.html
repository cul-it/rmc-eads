BIOGRAPHICAL NOTE

New York Grape Country Co-Op was incorporated into produce and market vinefresh, the pink grape juice made from wine grapes grown in New York State, primarily in the Finger Lakes region.
COLLECTION DESCRIPTION

Records document the production, marketing, and sale of Vinefresh, the pink grape juice, through board records, reports, correspondence, production and sales ledgers and promotional materials.
