Biographical / Historical

The Department of Human Centered Design (HCD) was established in November of 2021. It includes faculty members from the previous departments of Design and Environmental Analysis and Fiber Science and Apparel Design. The origins of both departments can be traced back to 1925, as the Department of Household Art and the Department of Textiles and Clothing. They evolved separately and then joined together in 1969 as the Department of Design and Environmental Analysis (D+EA). They stayed together until 1985, when the Department of Textiles and Apparel split from DEA.
